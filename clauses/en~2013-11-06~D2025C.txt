<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>All wood packaging materials used in shipping must conform to the <cite>International Standards for Phytosanitary Measures No. 15: Regulation of Wood Packaging Material in International Trade (ISPM 15)</cite>.</p>
<p>Pertinent additional information on Canada's import and export
programs is provided in the following Canadian Food Inspection
Agency policy directives:</p>

Entry Requirements for Wood Packaging Materials Produced in All

The Canadian Wood Packaging Certification Program (CWPCP)</cite></p>
