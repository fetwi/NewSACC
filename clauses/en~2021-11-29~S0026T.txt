<h3 class="clause-heading">Remarks – Recommended Use of SACC Item</h3><section class="remarks group-sacc-remarks field-group-section">
<p>
  Use the following clause in requests for supply arrangements when a suppliers' conference will be held.
 </p>
<p>
  Select Option 1 when no security requirement is associated with the suppliers’ conference and the COVID-19 Vaccination Policy for Supplier Personnel does not apply.
 </p>
<p>
  Select Option 2 when personnel security screening is required prior to gaining authorized access to PROTECTED information, assets, or sites or CLASSIFIED information, assets or sites and the COVID-19 Vaccination Policy for Supplier Personnel does not apply.
 </p>
<p>
  Select Option 3 when no security requirement is associated with the suppliers’ conference but the COVID-19 Vaccination Policy for Supplier Personnel applies.
 </p>
<p>
  Select Option 4 when personnel security screening is required prior to gaining authorized access to PROTECTED information, assets, or sites or CLASSIFIED information, assets or sites and the COVID-19 Vaccination Policy for Supplier Personnel applies.
 </p>
<p>
  Consult section
  
   3.115 Bidders' Conferences
  
  of the
  <em>
   Supply Manual
  </em>
  for more information. Further information on vaccination requirements can be found in section
  
   1.62 COVID-19 Vaccination Policy for Supplier Personnel
  
  .
 </p>
</section>
