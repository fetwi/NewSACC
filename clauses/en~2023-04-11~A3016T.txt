<h3 class="clause-heading">Remarks – Recommended Use of SACC Item</h3><section class="remarks group-sacc-remarks field-group-section">
<p>
  Use the following clause in competitive solicitations or requests for standing offers or supply arrangements for procurements valued above $25 million
  <abbr title="Canadian dollars">
   CAD
  </abbr>
  including taxes, fees and options when competition is conditionally limited to suppliers who are participating in a greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions disclosure, target-setting and reduction initiative such as the Net-Zero Challenge or equivalent, when evaluation criteria related to
  <abbr title="greenhouse gas">
   GHG
  </abbr>
  emission targets or participation in the Net-Zero challenge or equivalent, cannot be used. The bidders/offerors/suppliers must not be affiliated within the meaning used in the
  

    Competition Act
   

  , R.S.C., 1985, c. C-34.
 </p>
<p>
  Use this clause in conjunction with
  
   A9076T
  
  and
  
   A3021C
  
  if applicable. Use this clause in conjunction with
  
   A3015T
  
  and
  
   A3015C
  
  if the high complexity or medium complexity templates are not used.
 </p>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><p><kbd>Instruction to contracting officers: Select “bidder”, “offeror” or “supplier” accordingly.</kbd></p> <p>This procurement is conditionally limited to suppliers who are participating in the Net-Zero Challenge or equivalent.</p> <p>Subject to the evaluation procedures contained in the solicitation, <kbd>bidders/offerors/suppliers</kbd> acknowledge that only <kbd>bids/offers/arrangements</kbd> with a signed Net-Zero Challenge letter of participation may be considered for <var>____</var> <kbd>(<em>insert: “contract award”, “standing offer issuance”, “supply arrangement issuance”, whichever is applicable</em>)</kbd>.</p> <p>Failure to provide a letter of participation and this certification with the <kbd>bid/offer/arrangement</kbd> will result in the <kbd>bid/offer/arrangement</kbd> being treated as not participating in the Net-Zero Challenge or equivalent.</p> <p>The <kbd>bidder/offeror/supplier</kbd> certifies that:</p> <p>(<var>____</var>) they are participating in the Net-Zero Challenge or equivalent.</p> <p>(<var>____</var>) they will maintain participation in the Net-Zero Challenge or equivalent for the duration of the resulting <var>______</var> <kbd>(<em>insert: “contract”, “standing offer”, “supply arrangement”, as applicable</em>)</kbd>.</p></pre>
