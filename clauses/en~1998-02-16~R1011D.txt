<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>1.     The <em>Consultant</em> shall:

       (a)    review if requested, during the <em>Contractor</em>'s warranty period, 
              any defects reported by the <em>Departmental Representative</em>;

       (b)    thirty (30) days prior to the expiry of any warranty period, 
              visit the site, and record any defects observed or reported;

       (c)    at the end of any warranty period, carry out a final review 
              of the Project and report to the <em>Departmental Representative</em> 
              the status of defects.  If the <em>Departmental Representative</em> 
              accepts the rectification of the defects, a notice of "Final 
              Warranty Inspection" shall be issued to the <em>Contractor</em>.
