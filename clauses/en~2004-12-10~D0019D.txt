<pre>In view of operating problems that may result from handling trucks 
exceeding 3.66 m x 15.24 cm (12 ft. x 6 in.) in height in the lower level 
of the Printing Bureau, Gatineau, Quebec, it is mandatory that deliveries 
be made in vehicles not exceeding 3.66 m x 15.24 cm (12 ft. x 6 in.) when 
empty.
