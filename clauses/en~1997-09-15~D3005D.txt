<pre>1.     Methods of delivery shall conform to the National Standard of Canada 
       standard CAN/CGA-B149.2-M95 of the Canadian Gas Association, as 
       amended to date.

2.     METERED TRUCKS:

       (a)    Delivery trucks shall be equipped with meters capable of 
              giving printed meter slips;

       (b)    The Contractor shall provide printed meter slips for each 
              delivery of petroleum products;

       (c)    Meters shall be measured in litres.
