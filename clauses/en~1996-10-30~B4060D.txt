<pre>Unless authorized by the Contracting Authority, the Contractor shall not 
release for shipment any item which is not identified with a NATO Stock 
Number (NSN). If an NSN has not been provided, the Contractor shall request 
an NSN from the Contracting Authority sixty (60) days prior to the 
scheduled shipment date.  Such request shall include the technical 
documentation required to allow for the cataloguing and assignment of the 
NSN.


</pre>
