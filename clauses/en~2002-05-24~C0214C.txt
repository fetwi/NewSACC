       <strong>CATEGORY             FIRM PER DIEM RATE</strong>

       _______              $______

       Total Estimated Price: $______

Subject to the exercise of the Contract option to extend the Contract

       <strong>CATEGORY             FIRM PER DIEM RATE</strong>

       _______              $______
       _______              $______
                            UNFUNDED

Goods and Services Tax or Harmonized Sales Tax, estimated at $____, is 
extra to the "Total Estimated Price" shown herein and shall be paid in 
accordance with the provisions of clause "_____."

All deliverables are FOB Destination, and Canadian Customs Duty included, 
where applicable.

<strong>DEFINITION OF A DAY/PRORATION</strong>

A day is defined as 7.5 hours exclusive of meal breaks.  Payment shall be 
for days actually worked with no provision for annual leave, statutory 
holidays and sick leave.  Time worked which is more or less than a day 
shall be prorated to reflect actual time worked in accordance with the 
following formula:

       <span class="wsStyleUnderline">Hours worked</span>
       7.5  x  per diem rate

<strong>NOTE TO PAYING OFFICE</strong>

The amount of Canadian dollars has been calculated on the basis of exchange 
of C$_____ being equal to US$1.  Payment shall be made in American funds 
and the amount required in Canadian funds shall be adjusted to correspond 
to the rate of exchange in effect when the necessary foreign funds are 
purchased.

<strong>TRAVEL EXPENSES</strong>

The Contractor shall be paid for pre-authorized reasonable and proper 
travel expenses, supported by appropriate receipts, incurred by personnel 
directly engaged in the performance of the Work, calculated in accordance 
with the current Treasury Board Travel Directive (<strong class="wsStyleUnderline">http://www.tbs-sct.gc.ca/
travel/travel_e.html</strong>), without any allowance thereon for overhead or profit.  
Charges for air travel shall not exceed that for economy class.  All 
payments are subject to government audit.

Total Estimated Price: $______.
