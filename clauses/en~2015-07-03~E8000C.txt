<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">In order to provide funds for labour, services and material, the Contractor must, within <var>_____</var> calendar days after the date of contract award, provide to the Contracting Authority a duly executed labour and material payment bond form PWGSC-TPSGC 506. The labour and material bond must be <var>_____</var> percent of the Contract Price and must be accepted as security by one of the bonding companies listed in <cite>Treasury Board Contracting Policy, Appendix L, Acceptable Bonding Companies</cite>.</li>
<li value="2">The Contractor must post a copy of the bond in a plainly visible place where any of the Work is performed.</li>
<li value="3">If Canada does not receive the required bond within the specified period, Canada may terminate the Contract for default pursuant to the default provision of the Contract.</li>
</ol>
