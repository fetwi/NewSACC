<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>The Contractor shall have full responsibility for disposal of any hazardous 
   waste removed or uncovered in the performance of the Work.  Title to 
   such waste shall pass to the Contractor as soon as the Contractor takes 
   possession of the waste, and the Contractor shall dispose of such waste 
   in accordance with the requirements of the Contract, if any, and in 
   accordance with applicable law, whether federal, provincial or municipal.
