<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>The Contractor is responsible for performing or having performed all 
inspections and tests necessary to substantiate that the materiel or 
services provided conform to the drawings, specifications and Contract 
requirements including any applicable technical requirements of the 
specified manufacturers' part number.

This materiel is subject to inspection by a Department of National Defence 
Quality Assurance Representative/Unit Technical Inspector at destination.
