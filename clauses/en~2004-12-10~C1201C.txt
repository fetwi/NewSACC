<pre>Use the following clause in contracts where one or more items in the contract 
Basis of Payment is subject to a ceiling price, and when the basis of payment 
for these items involves audited costs in accordance with Contract Cost 
Principles 1031-2.
</pre>

<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>The ceiling price(s) specified in the Basis of Payment is(are) subject to 
downward adjustment so as not to exceed the actual cost incurred, as 
established by government audit in accordance with Contract Cost Principles 
1031-2 ________ (<strong><em>insert, if applicable, "plus a profit of _____ percent of 
such cost"</em></strong>).  Upon completion of the audit, the price(s) shall be adjusted 
to the extent necessary to reflect the results of the audit.  If there has 
been any overpayment, it shall be promptly refunded to Canada.

The Contractor shall segregate its costs for each item for which a ceiling 
price has been established so that the cost of each such item may be 
determined.


</pre>
