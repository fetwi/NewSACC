<h3 class="clause-heading">Remarks – Recommended Use of SACC Item</h3><section class="remarks group-sacc-remarks field-group-section">
<p>
  Use this clause in the ‘Summary’ section of any Requests for Supply Arrangement (RFSAs) where individual solicitations under the resulting Supply Arrangement (SA) may be subject to the COVID-19 Vaccination Policy for Supplier Personnel. Also include
  <abbr title="Standard Acquisition Clauses and Conditions">
   SACC
  </abbr>
  Manual clause 4013 - Compliance with on-site measures, standing orders, policies, and rules, and, if a ‘Suspension of the work’ clause is not already included in the general conditions that will be used,
  <abbr title="Standard Acquisition Clauses and Conditions">
   SACC
  </abbr>
  Manual clause 4014 - Suspension of the work as supplemental general conditions that will apply to the resulting contract.
 </p>
<p>
  For individual solicitations under
  <abbr title="Supply Arrangement">
   SA
  </abbr>
  s that are subject to the COVID-19 Vaccination Policy for Supplier Personnel, use:
 </p>
<ul>
<li>

    2035: General Conditions - Higher Complexity – Services
   
   ,
   
    2040: General Conditions - Research and Development
   
   , or
   
    4013: Compliance with on-site measures, standing orders, policies, and rules
   
   ;
  </li>
<li>

    4014: Suspension of the work
   
   (only if a ‘Suspension of the work’ clause is not already included in the General Conditions);
  </li>
<li>

    A3080T: COVID-19 vaccination requirement
   
   ;
  </li>
<li>

    A3081T: COVID-19 vaccination requirement certification
   
   ;
  </li>
<li>

    A3015T: Certifications - Bid
   
   (only if equivalent language is not already included in the solicitation; clause not needed if using
   <abbr title="Public Works and Government Services Canada">
    PWGSC
   </abbr>
   Standard Procurement Templates); and
  </li>
<li>

    A3015C: Certifications - Contract
   
   (only if equivalent language is not already included in the contract; clause not needed if using
   <abbr title="Public Works and Government Services Canada">
    PWGSC
   </abbr>
   Standard Procurement Templates).
  </li>
</ul>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><p>The requirement covered by the bid solicitation of any resulting supply arrangement may be subject to a vaccination requirement pursuant to the COVID-19 Vaccination Policy for Supplier Personnel.</p></pre>
