<p>The benefits that apply to this procurement are contained in
Part 6 - Employment and Contracting of Article 33 - Archaeology of
the Nunavut Land Claims Agreement, sections:</p>
<ul class="hierarchy">
<li><span class="number">33.6.1</span> Where any agency of the
Government intends to contract for carrying out of archaeological
work in the Nunavut Settlement Area, the agency shall:
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">give preferential treatment to qualified Inuit
contractors where the agency proposes to tender such contract;
and</li>
<li value="2">ensure that all contractors give preferential
treatment to qualified Inuit.</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li><span class="number">33.6.2</span> Any archaeological programs
in the Nunavut Settlement Area that are administered by Government
shall conform, at a minimum, to the employment and training
provisions set out in Article 23.</li>
</ul>
