<h3 class="clause-heading">Remarks – Recommended Use of SACC Item</h3><section class="remarks group-sacc-remarks field-group-section">
<p>
  Use the following clause in contracts, standing offers and supply arrangements when the successful bidder/offeror/supplier had to complete and submit a greenhouse gas disclosure and target setting initiative certification to be considered.
 </p>
<p>
  Use this clause in conjunction with
  
   A9076T
  
  ,
  
   A3016T
  
  and
  
   A3017T
  
  if applicable. Use this clause in conjunction with
  
   A3015T
  
  and
  
   A3015C
  
  if the high complexity or medium complexity templates are not used.
 </p>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><p><kbd>Instruction to contracting officers: Select “contractor”, “offeror” or “supplier” accordingly.</kbd></p> <p>The <kbd>contractor/offeror/supplier</kbd> warrants that the certification of Greenhouse gas emission reduction targets and disclosure submitted by the <kbd>contractor/offeror/supplier</kbd> is accurate and complete.</p> <p>The <kbd>contractor/offeror/supplier</kbd> must keep proper records and documentation relating to participation in the Greenhouse gas emission reduction targets and disclosure initiative. The <kbd>contractor/offeror/supplier</kbd> must not, without obtaining before the written consent of the <kbd>Contracting/Standing Offer/Supply Arrangement</kbd> Authority, dispose of any such records or documentation until the expiration of six (6) years after final payment under any contract resulting from the contract/standing offer/supply arrangement, or until settlement of all outstanding claims and disputes under the <kbd>contract/standing offer/supply arrangement</kbd>, whichever is later. All such records and documentation must at all times during the retention period be open to audit, inspection and examination by the representatives of Canada, who may make copies and take extracts. The <kbd>contractor/offeror/supplier</kbd> must provide all facilities for such audits, inspections and examinations, and must furnish all such information as the representatives of Canada may from time to time require with respect to such records and documentation.</p> <p>Nothing in this clause must be interpreted as limiting the rights and remedies which Canada may otherwise have pursuant any contract or contract resulting from the <kbd>standing offer/supply arrangement</kbd>.</p> <p>The <kbd>contractor/offeror/supplier</kbd> must notify the <kbd>Contracting/Standing Offer/Supply Arrangement</kbd> authority if they are no longer participating in an initiative.</p></pre>
