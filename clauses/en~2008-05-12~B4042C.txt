   A1030C

<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">When identification markings are required, the Contractor must arrange for their design and manufacture in
accordance with the current issue of Canadian Forces Specification D-02-002-001/SG-001. The markings must be affixed to the deliverable end item(s) before delivery.</li>
<li value="2">Identification markings applicable to the United States (U.S.) Navy F-18 program must be manufactured in accordance with the current issue of MIL-STD-130, except that the "U.S." must be blanked out and the Canadian contract number, prefixed with the word "CANADA", must be specified in the Contract Number block.</li>
<li value="3">The Contractor must submit drawings for identification markings for approval through the responsible
Department of National Defence Technical Authority at least sixty (60) days before production.</li>
</ol>
</div></pre>
