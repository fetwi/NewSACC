<pre>Use the following clause in competitive requests for standing offers for 
multiple items (line item) when competition is conditionally limited to Canadian 
goods and the certification must be done on an item by item basis as defined in 
paragraph 3(b) of clause A3050T.

Contracting officers must select one of the options provided in the clause.

Use clause M3063T for multiple items when the certification must be done on an 
aggregate basis.

Use this clause in conjunction with A3050T and M3060C.

Contracting officers must review the Canadian Content Policy (Supply Manual, 
Chapter 5 and Annex 5.1) to establish if the requirement is subject to the 
policy.</pre>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>This procurement is conditionally limited to Canadian goods.

Subject to the evaluation procedures contained in the request for standing 
offer, offerors acknowledge that only offers for items with a certification 
that the item(s) offered are Canadian goods, as defined in clause A3050T, 
may be considered.

Failure to provide this certification completed with the offer will result 
in the item(s) offered being treated as non-Canadian goods.
