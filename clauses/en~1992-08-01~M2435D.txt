<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>A default under one or more contracts created by a call-up under this 
Standing Offer shall entitle Her Majesty, in Her sole discretion, to treat 
such default as a default under any one or more of any remaining contracts 
created by a call-up under this Standing Offer, whether or not the Offeror 
is in fact in default under any of such remaining contracts.
