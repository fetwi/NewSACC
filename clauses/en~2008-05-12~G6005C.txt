<p>
  Use the following clause in contracts involving short-term automobile leases 
(fewer than 31 days).
 </p>
<p>
  This clause is used to ensure that any liability claim arising from the use of 
the rented vehicle by federal government employees traveling on official 
government business, be resolved by Canada rather than the employees own 
Automobile Liability policy.  (Bill 18, Ontario - changes to the Highway Traffic 
Act).
 </p>

   NCR.RMIAS-SCGRA@pwgsc.gc.ca
  
  .
 </p>
</section>

presented by <span class="input-required"><var>___________</var>
