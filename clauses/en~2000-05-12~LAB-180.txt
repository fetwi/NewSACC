<strong>LAB-180       01     (12/05/00)    Interpretation</strong>

1.     In these Conditions

       "<em>Act</em>" means the Fair Wages and Hours of <em>Labour Act</em>;

       "<em>Regulations</em>" means the Fair Wages and Hours of Labour Regulations 
       made pursuant to the Act;

       "<em>Contract</em>" means the contract of which these Labour Conditions are 
       part;

       "<em>Contracting Authority</em>" means the department of Government or a 
       crown corporation with whom the contract is made;

       "Contractor" means the person who has entered into the contract with 
       the contracting authority;

       "<em>Regional Director</em>" means the director of a regional office of the 
       Department of Human Resources Development or the director's 
       designated representative;

       "<em>Inspector</em>" has the meaning assigned to the term by Part III of the 
       Canada Labour Code. 

       "<em>Minister</em>" means the Minister of Labour of Canada;

       "<em>persons</em>" means those workers employed by the contractor, 
       subcontractor or any other person doing or contracting to do the 
       whole or any part of the work contemplated by the contract;


<strong>LAB-180       02     (12/05/00)    General Fair Wage Clause</strong>

1.     All persons in the employ of the <em>Contractor</em>, subcontractor, or any 
       other person doing or contracting to do the whole or any part of the 
       work contemplated by the <em>Contract</em>, shall during the continuance of 
       the work:

       (a)    be paid fair wages that is, such wages as are generally 
              accepted as current for competent workers in the district in 
              which the work is being performed for the character or class 
              of work in which such workers are respectively engaged; and

       (b)    in all cases, be paid no less than the minimum hourly rate of 
              pay established by the Labour Program of the Department of 
              Human Resources Development in the Fair Wage Schedules which 
              form a part of this <em>Contract</em> as Appendix A to these Labour 
              Conditions; and

       (c)    for contracts covering work performed in the province of 
              Quebec, be paid at least the wage rates established by that 
              province for the purposes of the Quebec "Construction Decree". 

2.     Where there is no wage rate in the schedules referred to in 1. for a 
       particular character or class of work, the <em>Contractor</em> shall pay 
       wages for that character or class of work at a rate not less than 
       the rate for an equivalent character or class of work.

3.     Where during the term of the <em>Contract</em>, the <em>Contractor</em> receives 
       notice from the <em>Contracting Authority</em> of any change in wage rates, 
       the <em>Contractor</em> shall pay not less than the changed wage rate 
       beginning on the first day after receipt, by the <em>Contractor</em>, of the 
       notice of the change in wage rates.

<strong>LAB-180       03     (12/05/00)    Hours of Work</strong>

1.     The hours of work in a day and in a week of <em>persons</em> employed in the 
       execution of the <em>Contract</em>, including the hours of work in excess of 
       which a person shall be paid overtime at a rate at least equal to 
       one and one half times the fair wage, are the hours of work for the 
       province in which the work is being performed as set out from time 
       to time in an Act of that province.

2.     The daily or weekly hours of work referred to in paragraph 1. may be 
       exceeded in accordance with the applicable provincial law.
 
<strong>LAB-180       04     (12/05/00)    Labour Conditions to be Posted</strong>

For the information and the protection of all <em>persons</em>, the <em>Contractor</em> 
agrees to post and keep posted, in a conspicuous place on the premises 
where work contemplated by the <em>Contract</em> is being carried out or on premises 
occupied or used by persons engaged in carrying out such work, a copy of 
these Labour Conditions, and a copy of the applicable Fair Wage Schedules 
along with any subsequent changes.


<strong>LAB-180       05     (12/05/00)    The Contractor to Keep Records which are 
                                   to be Kept Open for Inspection</strong>

1.     The <em>Contractor</em> agrees to keep books and records showing the names, 
       addresses, classifications of employment and work of all workers 
       employed under the <em>Contract</em>, the rate of wages to be paid, the wages 
       paid and the daily hours worked by the workers.

2.     The <em>Contractor</em> also agrees that the <em>Contractor</em>'s books, records and 
       premises will be open at all reasonable times for inspection by an <em>
       Inspector</em>. 

3.     The <em>Contractor</em> also agrees to furnish the Inspector and the <em>
       Contracting Authority</em>, on request, with such further information as 
       is required to ascertain that the requirements of the <em>Act</em>, the <em>
       Regulations</em> and the <em>Contract</em> with respect to wages, hours of work 
       and other labour conditions have been complied with.


<strong>LAB-180       06     (12/05/00)    Departmental Requirements before Payment 
                                   made to Contractor</strong>

1.     The <em>Contractor</em> agrees that the <em>Contractor</em> will not be entitled to 
       payment of any money otherwise payable under the <em>Contract</em> until the <em>
       Contractor</em> has filed with the <em>Contracting Authority</em> in support of a 
       claim for payment a sworn statement:

       (a)    that the <em>Contractor</em> has kept the books and records required 
              by these <em>Regulations</em>,

       (b)    that there are no wages in arrears in respect of work 
              performed under the <em>Contract</em>, and

       (c)    that to the <em>Contractor</em>'s knowledge, all the conditions in the <em>
              Contract</em> required by the <em>Act</em> and the <em>Regulations</em> have been 
              complied with.

2.     The <em>Contractor</em> also agrees that, where fair wages have not been paid 
       by the <em>Contractor</em> to <em>persons</em> employed under the <em>Contract</em>, the <em>
       Contracting Authority</em> shall withhold from any money otherwise 
       payable under the <em>Contract</em> to the <em>Contractor</em> the amount necessary to 
       ensure that fair wages are paid to all employees until fair wages 
       are paid.


<strong>LAB-180       07     (12/05/00)    Authority to pay Wages in the Event of 
                                   Default by the Contractor</strong>

1.     The <em>Contractor</em> agrees that where the <em>Contractor</em> is in default of 
       payment of fair wages to an employee, the <em>Contractor</em> will pay the <em>
       Minister</em> the amount the <em>Contractor</em> is in default.

2.     The <em>Contractor</em> agrees that where the <em>Contractor</em> fails to comply with 
       paragraph 1., the<em> Contracting Authority</em> will pay to the Receiver 
       General, out of any money otherwise payable to the <em>Contractor</em>, the 
       amount for which the <em>Contractor</em> is in default.


<strong>LAB-180       08     (12/05/00)    Conditions of Subcontracting</strong>

The <em>Contractor</em> and the subcontractor agree that in subcontracting any part 
of the work contemplated by the <em>Contract</em>, they will place in the 
subcontract the conditions respecting fair wages, hours of work and other 
labour conditions set out in the <em>Contract</em> and the requirements set out in 
Section 4.  The <em>Contractor</em> further agrees that the <em>Contractor</em> will be 
responsible for carrying out these conditions in the event the 
subcontractor fails to carry them out.

 
<strong>LAB-180       09     (12/05/00)    Non-discrimination in Hiring and 
                                   Employment of Labour</strong>

1.     The <em>Contractor</em> agrees that in the hiring and employment of workers 
       to perform any work under the <em>Contract</em>, the <em>Contractor</em> will not 
       refuse to employ and will not discriminate in any manner against any 
       person because

       (a)    of that person's race, national or ethnic origin, colour, 
              religion, age, sex, sexual orientation, marital status, 
              disability, conviction for which a pardon has been granted, 
              or family status;

       (b)    of the race, national or ethnic origin, colour, religion, age, 
              sex, sexual orientation, marital status, disability, 
              conviction for which a pardon has been granted, or family 
              status of any person having a relationship or association 
              with that person, or

       (c)    a complaint has been made or information has been given in 
              respect of that person relating to an alleged failure by the <em>
              Contractor</em> to comply with subparagraph (a) or (b).
