<p>
  Use the following clause in competitive bid solicitations for multiple items (line item) when competition is solely limited to Canadian goods and the certification must be done on an item by item basis as defined in paragraph 3.(b) of clause
  
   A3050T
  
  .
 </p>
<p>
  Contracting officers must select one of the options provided in the clause.
 </p>
<p>
  Use clause
  
   A3053T
  
  for multiple items when the certification must be done on an aggregate basis.
 </p>
<p>
  Use this clause in conjunction with
  
   A3050T
  
  and
  
   A3060C
  
  .
 </p>
<p>
  Follow these instructions only if the medium complexity (2T-MED1) or higher complexity (2T-HIGH1) template is not used.  Contracting officers must consult the procedures 2T-PROC1 to confirm they cannot use the template for the requirement.
 </p>
<ol>
<li>
   Insert one of the following options at the end of the clause:
   <ul class="no-markers">
<li>
     Option 1: use this paragraph when bidders must submit a completed certification with their bid.
     <br/>
     "Failure to provide this certification completed with the bid will render the 
bid non-responsive."
    </li>
<li>
     Option 2: use this paragraph when bidders should submit a completed certification with their bid but if the certification is not submitted with the bid, contracting officers will request the bidder to submit the certification within a time frame.
     <br/>
     "Bidders should submit this certification completed with their bid. If the certification is not completed and submitted with the bid, the Contracting Authority will so inform the Bidder and provide the Bidder with a time frame within which to submit this completed certification. Failure to comply with the request of the Contracting Authority and submit the completed certification will render the bid non-responsive."
    </li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
   Use this clause in conjunction with
   
    A3015T
   
   .
   <br/>
   Contracting officers must consult the Canadian Content Policy (Annex 3.6 of the
   <cite>

     Supply Manual
    
</cite>
   ) to establish if the requirement is subject to the policy.
  </li>
</ol>
</section>
