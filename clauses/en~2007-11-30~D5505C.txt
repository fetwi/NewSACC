<p>The Contractor must ensure that a packing note and copies of the
quality assurance document accompany each shipment. They must be
enclosed in a waterproof envelope fastened to the last package of
the shipment or inside the last package, which is to be marked to
indicate the enclosures. In the case of a carload shipment, they
must be fastened to the inside door frame of the railway car.</p>
