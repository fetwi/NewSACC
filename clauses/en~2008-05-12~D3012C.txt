 Use the following clause in contracts for the Department of National Defence for 
preservation, packaging and packing of goods.

Use this clause with the three options in the part "resulting contract clauses" 
of the bid solicitation.  At contract award, contracting officers must choose 
and complete one of the following options, as applicable:

1.	Option 1 when delivery preparation is performed in Canada, or
2.	Option 2 when delivery preparation is performed in the United States, or
3.	Option 3 when delivery preparation is performed in the United Kingdom.
</section>
