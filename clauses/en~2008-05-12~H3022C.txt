       forms-e.html).

       Each claim must show:

       (a)    all information required on form PWGSC-TPSGC 1111;
       (b)    all applicable information detailed under the section 
              entitled "Invoice Submission" of the general conditions;

       (d)    expenditures plus pro-rated profit or fee;
       (e)    the description and value of the milestone claimed as 
       detailed in the Contract.

       (a)    a copy of time sheets to support the time claimed;
       (b)    a copy of the invoices, receipts, vouchers for all direct 
              expenses, travel and living expenses;
       (c)    a copy of the monthly progress report.

       applicable, must be calculated on the total amount of the claim 
       before the holdback is applied.  At the time the holdback is claimed, 
       there will be no GST/HST payable as it was claimed and payable under 
       the previous claims for progress payments.
