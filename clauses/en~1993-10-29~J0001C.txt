<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>This Notice of Termination for Convenience confirms the Minister's Stop 
Work Order to you dated _____.  The Minister of Supply and Services hereby 
terminates the Contract pursuant to section ____ ("Termination for 
Convenience" provision) of the General Conditions. The Termination became 
effective on the date of receipt by you of the said Stop Work Order. 

You are requested to submit to the Contracting Authority, Department of 
Supply and Services, for consideration, any claim which you may have as a 
result of this termination.  Such claim and those of your subcontractors 
and/or suppliers, if any, are to be submitted on the prescribed 
departmental termination claim forms.  On receipt of this Notice, you 
should apply in writing to the Contracting Authority for the required set 
of forms.

In preparing such claim, your attention is drawn to section ____ ("
Termination for Convenience" provision) in the General Conditions of this 
Contract and to the "Instructions - Termination for Convenience", DSS-MAS 
9403-2, in Section 1 of the Standard Acquisition Clauses and Conditions 
Manual.  Your claim and all related correspondence are to be addressed to 
the Contracting Authority, Department of Supply and Services.

Please confirm your receipt of this Notice of Termination by signing and 
returning a copy to the Contracting Authority.  

</pre>
