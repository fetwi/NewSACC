<p>
  Use the following clause in contracts when the contractor has care, custody or 
control of government property while in transit.
 </p>
<p>
  The value and basis of valuation of government property must be filled in the 
clause.  The basis of valuation of this property should be established with the 
client and mentioned in the contractor's insurance policy.  Contracting officers 
must insert one of the options provided in the second blank of paragraph 1.
 </p>
<p>
  The limits are to be evaluated on a case-by-case basis.  For further assistance, 
contact
  <abbr title="Public Works and Government Services Canada">
   PWGSC
  </abbr>
  Risk Management Advisory Services, at:
  
   NCR.RMIAS-SCGRA@pwgsc.gc.ca
  
  .
 </p>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">The Contractor must obtain on the Government's
Property, and maintain in force throughout the duration of the
Contract, All Risk Property in Transit insurance coverage for all
applicable conveyances while under its care, custody or control, in
an amount of not less than $<var>______</var> per shipment.
Government Property must be insured on <span class="input-required"><var>_________</var> basis.
<kbd>(<em><strong>Contracting officers must insert one of the following
basis of valuation:</strong> "Replacement Cost (new)"; "Actual
Cash Value (depreciated cost)" <strong>or</strong> "Agreed
Value (appraisal)"</em>)</kbd></span></li>
<li value="2">Administration of Claims: The Contractor must notify
Canada promptly about any losses or damages to Government Property
and monitor, investigate and document losses of or damage to ensure
that claims are properly made and paid.</li>
<li value="3">The All Risk Property in Transit insurance must
include the following:
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">Notice of Cancellation: The Insurer will endeavour to
provide the Contracting Authority at least thirty (30) days written
notice of any policy cancellation.</li>
<li value="2">Loss Payee: Canada as its interest appears or as it
may direct.</li>
<li value="3">Waiver of Subrogation Rights: Contractor's Insurer to
waive all rights of subrogation against Canada as represented by
<span class="input-required"><var>____________</var> <kbd>(<em><strong>insert
department</strong></em>)</kbd></span> and Public Works and Government Services
Canada for any and all loss of or damage to the property however
caused.</li>
</ol>
</li>
</ol>
