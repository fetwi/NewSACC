<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>1.     The Contractor shall, at all times during the performance of the 
       Contract, hold a valid Facility Security Clearance, issued by the 
       Industrial Security Division (ISD) of the Department of Supply and 
       Services (DSS), at the level of ____.

2.     Contractor personnel who require access to CLASSIFIED information/
       assets or sensitive work sites shall EACH hold a valid personnel 
       security screening at the level of ____ granted by ISD.

3.     The Contractor SHALL NOT remove any CLASSIFIED information from the 
       identified work site(s), and the Contractor shall ensure that its 
       personnel are made aware of and comply with this restriction. The 
       Scientific/Technical Authority may, in writing, authorize TEMPORARY 
       REMOVAL of such information or assets by the Contractor from the 
       identified work site(s).

4.     The Contractor shall comply with the provisions of the:

       a)     Security Requirements Check List (SRCL), attached at Annex ____;

       b)     Industrial Security Manual (June 1992).
