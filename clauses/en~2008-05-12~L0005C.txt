<p>
  Use the following clause in Optimized Weapon Systems Management contracts for 
the Department of National Defence (DND) when Canada owns and will provide 
Special Production Tooling and Special Test Equipment (SPT/STE) to the 
contractor.
 </p>
<p>
  The listing of
  <abbr title="Special Production Tooling and Special Test Equipment">
   SPT/STE
  </abbr>
  will be used by
  <abbr title="Department of National Defence">
   DND
  </abbr>
  authorities to determine proper 
disposition instructions later in the program.
 </p>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">Canada will provide the Special Production Tooling
and Special Test Equipment (SPT/STE) to the Contractor under the
Contract. The <abbr title="Special Production Tooling and Special Test Equipment">SPT/STE</abbr> provided by Canada will be recorded and
managed under a Department of National Defence Loan Agreement.</li>
<li value="2">The Contractor must identify all <abbr title="Special Production Tooling and Special Test Equipment">SPT/STE</abbr> owned by
Canada as Canada's property by affixing a plate, or by etching or
by stamping. The plate or marking must include reference to Public
Works and Government Services Canada file number and contract
number and a number for the <abbr title="Special Production Tooling and Special Test Equipment">SPT/STE</abbr> to identify the individual
item.</li>
</ol>
</div></pre>
