<p>The benefits that apply to this procurement are contained in:
Section 5.3, <cite>Teslin Tlingit Council Final Agreement (TTFA)</cite>,
clauses:</p>
<ul class="hierarchy">
<li><span class="number">15.7.1.1</span> In evaluating any
competitive proposal, bid or tender for the survey of Teslin
Tlingit Council Settlement Land, Government shall include among the
factors for consideration Teslin Tlingit employment and Teslin
Tlingit ownership or equity investment in the firm submitting the
subcontractor to that firm.</li>
<li><span class="number">15.7.1.2</span> The determination of the
qualifications and experience appropriate for the survey of Teslin
Tlingit Council Settlement Land shall be set out in the economic
development opportunities plan required pursuant to 22.3.1.</li>
