<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<h2>General</h2>
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">The Contractor must provide two (2) copies of the
Canada Customs Invoice (CCI) or two (2) copies of the commercial
invoice marked "For Customs Purposes Only".</li>
<li value="2">For shipments from the United States and Mexico that
are of American, Mexican or Canadian origin, as defined by the
North American Free Trade Agreement (NAFTA), and for shipments from
Israel that are Israeli in origin, as defined by the Canada-Israel
Free Trade Agreement (CIFTA), the Contractor must provide proof of
origin of the goods. This proof must be in the form of a <abbr title="North American Free Trade Agreement">NAFTA</abbr> or
<abbr title="Canada-Israel Free Trade Agreement">CIFTA</abbr> Certificate of Origin for goods valued at C$1,600 or more, or
a simple statement on the invoice for goods valued at C$1,600 or
less. In either case, the document must include an original
signature and must reference the contract number. For contracts
valued at C$250,000 or more, the proof of origin will not be
required.</li>
<li value="3">The Contractor must not employ commercial customs brokers to custom clear the goods provided under the Contract, unless authorized by the Canadian Material Support Group / Customs, at National Defence Headquarters, telephone: 1-855-210-5149, facsimile: 1-800-306-1811 or 613-971-7333.</li>
</ol>
<h2>Completion of Documents</h2>
<ol type="1">
<li value="4">The <abbr title="Canada Customs Invoice">CCI</abbr> or commercial invoice must include the
following information:
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">complete description of the goods being shipped,
including the applicable United States <cite>"Schedule
B"</cite> codes or United States Harmonized Tariff Schedule
codes;</li>
<li value="2">value and terms of sale for each item (e.g. sale,
loan, warranty, Incoterms 2000), including value of repairs,
warranty repairs or replacement costs;</li>
<li value="3">the Contract number and financial codes (use Field 3
on the <abbr title="Canada Customs Invoice">CCI</abbr> form);</li>
<li value="4">country of origin of goods;</li>
<li value="5">when a <abbr title="North American Free Trade Agreement">NAFTA</abbr>/<abbr title="Canada-Israel Free Trade Agreement">CIFTA</abbr> Certificate of Origin has been
prepared, the "Description" field of the <abbr title="Canada Customs Invoice">CCI</abbr> or commercial invoice
must include a statement confirming that it has been completed and
is attached to that invoice.</li>
</ol>
</li>
</ol>
<h2>Distribution of Documents</h2>
<ol type="1">
<li value="5">The Contractor must attach the following to shipping
container <abbr title="Number">No.</abbr> 1 of all shipments using a waterproof envelope marked
"Canada Customs Documentation":
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">one (1) copy of the <abbr title="Canada Customs Invoice">CCI</abbr> or one (1) copy of the
commercial invoice as applicable, and;</li>
<li value="2">one (1) copy of the <abbr title="North American Free Trade Agreement">NAFTA</abbr> Certificate of Origin (if
applicable).</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li value="6">The second copy of each of the above-mentioned forms
must be attached to the shipping documents.</li>
<li value="7">A copy of the <abbr title="Canada-Israel Free Trade Agreement">CIFTA</abbr> Certificate of Origin must be
faxed to 1-800-306-1811 or emailed to
DCBSCustoms@forces.gc.ca.</li>
</ol>
</div></pre>
