<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>Suppliers who are subject to the Federal Contractors Program
(FCP) and have been declared ineligible contractors by Human
Resources and Skills Development Canada (HRSDC) are no longer
eligible to receive federal government contracts over the threshold
for solicitation of bids as set out in the <cite>Government Contracts Regulations</cite>. Suppliers may be declared ineligible contractors
either as a result of a finding of non-compliance by <abbr title="Human Resources and Skills Development Canada">HRSDC</abbr>, or
following their voluntary withdrawal from the <abbr title="Federal Contractors Program">FCP</abbr> for a reason
other than the reduction of their workforce to less than 100
employees. Any bids from ineligible contractors, including a bid
from a joint venture that has a member who is an ineligible
contractor, will be declared non-responsive.</p>
<p>The Bidder, or, if the Bidder is a joint venture the member of
the joint venture, certifies its status with the <abbr title="Federal Contractors Program">FCP</abbr>, as
follows:</p>

permanent employees, and/or temporary employees having worked 12
weeks or more in Canada;</li>
<li value="2"><var title="checkbox">( )</var> is not subject to the
