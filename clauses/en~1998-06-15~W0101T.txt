<p>The benefits that apply to this procurement are contained in:
Section 5.5, <cite>Selkirk First Nation Final Agreement (SFA)</cite>,
clauses:</p>
<ul class="hierarchy">
<li><span class="number">13.12.1.1</span> Government shall provide
written notice to the Selkirk First Nation of any public tender for
contracts associated with a Designated Heritage Site directly
related to the history or culture of Selkirk People within the
Traditional Territory of the Selkirk First Nation.</li>
<li><span class="number">13.12.1.2</span> Government shall include
the Selkirk First Nation in any invitational tender for contracts
associated with a Designated Heritage Site directly related to the
history or culture of Selkirk People within the Traditional
Territory of the Selkirk First Nation.</li>
<li><span class="number">13.12.1.3</span> The Selkirk First Nation
shall have the first opportunity to accept any contract offered by
Government other than by public or invitational tenders, associated
with a Designated Heritage Site directly related to the history or
culture of Selkirk People within the Traditional Territory of the
Selkirk First Nation upon the same terms and conditions as would be
offered to others.</li>
<li><span class="number">13.12.1.7</span> Government shall include
in any contract opportunities associated with a Designated Heritage
Site directly related to the history or culture of Selkirk People
within the Traditional Territory of the Selkirk First Nation:
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">a criterion for the employment of Selkirk People or
engagement of Selkirk Firms; and</li>
<li value="2">a criterion for special knowledge or experience of
Selkirk People related to the Designated Heritage Site.</li>
</ol>
</li>
</ul>
