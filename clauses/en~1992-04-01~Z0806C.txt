<h3 class="clause-heading">Remarks â€“ Recommended Use of SACC Item</h3><section class="remarks group-sacc-remarks field-group-section">
<p>
  Use the following clause in cost-reimbursement contracts for supplies and 
services (except contracts for experimental developmental, or research work with 
educational or non-profit institutions, where no profit to the contractor is 
contemplated) under which a department is to furnish to a contractor, or a 
contractor is to acquire
  <abbr title="United States">
   U.S.
  </abbr>
  Government property.
 </p>
<p>
  Note: This clause must be used in conjunction with clause
  
   Z0807C
  
  and may be used
in conjunction with clause
  
   Z0808C
  
  (formerly
  <abbr title="United States">
   U.S.
  </abbr>
  5B, Alternate I), as applicable.
 </p>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">Government-furnished property.
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">The Government shall deliver to the Contractor, for
use in connection with and under the terms of this Contract, the
Government-furnished property described in the Schedule or
specifications, together with such related data and information as
the Contractor may request and as may be reasonably required for
the intended use of the property (hereinafter referred to as
"Government-furnished property").</li>
<li value="2">The delivery or performance dates for this Contract
are based upon the expectation that Government-furnished property
suitable for use will be delivered to the Contractor at the times
stated in the Schedule or, if not so stated, in sufficient time to
enable the Contractor to meet the Contract's delivery or
performance dates.</li>
<li value="3">If Government-furnished property is received by the
Contractor in a condition not suitable for the intended use, the
Contractor shall, upon receipt, notify the Contracting Officer,
detailing the facts, and, as directed by the Contracting Officer
and at <abbr title="United States">U.S.</abbr> Government expense, either effect repairs or
modifications or return or otherwise dispose of the property. After
completing the directed action and upon written request of the
Contractor, the Contracting Officer shall make an equitable
adjustment as provided in paragraph (h) of clause Z0807C.</li>
<li value="4">If Government-furnished property is not delivered to
the Contractor by the required time or times, the Contracting
Officer shall, upon the Contractor's timely written request, make a
determination of the delay, if any, caused the Contractor and shall
make an equitable adjustment in accordance with paragraph (h) of
clause Z0807C.</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li value="2">Changes in Government-furnished property.
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">The Contracting Officer may, by written notice,
<ol type="i">
<li value="1">decrease the Government-furnished property provided
or to be provided under this Contract; or</li>
<li value="2">substitute other Government-furnished property for the
property to be provided by the <abbr title="United States">U.S.</abbr> Government or to be acquired by
the Contractor for the <abbr title="United States">U.S.</abbr> Government under this Contract. The
Contractor shall promptly take such action as the Contracting
Officer may direct regarding the removal, shipment, or disposal of
the property covered by this notice.</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li value="2">Upon the Contractor's written request, the
Contracting Officer shall make an equitable adjustment to the
Contract in accordance with paragraph (h) of clause Z0807C, if the <abbr title="United States">U.S.</abbr> Government has agreed in the
Schedule to make such property available for performing this
Contract and there is any:
<ol type="i">
<li value="1">decrease or substitution of this property pursuant to
subparagraph (b)1. above; or</li>
<li value="2">withdrawal of authority to use property, if provided under
any other Contract or lease.</li>
</ol>
</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li value="3">Title. 
<br/><span class="description">(If clause Z0808C is referenced in the procurement
document, this paragraph (c) does not apply.)</span>
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">The <abbr title="United States">U.S.</abbr> Government shall retain title to all
Government- furnished property.</li>
<li value="2">Title to all property purchased by the Contractor for
which the Contractor is entitled to be reimbursed as a direct item
of cost under this Contract shall pass to and vest in the <abbr title="United States">U.S.</abbr>
Government upon the vendor's delivery of such property.</li>
<li value="3">Title to all other property, the cost of which is
reimbursable to the Contractor, shall pass to and vest in the <abbr title="United States">U.S.</abbr>
Government upon:
<ol type="i">
<li value="1">issuance of the property for use in Contract
performance;</li>
<li value="2">commencement of processing of the property or use in
Contract performance; or</li>
<li value="3">reimbursement of the cost of the property by <abbr title="Canadian Commercial Corporation">CCC</abbr> or the <abbr title="United States">U.S.</abbr> Government,</li>
</ol>
whichever occurs first.
</li>
<li value="4">All Government-furnished property and all property
acquired by the Contractor, title to which vests in the <abbr title="United States">U.S.</abbr>
Government under this paragraph (collectively referred to as
"Government property"), are subject to the provisions of this
clause. Title to <abbr title="United States">U.S.</abbr> Government property shall not be affected by
its incorporation into or attachment to any property not owned by
the <abbr title="United States">U.S.</abbr> Government, nor shall Government property become a fixture
or lose its identity as personal property by being attached to any
real property.</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li value="4">Use of Government property. The Government property
shall be used only for performing this Contract, unless otherwise
provided in this Contract or approved by the Contracting
Officer.</li>
<li value="5">Property administration.
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">The Contractor shall be responsible and accountable
for all Government property provided under the Contract and shall
comply with <cite>Federal Acquisition Regulation</cite> (FAR) Subpart 45.5, as
in effect on the date of this Contract.</li>
<li value="2">The Contractor shall establish and maintain a program
for the use, maintenance, repair, protection, and preservation of
Government property in accordance with sound business practice and
the applicable provisions of <abbr title="Federal Acquisition Regulation">FAR</abbr> Subpart 45.5.</li>
<li value="3">If damage occurs to Government property, the risk of
which has been assumed by the <abbr title="United States">U.S.</abbr> Government under this Contract,
the <abbr title="United States">U.S.</abbr> Government shall replace the items or the Contractor shall
make such repairs as <abbr title="Canadian Commercial Corporation">CCC</abbr> or the <abbr title="United States">U.S.</abbr> Government directs. However,
if the Contractor cannot effect such repairs within the time
required, the Contractor shall dispose of the property as directed
by the Contracting Officer. When any property for which the <abbr title="United States">U.S.</abbr>
Government is responsible is replaced or repaired, the Contracting
Officer shall make an equitable adjustment in accordance with
paragraph (h) of clause Z0807C.</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li value="6">Access. The Government and all its designees shall
have access at all reasonable times to the premises in which any
Government property is located for the purpose of inspecting the
Government property.</li>
</ol>
</div></pre>
