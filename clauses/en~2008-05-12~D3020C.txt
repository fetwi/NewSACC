<p>
  Use the following clause in contracts for the Department of National Defence 
(DND) with European Union-based contractors to define
  <abbr title="Department of National Defence">
   DND
  </abbr>
  's military packaging 
requirements for militarized items which are covered by the following categories:
 </p>
<ol type="a">
<li>
   items which are not covered by another Canadian Forces commodity packaging 
specification (see
   
    D3016C
   
   ) or commercial packaging (see
   
    D3018C
   
   );
  </li>
<li>
   repairable material for national stock; or
  </li>
<li>
   repair and overhaul of repairable material.
  </li>
</ol>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>The Contractor must prepare item number(s) <var>_____</var> for
preservation and packaging in accordance with <abbr title="North Atlantic Treaty Organization">NATO</abbr> Marking and
Packaging Standards as contained in the latest issue of
TL8100-0101/<abbr title="North Atlantic Treaty Organization">NATO</abbr>-4.</p>
<p>The Contractor must use packaging data forms previously approved
or contained in <abbr title="North Atlantic Treaty Organization">NATO</abbr>-4.</p>
<p>The Contractor must ensure approved coded packaging data is
shown immediately below the description of the corresponding item.
Where no packaging data is shown, the Contractor must submit a
packaging data form for approval.</p>
