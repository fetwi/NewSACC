<p>
  Use the following clause in contracts for the Department of National Defence (DND) when a system or equipment is newly introduced in the Canadian Forces Supply System, and when publications must be provided in accordance with
  <abbr title="Department of National Defence">
   DND
  </abbr>
  specifications and standards.
 </p>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">The Contractor must provide publications in accordance with the following specifications and standards:
<ol type="a">
<li value="1"><strong>Format:</strong> The latest issue of C-01-100-100/AG-002, <cite>Preparation of Technical Manuscripts by Contractors</cite>.
<br/> The latest issue of C-01-100-100/AG-003, <cite>Production of Reproducibles for Department of National Defence Technical Publications</cite>.
<br/>The latest issue of <cite>C-01-100-100/AG-005, Adoption of Commercial and Foreign Government Publications</cite>.</li>
<li value="2"><strong>Procurement:</strong> The latest issue of D-01-000-100/SF-000, Specification for Procurement of Publishing Services and Published Works.</li>
<li value="3"><strong>Packaging:</strong> The latest issue of D-LM-008-022/SG-000, Standard of Packaging for Documentation.</li>
<li value="4"><strong>Policy and Procedures:</strong> The latest issue of A<cite>-AD-100-100/AG-000, National Defence Publishing Policy and Administration Procedures</cite>.</li>
<li value="5"><strong>Technical Content:</strong> The technical content must meet the requirements of the latest issue of the following specifications:
<br/><cite>D-01-100-200/SF-000 - Preparation of Equipment Data Summaries</cite>;
<br/><cite>D-01-100-202/SF-000 - Preparation of Equipment Descriptions</cite>;
<br/><cite>D-01-100-203/SF-000 - Preparation of Operating Instructions</cite>;
<br/><cite>D-01-100-204/SF-000 - Preparation of Preventative Maintenance Instructions</cite>;
<br/><cite>D-01-100-205/SF-000 - Preparation of Corrective Maintenance Instructions</cite>;
<br/><cite>D-01-100-207/SF-000 - Preparation of Parts Identification Lists</cite>.</li>
<li value="6"><strong>Quality Assurance:</strong> The Contractor must conform to the Quality Assurance Program detailed in the latest issue of <cite>C-01-100-100/AG-002</cite> and
<cite>C-01-100-100/AG-003</cite>.</li></ol></li>
<li value="2">Upon request from the Contractor, the specification will be provided by the Contracting Authority.</li>
</ol>
</div></pre>
