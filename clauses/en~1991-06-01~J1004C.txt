<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>This is further to your telex/facsimile/letter of _____ addressed to _____.

As your have indicated that you are unwilling or unable to deliver the 
supplies described in the above-referenced Contract in accordance with the 
specifications, terms and conditions of the Contract, the Minister of 
Supply and Services hereby terminates the said Contract pursuant to the 
Default provisions of the General Conditions forming part of the Contract.

The Minister reserves Her Majesty's right under the Contract to charge you 
   for all loss and damage which may be suffered by Her Majesty by reason 
   of your default, including any amount in excess of the Contract Price 
   which Her Majesty may be obliged to pay in procuring the said supplies 
   elsewhere. 
</pre>
