<p>
  Use the following clause in standing offers for temporary help when personnel 
for administrative support classification must meet the standards identified in 
the clause below.
 </p>
<p>
  Contracting officers may delete the standard which does not apply to the 
requirement.
 </p>
<p>
  Contracting officers must choose one of the options under paragraph 2 and 
complete the information to advise offerors how they can obtain a copy of that 
standard.
 </p>
</section>
