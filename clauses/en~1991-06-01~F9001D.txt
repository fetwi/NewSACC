<pre>The Contractor shall treat as confidential, during as well as after the 
provision of goods or services contracted for, any information of a 
character confidential to the affairs of Her Majesty to which their 
servants or agents become privy.  All personnel assigned to provide 
services to the requisitioning department must have a current Security 
Clearance to the level specified by that department and granted by the 
Security Branch, Supply and Services Canada.

Should it become necessary during the course of the service for the 
   requisitioning department or agency to invoke further security measures, 
   it will be necessary for the Contractor to comply with the security 
   classification established at that time. 
</pre>
