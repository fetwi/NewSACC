<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>All installation work shall be subject to Government Quality Assurance (GQA).  
The Quality Assurance Authority for all work provided under this Contract 
is the Director General Quality Assurance, Department of National Defence, 
MGen George R. Pearkes Building, Ottawa, Ontario.  K1A 0K2, or his 
delegated representative.  At least 30 days prior to the commencement of 
work at the installation, the Contractor shall make arrangements for 
on-site GQA with the Director, Quality Assurance Operations, Site 
Installation Supervisor, (613) 996-4625.

The Design Change, Deviation and Waiver Procedure as defined in National 
Defence Standard D-02-006-008/SG-001 shall apply to this Contract.                                               

All work performed by the Contractor at the installation site  shall be 
controlled and inspected in compliance with the requirements of AQAP-4, 
NATO Inspection System Requirements for Industry.

Quality Control and Inspection records shall be retained by the Contractor 
for a period of three (3) years from the date of completion or termination 
of the Contract.

The Contractor is responsible for delivery, installation and proving of the 
equipment.  The equipment shall remain the responsibility of the Contractor 
until accepted by an authorized DND representative.

Proof of Government Quality Assurance is required in the form of properly 
completed release documents.  Such proof is required by DND prior to 
payment of invoices.

On completion of the installation, form CF 1280, Certificate of Inspection 
and Release, shall be prepared by the Contractor listing the contract items 
and, when properly completed and signed by the Department of National 
Defence Quality Assurance Representative (QAR), be distributed as directed 
in the Contract.


</pre>
