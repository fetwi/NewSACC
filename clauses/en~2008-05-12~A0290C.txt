<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">The Contractor acknowledges that sufficient information has been provided by Canada with respect to the location and estimated amount of hazardous materials such as asbestos, lead, <abbr title="Polychlorinated Biphenyls">PCBs</abbr>, silica or other hazardous materials or toxic substances.</li>
<li value="2">The price includes all costs associated with the removal, handling, storage, disposal and/or working in the vicinity of hazardous materials such as asbestos, lead, <abbr title="Polychlorinated Biphenyls">PCBs</abbr>, silica and other hazardous materials or toxic substances on board the vessel, including those costs resulting from the need to comply with applicable laws and regulations in relation to the removal, handling, disposal or storage of hazardous materials or toxic substances.</li>
<li value="3">The completion date for the Work takes into account the fact that the removal, handling, storage, disposal and/or working in the vicinity of hazardous materials such as asbestos, lead, <abbr title="Polychlorinated Biphenyls">PCBs</abbr>, silica and other hazardous materials or toxic substances may be affected by the need to comply with applicable laws or regulations and that this will not be considered to be an excusable delay.</li>
</ol>
