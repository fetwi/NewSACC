<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>Canada's liability to the Contractor under this Contract shall not exceed $_____, 
   unless otherwise authorized in writing by the Minister. The Contractor 
   shall not be obliged to perform any work or provide any services which 
   would cause the total liability of Canada to exceed the said sum, unless 
   an increase is so authorized.  The Contractor shall notify the Minister 
   as to the adequacy of this sum when it is 75 percent committed, or four 
   (4) months prior to the estimated date of completion of the Contract, 
   whichever comes first.  However, if at any time the Contractor considers 
   that the said sum may be exceeded, the Contractor must promptly notify 
   the Minister.
