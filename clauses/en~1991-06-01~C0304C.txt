<pre>On completion of the Contractor's obligations under the Contract, the 
Contractor shall prepare and submit a cost submission to the Science 
Contracting Officer specified in the Contract.  The cost submission shall 
be a statement of the costs incurred by the Contractor, in accordance with 
the Basis of Payment, for the Work performed under the terms of the 
Contract.

The cost submission shall be signed and certified by the Contractor's 
Senior Financial Officer and shall contain a breakdown of all applicable 
elements of cost, for example:

-     Direct material                -     General and administrative -     
Direct labour                        overhead -     Direct charges                 
-     Profit -     Subcontracts                   -     Overhead -     
Goods and services tax         -     Delivery charges       

 Supporting information for each element of cost shall be available and 
   shall be in sufficient detail that an in-depth audit can be performed. 
</pre>
