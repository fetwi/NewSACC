<pre>Use the following clause in contracts to specify the amount that will be paid 
for the pre-contractual period and to include a basis of payment for the period 
from the contract date to contract completion.  

Use this clause in conjunction with A9094C; and with C6001C which should reflect 
the total amount of paragraphs 1. and 2. below.
