indicated that Government of Canada Acquisition Card (credit card) may be used 
to pay invoices in the resulting contract, if applicable.

   H3027C

 </p>

<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>Canada requests that bidders complete one of the following:</p>
<ul class="no-markers">
<li>
<var title="checkbox">( )</var> Government of Canada Acquisition
Cards (credit cards) will be accepted for payment of invoices.
<br/>The following credit card(s) are accepted:
<ul>
<li><var title="checkbox">( )</var> VISA</li>
<li><var title="checkbox">( )</var> MasterCard</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>OR
<br/><var title="checkbox">( )</var> Government of Canada Acquisition
Cards (credit cards) will not be accepted for payment of
invoices.</li>
</ul>
<p>The Bidder is not obligated to accept payment by credit
card.</p>
<p>Acceptance of credit cards for payment of invoices will not be
considered as an evaluation criterion.</p>
</div></pre>
