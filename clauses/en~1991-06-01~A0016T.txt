<pre>(a) Bidder:  The lowest bidder is to be interpreted to mean the lowest 
responsive bidder. A bid is responsive if it meets the specifications for 
the work and other specified conditions.    

(b) Qualifications: The quoted price should reflect the requirement as 
specified; however, bid qualifications, exceptions or alterations may be 
submitted with a bid and will be considered as counter offers which may or 
may not be accepted.
