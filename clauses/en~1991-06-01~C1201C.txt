<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>The prices specified are ceiling prices and are subject to adjustment 
   downward so as not to exceed the actual cost incurred computed in 
   accordance with Contract Cost Principles, DSS-MAS 1031-2, as established 
   by government audit, plus a profit of _____ percent of such cost, and on 
   completion of the audit the prices shall be adjusted as aforesaid, and 
   if there has been any overpayment it shall be promptly refunded to 
   Canada.  The Contractor will segregate costs for each item or group of 
   items for which a ceiling price has been established so that the costs 
   of each such item or group of items may be determined.
</pre>
