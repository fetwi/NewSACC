<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>The Contractor will be paid its costs reasonably and properly
incurred in the performance of the Work, in accordance with
<cite>Contract Cost Principles</cite> 1031-2, as determined by a government
audit, plus a profit computed in accordance with Chapter 10, Cost
and Profit, of the <cite>Supply Manual</cite>, Public Works and
Government Services Canada. The results and findings of the
government's audit will be conclusive.</p>
