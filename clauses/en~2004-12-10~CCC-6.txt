       the documents and reports specified in this Contract and such other 
       documents and reports as may be required by the Buyer.

11.    Any necessary export permits are to be obtained by the Supplier 
       prior to shipment.  Assistance is available from CCC.

12.    Inspection is to be arranged by and to the satisfaction of the 
       Inspector, if any, named in this Contract to whom all questions
