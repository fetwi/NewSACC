<h3 class="clause-heading">Remarks – Recommended Use of SACC Item</h3><section class="remarks group-sacc-remarks field-group-section">
<p>
  Use the following clause in contracts for the Department of National Defence when the requisition contains line items assigned as
  <abbr title="Quality Assurance Code">
   QAC
  </abbr>
  Q. In the event the requisition has multiple quality assurance codes, the contracting officer is to identify the line item numbers which are associated with each quality assurance clause.
 </p>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>In the performance of the Work described in the Contract, the Contractor must comply with the requirements of:</p>
<p><cite><abbr title="International Organization for Standardization">ISO</abbr> 9001:2015 - Quality management systems</cite> - Requirements, published by the International Organization for Standardization (ISO), current edition at date of submission of Contractor's
bid.</p>
<p>It is not intended that the Contractor be registered to <abbr title="International Organization for Standardization">ISO</abbr>
9001; however, the Contractor's quality management system must address all requirements appropriate to the scope of the Work. Only
exclusions in accordance with clause A.5 and 4.3 of <abbr title="International Organization for Standardization">ISO</abbr> 9001 are
acceptable.</p>
<p>Assistance for Government Quality Assurance (GQA)</p>
<p>The Contractor must provide the Quality Assurance Representative (QAR) with the accommodation and facilities required for the proper accomplishment of <abbr title="Government Quality Assurance">GQA</abbr> and must provide any assistance required by
the <abbr title="Quality Assurance Representative">QAR</abbr> for evaluation, verification, validation, documentation or release of product.</p>
<p>The <abbr title="Quality Assurance Representative">QAR</abbr> must have the right of access to any site of the Contractor’s, sub-contractors or any sub-tier external providers of goods and/or services where any part of the Work is being carried out. The <abbr title="Quality Assurance Representative">QAR</abbr> must be afforded unrestricted opportunity to evaluate and verify Contractor conformity with quality system procedures and to validate product or service conformity with the requirements of the Contract. The Contractor must make available for reasonable use by the <abbr title="Quality Assurance Representative">QAR</abbr> the equipment necessary for all validation purposes. Contractor personnel must be made available for operation of such equipment as required.</p>
<p>When the <abbr title="Quality Assurance Representative">QAR</abbr> determines that <abbr title="Government Quality Assurance">GQA</abbr> is required at a subcontractors or external provider's facilities, the Contractor must provide for this in the purchasing document or other documented means and forward copies to the <abbr title="Quality Assurance Representative">QAR</abbr>, together with relevant technical data as the <abbr title="Quality Assurance Representative">QAR</abbr> may request.</p>
<p>The Contractor must notify the <abbr title="Quality Assurance Representative">QAR</abbr> of non-conforming product or service  received from a subcontractor or external provider when the product or service has been subject to <abbr title="Government Quality Assurance">GQA</abbr>.</p>
<p>For the design, development or maintenance of software, the Contractor must interpret the requirements of <cite><abbr title="International Organization for Standardization">ISO</abbr> 9001:2015 "Quality management systems - Requirements"</cite>, according to the guidelines of the latest issue (at contract date) of <cite><abbr title="International Organization for Standardization">ISO</abbr>/IEC 90003:2014 "Software engineering - Guidelines for the application of <abbr title="International Organization for Standardization">ISO</abbr> 9001:2008 to computer software"</cite>.</p>
</div></pre>
