Commercial General Liability (CGL) insurance might not be sufficient or 
appropriate for certain construction contracts. For work taking place on the 
airside of an airport,
 <abbr title="Commercial General Liability">
  CGL
 </abbr>
 should be substituted with Aviation Liability 
Insurance (G2030C). For work done from a floating plant or with a dredge,
 <abbr title="Commercial General Liability">
  CGL
 </abbr>
 should be substituted with Marine Liability Insurance (G5003C).

<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<h2>GC 9.1 General</h2>
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">The Contractor shall, at the Contractor's expense,
obtain and maintain insurance contracts in accordance with the
requirements contained herein.</li>
<li value="2">The payment of monies up to the deductible amount
made in satisfaction of a claim shall be borne by the
Contractor.</li>
</ol>
<h2>GC9.2 Proof of Insurance</h2>
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">Before commencement of the Work, and within thirty
(30) days after acceptance of its bid, the Contractor shall deposit
with Canada a Certificate of Insurance form PWGSC-TPSGC 357.
</li>
<li value="2">Upon request by Canada, the Contractor shall provide
originals or certified true copies of all contracts of insurance
maintained by the Contractor pursuant to the provisions contained
herein.</li>
<li value="3">The insurance policies shall be endorsed to provide
Canada with not less than thirty (30) days notice in writing in
advance of a cancellation of insurance or any reduction in
coverage.</li>
</ol>
<h2>GC9.3 Commercial General Liability</h2>
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">The insurance coverage provided shall not be less
than that provided by <abbr title="Insurance Bureau of Canada">IBC</abbr> Form 2100, as amended from time to time,
and shall have an Each Occurrence Limit of not less than
$5,000,000.</li>
<li value="2">The policy shall either include or be endorsed to
include coverage for the following exposures or hazards if the Work
is subject thereto:
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">Blasting.</li>
<li value="2">Pile driving and caisson work.</li>
<li value="3">Underpinning.</li>
<li value="4">Removal or weakening of support of any building or
land whether such support be natural or otherwise if the work is
performed by the insured contractor.</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li value="3">The policy shall insure the Contractor and shall
include Her Majesty the Queen in right of Canada, represented by
the Minister of Public Works and Government Services Canada as an
additional Insured, with respect to liability arising out of the
operations of the contractor with regard to the work.</li>
<li value="4">Unless otherwise directed in writing by Canada, or,
otherwise stipulated elsewhere herein, the policy required herein
shall be in force and be maintained from the date of contract award
until the day of issue of the Certificate of Completion.</li>
</ol>
</div></pre>
