<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>All work is subject to Government Quality Assurance performed at<br/>
the Contractor's or subcontractor's facility, and at the<br/>
installation site, by the Director of Quality Assurance, or its<br/>
designated Quality Assurance Representative (QAR).<br/>
<br/>Director of Quality Assurance<br/>
<br/>National Defence Headquarters<br/>
<br/>MGen George <abbr title="Reference clauses">R</abbr>. Pearkes Building<br/>
<br/>101 Colonel By Drive<br/>
<br/>Ottawa, ON K1A 0K2<br/>
<br/>E-mail: ContractAdmin.DQA@forces.gc.ca</p>
<p>Within forty-eight (48) hours of contract award, the Contractor<br/>
must contact the <abbr title="Quality Assurance Representative">QAR</abbr>. The name, location and phone number of the<br/>
<abbr title="Quality Assurance Representative">QAR</abbr> can be obtained from the nearest National Defence Quality<br/>
Assurance Region (NDQAR) listed below:</p>
<ul class="no-markers">
<li>Atlantic - Halifax 902-427-7224 or 902-427-7150</li>

<li>Ontario - Toronto 416-635-4404, ext. 6081 or 2754</li>
<li>Ontario - London 519-964-5757</li>
<li>Manitoba/Saskatchewan - Winnipeg 204-833-2500, ext. 6574</li>
<li>Alberta - Calgary 403-410-2320, ext. 3830</li>

<li>British Columbia - Vancouver 604-225-2520, ext. 2460</li>
<li>British Columbia - Victoria 250-363-5662</li>
</ul>
<p>The Contractor is responsible for performing, or having<br/>
performed, all inspections and tests necessary to substantiate that<br/>
the material or services provided conform to the requirements of<br/>
the Contract.</p>
<p>The Contractor must provide, at no additional cost, all<br/>
applicable test data, all technical data, test pieces and samples<br/>
as may reasonably be required by the <abbr title="Quality Assurance Representative">QAR</abbr> to verify conformity to<br/>
the requirements of the Contract. The Contractor must forward at<br/>
its expense such technical data, test data, test pieces and samples<br/>
to such location as the <abbr title="Quality Assurance Representative">QAR</abbr> may direct.</p>
<p>Quality control, inspection and test records that substantiate<br/>
conformity to the specified requirements, including records of<br/>
corrective actions, must be retained by the Contractor for three<br/>
(3) years from the date of completion or termination of the<br/>
Contract and must be made available to the <abbr title="Quality Assurance Representative">QAR</abbr> upon request.</p>
