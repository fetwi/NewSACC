<pre>In the event that the services provided by any employee of the supplier are 
unacceptable to the Designated User and the supplier is notified thereof 
within the first four (4) hours, the Crown shall not be charged or 
otherwise obligated to pay for those unsatisfactory services and the 
supplier shall replace the employee forthwith.

It is further understood and agreed that the supplier shall be entirely 
   responsible for any and all costs incurred with respect to such 
   temporary employee for the four (4) hour limitation period referred to 
   above.
