<pre>The Contractor must provide to Canada a technical data summary, a technical 
description or a technical specification that must include the physical and 
performance characteristics and a functional description of the deliverable 
end item, and where applicable, a complete list of accessories and 
peripheral items required to complete the deliverable end item supplied.  
For a deliverable end item purchased by the Contractor from a subcontractor 
or supplier, the Contractor must provide the name of the manufacturer and 
its part numbers along with the necessary documentation.

The Contractor must also identify any controlled goods as defined in the 
Schedule to the <em>Defence Production Act</em>, R.S., 1985, c. D-1.   All data must 
be submitted at least sixty (60) days before the scheduled delivery date of 
the deliverable end item.
