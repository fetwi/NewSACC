<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>In consideration of the Contractor satisfactorily completing all of its 
   obligations under this Contract, the Contractor shall be paid a firm 
   price of $_____.  No increase in the total liability of Canada or in the 
   price of the Work or Services resulting from any design changes, 
   modifications or interpretations of the specifications, will be 
   authorized or paid to the Contractor unless such design changes, 
   modifications or interpretations shall have been approved by the 
   Minister prior to their incorporation in the Work or Services.
