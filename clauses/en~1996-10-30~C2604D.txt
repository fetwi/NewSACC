<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>The Contractor is responsible for customs clearance of any tools, equipment 
or spare parts imported into Canada by its own employees or by those of a

for any customs duty, excise taxes and goods and services tax assessed by 
the customs officials.
