<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<ol type="1">
<li value="1"><p>The Traffic Management Directorate (TMD) is
responsible for the issue of shipping instructions and
transportation overseas.</p>
<p>Shipments shall not be effected until shipping instructions are
received from <abbr title="Traffic Management Directorate">TMD</abbr> and these will not be issued to the Contractor
until after documents identified in paragraph 8 below are
received.</p></li>
<li value="2">All documents are to reflect the financial code,
Contract Number and Consignee Number. Invoices must reflect the
Contract Item Number, Client Reference Number, description of the
commodity and values. The packing list must detail the contents
packed in each carton, box, etc., together with the individual
dimensions, cubic displacement and gross weight per carton, box,
etc.</li>
<li value="3">Documents are to be submitted covering each shipment
and are to state whether shipment is partial or complete.</li>
<li value="4">A customs export entry form B-13, obtainable from the
local Customs House or Post Office, is to be prepared for each
shipment.</li>
<li value="5">Inland bills of lading:
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">For contracts with Incoterms other than <abbr title="Ex Works">EXW</abbr> and <abbr title="Free Carrier">FCA</abbr>
(<abbr title="Free Alongside Ship">FAS</abbr>, <abbr title="Free on Board">FOB</abbr>, <abbr title="Code of Federal Regulations">CFR</abbr>, <abbr title="Cost, Insurance and Freight">CIF</abbr>, <abbr title="Carriage and Insurance Paid to">CIP</abbr>, <abbr title="Delivered At Frontier">DAF</abbr>, <abbr title="Delivered Ex Ship">DES</abbr>, <abbr title="Delivered Ex Quay">DEQ</abbr>, <abbr title="Delivered Duty Unpaid">DDU</abbr>, <abbr title="Delivered Duty Paid">DDP</abbr>) - show the
Contractor as the shipper.</li>
<li value="2">For <abbr title="Ex Works">EXW</abbr> and <abbr title="Free Carrier">FCA</abbr> contracts, prepay inland
transportation charges and show them as a separate item on the
invoice - show Canada as the shipper.</li>
</ol>
</li>
</ol>
<h2>Documentation</h2>
<h3>Dangerous Goods:</h3>
<ol type="1">
<li value="6">The nature, quantity and United Nations number of any
dangerous good are to be included with each dangerous good, in
accordance with the <cite>International Maritime Dangerous Goods Code</cite> and
<cite>International Air Transport Association Regulations</cite>.</li>
<li value="7">Non-compliance with this requirement will render the
supplier liable for any punitive action that may be taken by
regulating authorities.</li>
<li value="8"><p>Documents hereunder to accompany request for shipping
instructions to:</p>
<p>Traffic Management Directorate<br/>
National Programs Sector<br/>
Public Works and Government Services Canada<br/>
Place du Portage, Phase III<br/>
Gatineau, Quebec K1A 0S5</p>
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">seven (7) copies of the commercial invoice;</li>
<li value="2">Seven (7) copies of the packing list;</li>
<li value="3"><p>three (3) copies of the inspection report, if
inspection is required by the Contract.<br/>
<strong>Note</strong>: The Contractor must give the location of
the commodity(ies) to be shipped.</p></li>
</ol>
</li>
<li value="9">
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">For sea shipments: one (1) copy of the inland
bill of lading to <abbr title="Traffic Management Directorate">TMD</abbr>, after shipment is effected.</li>
<li value="2">For air shipments: two (2) invoices and packing lists
to accompany shipment(s) to the airport in an envelope addressed to
the consignee/clearing agent, and annotated: "Airlines: Clearing
documents for consignee/clearing agent; please attach to the air
waybill."</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li value="10">To the originating Department, at the address to
which invoices are to be forwarded:
<ol type="a">
<li value="1"><p>the original and four (4) copies of the commercial
invoice;</p>
<p>The Contractor shall include the following statement on all
invoices:</p>
<blockquote>"We certify that prices on this invoice exclude the
Goods and Services Tax, the Harmonized Sales Tax, sales tax,
refundable customs duty and excise tax paid on goods or on parts
and components incorporated in the goods."</blockquote></li>
<li value="2">one (1) signed copy of the inspection report, if
applicable;</li>
<li value="3">proof of delivery (wharf receipt, bill of lading or
air waybill).</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li value="11">To the Contracting Authority of Public Works and
Government Services Canada: one (1) copy of the commercial
invoice.</li>
