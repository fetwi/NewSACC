<p>
  Use the following clause in firm price contracts.
 </p>
<p>
  Use option 1 when all the work is subject to a firm price, firm unit price(s) or 
firm lot price(s).
 </p>
<p>
  Use option 2 when only a portion of the work is subject to a firm price or firm 
lot price(s).
 </p>
<p>
  Alternatively, use clause
  
   C0209C
  
  for contracts with task authorizations.
 </p>
</section>
