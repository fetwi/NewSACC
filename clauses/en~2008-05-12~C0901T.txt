<p>
  Use the following clause in bid solicitations for short term rental of 
photocopiers when the rental will be on a month to month basis for a period of 
less than one year.
 </p>

<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>Bidders must quote prices on a straight-line basis for the
rental of photocopiers, that is, fixed monthly rental rate (not to
include copies) and a fixed cost per copy (meter click) charge for
each model of equipment offered using the average monthly
volume.</p>
</div></pre>
