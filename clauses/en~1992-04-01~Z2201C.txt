<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>Form B13 (Customs Export Entry Form) must accompany shipments
under the following circumstances for all goods:</p>
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">exported in transit from Canada through the United
States (FMS contracts):</li>
<li value="2">exported to a country other than the United States;
or</li>
<li value="3">that fall within the following Canadian tariff
classification:
<ol type="i">
<li value="1">Airplanes (8802.30.00; 8802.40.00)</li>
<li value="2">Satellites &amp; Telecommunication equipment (8802.50.10;
8802.50.90)</li>
<li value="3">Simulators (8805.20.00)</li>
<li value="4">Ships &amp; Boats (8901.10.00; 8901.20.00; 8901.30.00;
8901.90.10; 8901.90.90; 8902.00.10; 8902.00.20)</li>
<li value="5">Uranium (2612.10.00; 2844.10.00)</li>
<li value="6">Gold (2616.90.00; 7108.11.00; 7108.12.00; 7108.13.10;
7108.13.20; 7108.20.00; 7109.00.00; 7112.10.00; 7115.90.90;
7118.90.00).</li>
</ol>
</li>
</ol>
<p>In completing form B13:</p>
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">Canadian Commercial Corporation, Ottawa, Canada, must
be shown as the exporter;</li>
<li value="2"><abbr title="Canadian Commercial Corporation">CCC</abbr> exporter <abbr title="Number">No.</abbr> MAQ616025 is to appear in "Block
No." just above <abbr title="Canadian Commercial Corporation">CCC</abbr>'s name;</li>
<li value="3">Forms are to be signed by the Contractor as
authorized agent of Canadian Commercial Corporation and show the
name of the Contractor in full.</li>
</ol>
<p class="description">N.B. All other shipments of goods, not included in the above do not require completion of form B13.</p>
