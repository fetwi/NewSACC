 Use the following clause in contracts when delivery is at destination and the 
contractor is responsible for all delivery charges, administration, costs and 
risks of transport and customs clearance, including the payment of customs 
duties and taxes.

Use Option 1 when delivery is
 <abbr title="Free on Board">
  FOB
 </abbr>
 Destination.  Use Option 2 when Incoterms 2000 
are used and delivery is "Delivered Duty Paid".

Do not use this clause in Department of National Defence (DND) contracts when
 <abbr title="Department of National Defence">
  DND
 </abbr>
 is responsible for shipping.
</section>
