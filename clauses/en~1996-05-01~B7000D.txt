<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>If prevalent throughout, the following conditions will cause rejection of 
the full pieces:

       (a)    mill creases/calendar marks;
       (b)    edge to edge shading;
       (c)    tears, holes or marks beyond 12 mm from the outer edge of the 
              selvedge;
       (d)    poor dye penetration and/or streaks;
       (e)    weak or tender fabric;
       (f)    warp or filling defects throughout.
