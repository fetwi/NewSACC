<h3 class="clause-heading">Remarks â€“ Recommended Use of SACC Item</h3><section class="remarks group-sacc-remarks field-group-section">
<p>
  Use the following clause in bid solicitations for goods when transportation 
costs must be submitted to Traffic Management Directorate, Services and 
Specialized Acquisitions Management Sector, for analysis.
 </p>
<p>
  Do not use this clause in bid solicitations for the Department of National 
Defence when it is responsible for shipping.
 </p>
<p>
  Use this clause in conjunction with
  
   C5200C
  
  , and
  
   D4000C
  
  or
  
   D4001C
  
  , if applicable.
 </p>
<p>
  For more information, consult
  
   4.70.100
  
  and
  
   5.45.10
  
  of the
  <cite>

    Supply Manual
   
</cite>
  .
 </p>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>The Bidder must provide the following information concerning
transportation costs for the delivery of the units to
destination:</p>
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">shipping weight by unit;</li>
<li value="2">number of items by unit;</li>
<li value="3">cubic measurement by unit;</li>
<li value="4">freight classification;</li>
<li value="5">name of shipping point;</li>
<li value="6">name of rail carrier, if shipment is by rail;
and</li>
<li value="7">recommended method of shipment and its costs.</li>
</ol>
</div></pre>
