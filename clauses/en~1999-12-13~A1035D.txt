<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>1.     Nothing in the Contract is intended to constitute or shall be 
       interpreted as constituting a partnership or a joint venture or an 
       agency relationship between the Parties.

2.     In its dealings with third parties in relation to the Contract, the 
       Contractor shall not represent itself as an agent or a 
       representative of the Government of Canada.  It is understood and 
       agreed that neither the Contractor nor any of its personnel is 
       engaged as an employee, servant or agent of Canada.

</pre>
