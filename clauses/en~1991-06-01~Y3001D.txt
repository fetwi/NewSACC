<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>Packaging shall be suitable for domestic shipment to consignment address. 
Only identical items are to be contained in any one interior package, 
except that sets of tools, parts, etc., are to be packed as individual sets, 
whenever possible.  The contents of each interior package are to be clearly 
indicated by label or tag showing the item number, quantity, customer 
reference number, and Contract number.

Only items from one Contract shall be packaged in one exterior container.

One copy of the detailed packing list shall be included with the package.
