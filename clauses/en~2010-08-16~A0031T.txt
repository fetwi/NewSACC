<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>A bid must comply with the requirements of the bid solicitation
and meet all mandatory technical evaluation criteria to be declared
responsive. The responsive bid with the lowest evaluated price will
be recommended for award of a contract.</p>
