<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>The Contractor shall use the Contract Plan and Report Form, DSS-MAS 9143 
(or an equivalent form which is acceptable to the Contracting Authority)to 
report the progress of the work and the costs to date against the original 
workplan. An updated copy of the form must accompany each claim for payment.  
Receipt and acceptance of the form by the Contracting Authority will be a 
condition of payment for such claims.  (A French version,<strong> "Formule de 
projet de contrat et de rapport</strong>", form DSS-MAS 9143-1, is also available.)
