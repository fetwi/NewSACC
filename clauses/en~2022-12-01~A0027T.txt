<h3 class="clause-heading">Remarks â€“ Recommended Use of SACC Item</h3><section class="remarks group-sacc-remarks field-group-section">
<p>
  Use the following clause when the bid solicitation contains mandatory and point-rated technical evaluation criteria, and the basis of selection will be the responsive bid with the highest combined rating of technical merit and price.
 </p>
<p>
  Contracting officers must select one of the options provided in the clause.
 </p>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">To be declared responsive, a bid must: <ol type="a">
<li value="1">comply with all the requirements of the bid solicitation; and</li>
<li value="2">meet all mandatory criteria; and</li>
</ol> <span class="description">Instruction to contracting officers: choose one of the following options:</span>
<ul class="no-markers">
<li>
<h2>Option 1</h2>
<ol type="a">
<li value="3">obtain the required minimum of <span class="input-required"><var>____</var> <kbd>(<em>insert minimum number of points</em>)</kbd></span> points overall for the technical evaluation criteria which are subject to point rating. <br/>The rating is performed on a scale of <span class="input-required"><var>____</var> <kbd>(<em>insert total number of available points</em>)</kbd></span> points. </li>
</ol>
</li>
<li><strong>OR</strong> <br/>
<h2>Option 2</h2>
<ol type="a">
<li value="3"> obtain the required minimum points specified <span class="input-required"><kbd>(choose "for each criterion"<strong> OR </strong>"for criterion number <var>___</var>"<strong> OR </strong>"for criteria numbers <var>___</var> , <var>___</var>")</kbd></span> for the technical evaluation, and </li>
<li value="4"> obtain the required minimum of <span class="input-required"><var>____</var> <kbd>(<em>insert minimum number of points</em>)</kbd></span> points overall for the technical evaluation criteria which are subject to point rating. <br/>The rating is performed on a scale of <span class="input-required"><var>____</var> <kbd>(<em>insert total number of available points</em>)</kbd></span> points. </li>
</ol>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li value="2"> Bids not meeting (choose "(a) or (b) or (c)" OR "(a) or (b) or (c) and (d)") will be declared non-responsive. </li>
<li value="3"> The selection will be based on the highest responsive combined rating of technical merit and price. The ratio will be <span class="input-required"><var>____</var> % <kbd>(<em>insert the percentage for technical merit</em>)</kbd></span> for the technical merit and <span class="input-required"><var>____</var> % <kbd>(<em>insert the percentage for price</em>)</kbd> for the price.</span> </li>
<li value="4"> To establish the technical merit score, the overall technical score for each responsive bid will be determined as follows: total number of points obtained / maximum number of points available multiplied by the ratio of <span class="input-required"><var>____</var> % <kbd>(<em>insert the percentage for technical merit</em>)</kbd></span>. </li>
<li value="5"> To establish the pricing score, each responsive bid will be prorated against the lowest evaluated price and the ratio of <span class="input-required"><var>____</var> % <kbd>(<em>insert the percentage for price</em>)</kbd></span>. </li>
<li value="6"> For each responsive bid, the technical merit score and the pricing score will be added to determine its combined rating. </li>
<li value="7"> Neither the responsive bid obtaining the highest technical score nor the one with the lowest evaluated price will necessarily be accepted. The responsive bid with the highest combined rating of technical merit and price will be recommended for award of a contract. </li>
</ol>
