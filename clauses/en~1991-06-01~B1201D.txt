<pre>Packages containing items having a shelf life are to be marked with the 
   date of manufacture.  Where the item is fabricated of rubber or 
   synthetic rubber, the cure date of the rubber is to be shown in lieu of 
   the date of manufacture.  Where the item contains rubber or synthetic 
   rubber components, the cure date of the rubber is to be shown in 
   addition to the date of manufacture or date of overhaul. Markings are to 
   be placed in accordance with the identification requirements of the 
   applicable Marking Specification CFPS-2 _____.
