<p>
  Use the following clause in contracts for the purchase of wheat when prices are 
subject to adjustment in accordance with any increase or decrease established by 
the
  
   Canadian Wheat Board
  
  or the Ontario Marketing Board.
 </p>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>The prices detailed in the Contract are subject to upward or
downward adjustment to allow for any increase or decrease
established by the Canadian Wheat Board or the Ontario Marketing
Board. A copy of the Contractor's notification of price increase or
decrease from the applicable Board must be provided to the
Contracting Authority.</p>
<p>The Contract will be amended to reflect the actual price of the
increase or decrease at time of delivery. The Contractor must not
invoice at prices other than those specified in the Contract.</p>
</div></pre>
