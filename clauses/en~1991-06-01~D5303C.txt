<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>All Work shall be subject to Department of National Defence quality 
assurance.  Within forty-eight (48) hours of receipt of this Contract, the 
Contractor will make arrangements with the Department of National Defence 
Inspector who normally services the Contractor's plant and provide the 
Inspector with a copy of this Contract. The name, location and telephone 
number of the Inspector may be obtained from the Canadian Forces Technical 
Services Agency _____(insert city), _____(insert telephone number).

For contracts involving site installation, the Contractor will make 
arrangements with the Director, Quality Assurance Operations, Site 
Installation Supervisor, Ottawa, Ontario (613) 996-4625.

Proof of DND quality assurance is required in the form of properly 
   completed release documents.  Such proof is required by DND prior to 
   payment of invoices.
