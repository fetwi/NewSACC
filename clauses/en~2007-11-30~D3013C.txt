<p>
  Use the following clause in contracts with Canadian-based contractors to define 
Department of National Defence's military packaging requirements for militarized 
items which are covered by the following categories:
 </p>
<ol type="a">
<li>
   items not covered by a Canadian Forces commodity packaging specification 
(see
   
    D3016C
   
   ) or commercial packaging (see
   
    D3018C
   
   );
  </li>
<li>
   repairable material for national stock;
  </li>
<li>
   repair and overhaul of repairable material.
  </li>
</ol>
<p>
  Use this clause in conjunction with
  
   D3019C
  
  in the part "resulting contract 
clauses" of the bid solicitation.  At contract award, use either this clause or
  
   D3019C
  
  depending on whether the contractor is Canadian-based or from the United 
States.
 </p>
</section>
