 Use the following clause in contracts when General Conditions 2030 or 2040 are 
used and the warranty section is to be amended to indicate that the contractor 
will be responsible for all costs associated with the replacement, repair or 
making good of the work or any part of the work.

For General Conditions 2010A, use clause K0031C.
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>Section 21 entitled Warranty of general conditions <span class="input-required"><var>_________</var> <kbd>(<i>insert 2030 or
2040</i>)</kbd></span> is amended by deleting subsections 3 and 4 in its
entirety and replacing it with the following:</p>
<ol type="1">
<li value="3">The Work or any part of the Work found to be
defective or non-conforming will be returned to the Contractor's
plant for replacement, repair or making good. However, when in the
opinion of Canada it is not expedient to remove the Work from its
location, the Contractor must carry out any necessary repair or
making good of the Work at that location. In such cases, the
Contractor will be responsible for all Costs (including travel and
living expenses) incurred in so doing, Canada will not reimburse
these Costs.</li>
<li value="4">The Contractor must pay the transportation cost
associated with returning the Work or any part of the Work to the
Contractor's plant pursuant to subsection 3. The Contractor must
also pay the transportation cost associated with forwarding the
replacement or returning the Work or part of the Work when
rectified to the delivery point specified in the Contract or to
another location directed by Canada.</li>
</ol>
<p>All other provisions of the warranty section remain in
effect.</p>
</div></pre>
