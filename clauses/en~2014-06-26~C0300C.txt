<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">Upon completion of the Contract or annually for
multi-year contracts spanning more than one contractor fiscal year,
the Contractor must complete the form PWGSC-TPSGC 7953 (PDF 87KB) - (Help on File Formats), Contractors Cost Submission, and forward three (3) copies
to the Contracting Authority. Costs will be computed in accordance
with <cite>Contract Cost Principles</cite> 1031-2.</li>
<li value="2">The cost submission must contain a breakdown of all
applicable cost elements as detailed in the Contract and must be
signed and certified accurate by the Contractor's Senior Financial
Officer, unless stated otherwise in writing by the Contracting
Authority.</li>
<li value="3">Supporting information for each cost element must be
available in sufficient detail to allow for an in-depth audit.</li>
</ol>
</div></pre>
