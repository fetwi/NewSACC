<pre>A Packing Note and copies of the Quality Assurance document are to 
   accompany each shipment.  They are to be enclosed in a waterproof 
   envelope fastened to the last package of the shipment or inside the last 
   package, which is to be marked to indicate the enclosures, or in the 
   case of a carload shipment, are to be fastened to the inside door frame 
   of the railway car.
