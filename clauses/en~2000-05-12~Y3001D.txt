<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>Packaging shall be suitable for domestic shipment to consignment
address. Only identical items are to be contained in any one
interior package, except that sets of tools, parts, etc., are to be
packed as individual sets, whenever possible. The contents of each
interior package are to be clearly indicated by label or tag
showing the item number, quantity, customer reference number, and
contract number.</p>
<p>Only items from one contract shall be packaged in one exterior
container.</p>
<p>One copy of the detailed packing list shall be included with the
package.</p>
