<pre>Use the following clause in contracts for services when firm hourly rates are 
established for professional fees and when travel and living expenses and direct 
expenses are to be reimbursed at actual cost without mark-up.

Add or delete paragraphs as applicable to meet specific requirements.  Add the 
paragraph relative to the option to extend the contract, if applicable.

Use this clause in conjunction with C6001C.</pre>
</section>
