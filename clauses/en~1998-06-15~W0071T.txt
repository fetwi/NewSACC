<p>The benefits that apply to this procurement are contained in:
Section 5.2, <cite>Champagne and Aishihik First Nations Final Agreement</cite>
(CAFA), Chapter 10, Schedule A, clauses:</p>
<ul class="hierarchy">
<li><span class="number">9.3</span> The Canadian Parks Service
shall provide the Champagne and Aishihik First Nations with a right
of first refusal to accept any contract offered by the Canadian
Parks Service for the use of horses in the Park, which right of
first refusal shall be offered in the following manner:
<ul class="hierarchy">
<li><span class="number">9.3.1</span> the Canadian Parks Service
shall provide notice to the Champagne and Aishihik First Nations
specifying the terms and conditions of the contract;</li>
<li><span class="number">9.3.2</span> where the Champagne and
Aishihik First Nations does not tender acceptance, the Canadian
Parks Service may offer the contract publicly on the same terms and
conditions specified in the notice pursuant to 9.3.1; and</li>
<li><span class="number">9.3.3</span> if the contract offered
publicly is not accepted, the Canadian Parks Service may re-offer
the contract on new terms and conditions in accordance with the
procedure set out in 9.3.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
