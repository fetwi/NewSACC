<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>Compliance with the certifications provided by the Contractor in
its bid is a condition of the Contract and subject to verification
by Canada during the term of the Contract. If the Contractor does
not comply with any certification or it is determined that any
certification made by the Contractor in its bid is untrue, whether
made knowingly or unknowingly, Canada has the right, pursuant to
the default provision of the Contract, to terminate the Contract
for default.</p>
