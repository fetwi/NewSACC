<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>When the Contractor is required to supply other than commercial
off-the-shelf batteries or must make a special production run of
batteries, the following over/under run allowances are
acceptable:</p>
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">for quantities 1 to 500, plus or minus 5
percent;</li>
<li value="2">for quantities 501 to 5,000, plus or minus 2.5
percent; and</li>
<li value="3">for quantities in excess of 5,000, plus or minus 1
percent.</li>
</ol>
