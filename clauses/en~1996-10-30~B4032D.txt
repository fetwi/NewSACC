<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>The pilot-in-command of the aircraft shall provide a safety briefing for 
all passengers before flight. Said briefing is to be conducted in 
accordance with the document entitled "Safety Briefing", and documented by 
the signature of the senior representative of the Charterer boarding the 
aircraft. Such documentation may be provided on a separate confirmation 
form, provided it contains, as a minimum, the information stipulated under 
the heading "Customer Safety Briefing Confirmation".  Copies of the Safety 
Briefing document can be obtained from the Contracting Authority named on 
page 1 of this document.
