<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>This Notice of Partial Termination for Convenience confirms the Minister's 
Stop Work Order to you dated _____, in respect only of Item _____(part 
number, FSN, description, quantity). The Minister of Supply and Services 
hereby partially terminates the Contract in respect only of the 
above-described item.  The termination became effective on the date of 
receipt by you of the said Stop Work Order.  You shall continue with the 
rest of the Work in accordance with the terms, conditions and 
specifications of the Contract.

You are requested to submit to the Department of Supply and Services, any 
claim which you may have as a result of this partial termination. Such 
claim and those of your subcontractors and/or suppliers, if any, are to be 
submitted on the prescribed departmental termination claim forms. On 
receipt of this Notice, you should apply in writing for the required set of 
forms.

In preparing such claim, your attention is drawn to section _____ ("
Termination for Convenience" provision) in the General Conditions of the 
Contract, and to the "Instructions" on the back of this Notice of 
Termination. Your claim and all related correspondence are to be addressed 
to the Contracting Officer, Department of Supply and Services. 

Please confirm your receipt of this Notice of Partial Termination by 
   signing and returning a copy to the Contracting Officer.  
</pre>
