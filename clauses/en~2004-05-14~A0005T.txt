<pre>The following factors will be taken into consideration in the evaluation of 
each bid:

(a)    Technical compliance;
(b)    Delivery requirement;
(c)    Inspection requirement;
(d)    Packaging requirement;

(f)    Transportation costs (where applicable);
(g)    Compliance with proposed pricing methods;
(h)    Price;
(i)    Acceptance of terms and conditions as mentioned in the bid 
       solicitation;
(j)    Completion of the solicitation.
