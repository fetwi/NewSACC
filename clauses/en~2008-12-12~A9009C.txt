<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>The Contractor grants to Canada the irrevocable option to extend
the term of the Contract by up to <var>_______</var> additional
<var>________</var> year period(s) under the same conditions. The
Contractor agrees that, during the extended period of the Contract,
it will be paid in accordance with the applicable provisions as set
out in the Basis of Payment.</p>
<p>Canada may exercise this option at any time by sending a written
notice to the Contractor at least <var>_____</var> calendar days
before the expiry date of the Contract. The option may only be
exercised by the Contracting Authority, and will be evidenced for
administrative purposes only, through a contract amendment.</p>
</div></pre>
