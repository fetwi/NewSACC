<p>In order to ensure that funds will be available to complete the Work in accordance with the conditions of the Contract, the Contractor must, within <var>_____</var> calendar days after the date of contract award, provide to the Contracting Authority a duly executed performance bond form PWGSC-TPSGC 505. The performance bond must be <var>______</var> percent of the Contract Price and must be accepted as security by one of the bonding companies listed in <cite>Treasury Board Contracting Policy, Appendix L</cite>, Acceptable Bonding Companies.</p>
<p>If Canada does not receive the performance bond within the specified period, Canada may terminate the Contract for default pursuant to the default provision of the Contract.</p>
