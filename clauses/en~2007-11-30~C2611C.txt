<ol type="1">
<li value="1">As the goods to be supplied under the Contract are
defence supplies, customs duties on importation to Canada may be
remitted under the Tariff Item Number 9982.00.00 of the Schedule to
the <cite>Customs Tariff</cite>.</li>
<li value="2">Remission of customs duties payable may be granted
under the Tariff Item Number 9982.00.00 when the total contract
value of the defence supplies is C$250,000 or more. This reflects
the import value of the goods plus the duty that would be
applicable in the absence of the <cite>Customs Tariff</cite>.</li>
<li value="3">The Contractor will be responsible for pre-arranging
remission on importation or for paying customs duties on
importation and applying to Canada Border Services Agency for a
refund. The Contractor is also responsible for applying to Public
Works and Governments Services Canada in good time for the
certification required by the <cite>Customs Tariff</cite>.</li>
