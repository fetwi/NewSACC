<pre>Canada requires that the bid be signed by the Bidder or by an authorized 
   representative of the Bidder.  If a bid is being submitted by a joint 
   venture, the bid must clearly state that it is submitted as a joint 
   venture and must be signed by all members of the joint venture or a 
   statement must be provided to the effect that the signatory represents 
   all members of the joint venture.
</pre>
