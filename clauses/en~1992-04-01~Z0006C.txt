<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">The term "<dfn><abbr title="Free on Board">FOB</abbr> origin, freight allowed</dfn>", as used in
this clause, means:
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">free of expense to the <abbr title="United States">U.S.</abbr> Government, delivered:
<ol type="i">
<li value="1">on board the indicated type of conveyance of the
carrier (or of the <abbr title="United States">U.S.</abbr> Government, if specified) at a designated
point in the city, county, and province or state from which the
shipment will be made and from which line-haul transportation
service (as distinguished from switching, local drayage, or other
terminal service) will begin;</li>
<li value="2">to, and placed on, the carrier's wharf (at shipside within
reach of the ship's loading tackle when the shipping point is
within a port area having water transportation service) or the
carrier's freight station;</li>
<li value="3">to a <abbr title="United States">U.S.</abbr>/Canadian postal service facility; or</li>
<li value="4">if stated in the solicitation, to any Government-designated
point located within the same city or commercial zone as the <abbr title="Free on Board">FOB</abbr>
origin point specified in the Contract (commercial zones are
prescribed by the <abbr title="United States">U.S.</abbr> Interstate Commerce Commission at <cite>49 <abbr title="Code of Federal Regulations">CFR</abbr>
1048</cite>); and</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li value="2">an allowance for freight based on applicable
published tariff rates (or Government rate tenders) between the
points specified in the Contract, is deducted from the Contract
Price.</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li value="2">The Contractor shall:
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">
<ol type="i">
<li value="1">pack and mark the shipment to comply with
Contract specifications; or</li>
<li value="2">in the absence of specifications, prepare the shipment in
conformance with carrier requirements to protect the goods and to
ensure assessment of the lowest applicable transportation
charge;</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li value="2">
<ol type="i">
<li value="1">order specified carrier equipment when requested
by the <abbr title="United States">U.S.</abbr> Government; or</li>
<li value="2">if not specified, order appropriate carrier equipment not
in excess of capacity to accommodate shipment;</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li value="3">deliver the shipment in good order and condition to
the carrier, and load, stow, trim, block, and/or brace carload or
truckload shipment (when loaded by the Contractor) on or in the
carrier's conveyance as required by carrier rules and
regulations;</li>
<li value="4">be responsible for any loss of and/or damage to the
goods
<ol type="i">
<li value="1">occurring before delivery to the carrier;</li>
<li value="2">resulting from improper packing and marking; or</li>
<li value="3">resulting from improper loading, stowing, trimming,
blocking, and/or bracing of the shipment if loaded by the
Contractor on or in the carrier's conveyance;</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li value="5">complete the <abbr title="United States">U.S.</abbr> Government Bill of Lading supplied
by the ordering agency, or when a <abbr title="United States">U.S.</abbr> Government Bill of Lading is
not supplied, prepare a commercial Bill of Lading or other
transportation receipt. The Bill of Lading shall show:
<ol type="i">
<li value="1">a description of the shipment in terms of governing
freight classification or tariff (or <abbr title="United States">U.S.</abbr> Government rate tender)
under which lowest freight rates are applicable;</li>
<li value="2">the seals affixed to the conveyance with their serial
numbers or other identification;</li>
<li value="3">lengths and capacities of cars or trucks ordered and
furnished;</li>
<li value="4">other pertinent information required to effect prompt
delivery to the consignee, including name, delivery address, postal
address and code or ZIP code of consignee, routing, etc.;</li>
<li value="5">special instructions or annotations requested by the
ordering agency for commercial Bills of Lading, e.g. (A) "to be
converted to a <abbr title="United States">U.S.</abbr> Government Bill of Lading", or (B) "this
shipment is the property of, and the freight charges paid to the
carrier(s) will be reimbursed by, <abbr title="Canadian Commercial Corporation">CCC</abbr>"; and</li>
<li value="6">the signature of the carrier's agent and the date the
shipment is received by the carrier; and</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li value="6">distribute the copies of the Bill of Lading, or other
transportation receipts, as directed by the ordering agency.</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li value="3">These Contractor responsibilities are specified for
performance at the plant or plants at which the supplies are to be
finally inspected and accepted, unless the facilities for shipment
by carrier's equipment are not available at the Contractor's plant,
in which case the responsibilities shall be performed <abbr title="Free on Board">FOB</abbr> the point
or points in the same or nearest city where the specified carrier's
facilities are available subject, however, to the following
qualifications:
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">If the Contractor's shipping plant is located in the
State of Alaska or Hawaii, the Contractor shall deliver the
supplies listed for shipment outside Alaska or Hawaii to the port
of loading in Alaska or Hawaii, respectively, as specified in the
Contract, at Contractor's expense, and to that extent the Contract
shall be "FOB destination."</li>
<li value="2">Notwithstanding subparagraph (c) 1. of this clause,
if the Contractor's shipping plant is located in the State of
Hawaii, and the Contract requires delivery to be made by container
service, the Contractor shall deliver the supplies, at Contractor's
expense, to the container yard in the same or nearest city where
seavan container service is available.</li>
</ol>
</li>
</ol>
