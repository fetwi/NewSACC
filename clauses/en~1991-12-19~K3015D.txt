<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>The Contractor, during the performance of the Contract and for a period of ____ 
   months thereafter, shall keep confidential and shall not publish or 
   otherwise disclose to any person any Technical Information, except as 
   may be necessary to carry out the Work under the Contract in which case 
   the Contractor shall impose the same obligation of confidentiality on 
   any person to whom the information is disclosed.  The foregoing 
   obligation does not apply to any Technical Information where the same 
   information is publicly available from a source other than Canada.  
</pre>
