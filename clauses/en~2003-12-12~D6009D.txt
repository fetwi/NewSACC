<h3 class="clause-heading">Remarks – Recommended Use of SACC Item</h3><section class="remarks group-sacc-remarks field-group-section">
<pre>Use the following clause in contracts and bid solicitations competed on behalf 
of Department of National Defence where destination and delivery schedules are 
not known at the time of execution.
</pre>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>1.     The Contractor shall ship prepaid DDP Delivery Duty Paid (_____named 
       placeof destination).  Unless otherwise directed, delivery shall be 
       made by the most economical means.  Shipping charges shall be shown 
       as a separate item on the Contractor's invoice.  The Contractor is 
       responsible for all delivery charges, administration, costs and 
       risks of transport and customs clearance, including the payment of 
       customs duties and taxes.

2.     The Contractor shall make deliveries to Canadian Forces (CF) Supply 
       Depots by appointment only.  The Contractor or its carrier shall 
       arrange delivery appointments by contacting the Depot Traffic 
       Section at the appropriate location shown below.  The consignee may 
       refuse shipments when prior arrangements have not been made.
