<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>The Bidder certifies that the price proposed

(a)    is not in excess of the lowest price charged anyone else, including 
       the Bidder's most favoured customer, for the like quality and 
       quantity of the goods, services or both;

(b)    does not include an element of profit on the sale in excess of that 
       normally obtained by the Bidder on the sale of goods, services or 
       both of like quality and quantity, and

(c)    does not include any provision for discounts to selling agents.
</pre>
