 Use the following clause in contracts which include specific disposal 
requirements, when work is performed on government premises and the contractor 
is required to dispose of hazardous waste that is removed from or uncovered on 
the site in accordance with applicable laws.

Use clause
 
  A9019C
 
 when the contract does not include specific disposal 
requirements but the contractor must still comply with any applicable laws.
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>The Contractor must dispose of any hazardous waste removed or
uncovered in the performance of the Work in accordance with the
requirements of the Contract and any applicable law.</p>
