<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>Following issuance of a Standing Offer, it is the Offeror's responsibility 
to supply and update price lists and/or catalogues as Canada may require.  
The Offeror shall provide one (1) copy of its catalogue and price list and 
updates thereto to each Identified User requesting a copy.  The Offeror 
shall further send one (1) copy to the Contracting Authority at the address 
stated in this document.
