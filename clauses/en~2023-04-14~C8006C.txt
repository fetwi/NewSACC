<h3 class="clause-heading">Remarks – Recommended Use of SACC Item</h3><section class="remarks group-sacc-remarks field-group-section">
<p>
  Use the following clause where a contractor is provided with the opportunity to earn additional profit upon achievement of one or more technical performance goals, in accordance with Supply Manual section
  
   4.70.25.5
  
  and the
  
   Practitioner’s Guide for Procurement Pricing
  
  , section 4.4.1.
 </p>
<p>
  The contractor may earn an incentive based on pre-defined measurable and objective criteria on technical parameters related to the procurement that are of
  <strong>
   critical importance to Canada
  </strong>
  .
  <strong>
   To earn the incentive, the contractor’s performance must be over and above the requirements specified in the Statement of Work
  </strong>
  .
 </p>
<p>
  The specified performance levels must be accepted by the client and be reasonable.
 </p>
<p>
  Use option 1 when the incentive payment is fixed.
 </p>
<p>
  Use option 2 when the incentive payment is variable in relation to the performance of the contractor.
 </p>
</section>
