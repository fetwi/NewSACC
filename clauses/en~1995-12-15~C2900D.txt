<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>The Contractor agrees that, pursuant to the provisions of the <em>Income Tax 
Act</em>, Canada is empowered to withhold an amount of 15 percent of the price 
to be paid to the Contractor, if the Contractor is a non-resident 
contractor as defined in said act.  This amount will be held on account 
with respect to any liability for taxes which may be owed to Canada.
