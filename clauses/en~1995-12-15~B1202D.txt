<pre>Age control for age sensitive elastomeric materials as specified in 
Canadian Forces Specification D-05-001-001/SF-000 shall be applied to all 
aircraft, aircraft components, aircraft accessories and to elastomeric 
items when the elastomeric items are used in contact with fuel, hydraulic 
fluid, oil, alcohol, or oxygen, or when the elastomeric items form part of 
a pneumatic, coolant or any other fluid or gaseous systems.



</pre>
