<p>
  Use the following clause in competitive requests for standing offers for 
multiple items (line item) when competition is conditionally limited to Canadian 
goods and the certification must be done on an item by item basis as defined in 
paragraph 3(b) of clause
  
   A3050T
  
  .
 </p>
<p>
  Contracting officers must select one of the options provided in the clause.
 </p>
<p>
  Use clause
  
   M3063T
  
  for multiple items when the certification must be done on an 
aggregate basis.
 </p>
<p>
  Use this clause in conjunction with
  
   A3050T
  
  and
  
   M3060C
  
  .
 </p>
<p>
  Contracting officers must consult the Canadian Content Policy (
  
   Annex 3.6
  
  of the
  <cite>

    Supply Manual
   
</cite>
  ) to establish if the requirement is subject to the policy.
 </p>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>This procurement is conditionally limited to Canadian goods.</p>
<p>Subject to the evaluation procedures contained in the request
for standing offer, offerors acknowledge that only offers for items
with a certification that the item(s) offered are Canadian goods,
as defined in clause A3050T, may be
considered.</p>
<p>Failure to provide this certification completed with the offer
will result in the item(s) offered being treated as non-Canadian
goods.</p>
<p class="description"><strong>Instruction to contracting
officers:</strong> Choose one of the following options:</p>
<ul class="no-markers">
<li>
<blockquote>"Offerors must clearly identify beside each item listed
in the request for standing offer document which items meet the
definition of Canadian good and complete the following
certification."</blockquote>
<strong>OR</strong>
</li>
<li>
<blockquote>"Offerors must clearly identify below which items meet
the definition of Canadian good and complete the certification
below."</blockquote>
</li>
</ul>
<p>The Offeror certifies that:</p>
<p><var title="checkbox">( )</var> the item(s) offered and
identified as Canadian goods are Canadian goods as defined in
paragraph 1 of clause A3050T.</p>
</div></pre>
