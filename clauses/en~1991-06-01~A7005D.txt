<pre>1.     Monthly maintenance charges shall be suspended for any item of 
       equipment which is not operational. 

2.     A maintenance credit shall apply to all items of equipment which are 
   not operational and whose availability falls below 90 percent over any 
   thirty-day (30) period.  The credit shall be equal to the monthly 
   maintenance rate for that non-operational equipment multiplied by the 
   difference between 100 percent and the availability of the equipment. 
</pre>
