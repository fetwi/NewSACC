<pre>Packages containing items having a shelf life must be marked with the date 
of manufacture. Where the item contains elastomeric material, the cure date 
is to be shown in addition to the date of manufacture or date of overhaul. 
Markings are to be placed in accordance with the identification 
requirements of the current issue of the applicable Marking Specification 
D-LM-008-002/SF-001.
