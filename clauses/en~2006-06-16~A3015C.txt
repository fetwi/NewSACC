<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>Compliance with the certifications provided by the Contractor in its bid is 
   a condition of the Contract and subject to verification by Canada during

   unknowingly, the Minister has the right, pursuant to the default 
   provision of the Contract, to terminate the Contract for default.
