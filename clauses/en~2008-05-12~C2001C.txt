<pre>Use the following clause in contracts for goods when the contract price does not 
include any duties and/or taxes required to be paid on imported goods to be 
delivered to Canada under the contract and that will be exported by Canada.

Refer to Supply Manual procedure 7E.602.</pre>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>The Contract Price does not include any duties and/or taxes required to be 
paid on imported goods to be delivered to Canada under the Contract and 
that will be exported by Canada.

Canada waives to the Contractor all its rights of drawback accruing in 
connection with the Contract pursuant to the <em>Goods Imported and Exported 
Refund and Drawback Regulations</em> and the Canada Border Services Agency (CBSA) 
Memorandum D7-3-8, Goods Purchased by Canadian Government Departments.

Canada will provide upon request from the Contractor a Drawback Certificate 
which will enable the Contractor to claim duties drawback from CBSA.


</pre>
