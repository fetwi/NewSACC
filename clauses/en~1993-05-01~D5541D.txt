<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>In the performance of the Work described herein, the Contractor shall 
comply with the following:

ISO 9002  Quality System Requirements for Production and Installation, 
published by the International Standard Organization (ISO), current edition 
at date of submission of Contractor's bid.

<strong>Inspection, Measuring and Test Equipment</strong>

The Contractor's measurement and calibration system shall be in accordance 
with the requirements of ISO 10012-1 (Quality Assurance Requirements for 
Measuring Equipment), current edition at date of submission of Contractor's 
bid.

<strong>Assistance for Government Quality Assurance (GQA)</strong>

The Contractor shall provide the Quality Assurance Representative (QAR) 
with the accommodation and facilities required for the proper 
accomplishment of GQA and shall provide any assistance required by the QAR 
for evaluation, verification, documentation or release of product. 

The QAR shall have the right of access to any area of the Contractor's or 
Subcontractor's facilities where any part of the contracted work is being 
performed.  The QAR shall be afforded unrestricted opportunity to evaluate 
compliance with Quality System procedures and to verify conformance of 
product with contract requirements.  The Contractor shall make available 
for reasonable use by the QAR the equipment necessary for all verification 
purposes.  Contractor personnel shall be made available for operation of 
such equipment as required.

When the QAR determines that government verification is required at a 
Subcontractor's facilities, the Contractor shall provide for this in the 
purchasing document and provide a copy to the QAR along with such technical 
data relating to the Subcontractor's product as the QAR may request.

The Contractor shall notify the QAR when a product has been received from a 
Subcontractor and GQA reveals that the product does not conform to contract 
requirements.

</pre>
