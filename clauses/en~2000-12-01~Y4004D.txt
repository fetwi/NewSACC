<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<h2>Dangerous Goods:</h2>
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">The nature, quantity and United Nations number of any
dangerous goods is to be included with each dangerous good, in
accordance with the <cite>International Maritime Dangerous Goods Code</cite> and
the latest <cite>International Air Transport Association
Regulations</cite>.</li>
<li value="2">Non-compliance with this requirement by the supplier
will render him liable for any punitive action that may be taken by
regulating authorities.</li>
<li value="3">Documents are to be distributed as follows:
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">To the Canadian International Development Agency
(CIDA), at the address to which invoices are to be forwarded:
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">three (3) copies of non-negotiable bills of lading;</li>
<li value="2">original and four (4) copies of the commercial invoice made
out to <abbr title="Canadian International Development Agency">CIDA</abbr>.</li>
</ol>
<p class="description">The Contractor shall include the following statement on all
invoices:</p>
<blockquote>"We certify that the prices on this invoice exclude the
Goods and Services Tax, the Harmonized Sales Tax, sales tax,
refundable customs duty and excise tax paid on goods or on parts
and components incorporated in the goods."</blockquote>
<ol type="1">
<li value="3">five (5) copies of the packing list;</li>
<li value="4">one (1) copy of the inspection report, if inspection is
required by the Contract;</li>
<li value="5">delivery report at destination.</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li value="2">To the clearing agent (consignee if no clearing agent
is stated in the Contract):
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">two (2) original bills of lading (for custom purposes);</li>
<li value="2">two (2) copies of non-negotiable bills of lading;</li>
<li value="3">two (2) copies of the commercial invoice;</li>
<li value="4">two (2) copies of the packing list.</li>
</ol>
</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>Note</strong>: The above two (2) sets of documentation
to the clearing agent are to be airmailed under two (2) separate
covers to ensure safe arrival of at least one (1) set at
destination.</p>
<ol type="a">
<li value="3">To the Contracting Authority of Public Works and
Government Services Canada: one (1) copy of the commercial
invoice.
<p><strong>Payment</strong>: Against commercial invoices and
documentation as stated above by <abbr title="Canadian International Development Agency">CIDA</abbr>. Delivery Report is the
critical document for payment. It replaces the original bill of
lading used for other terms, such as C. &amp; F. or C.I.F.</p></li>
</ol>
</li>
</ol>
