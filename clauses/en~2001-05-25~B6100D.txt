<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>The Contractor shall be solely responsible for the stability and trim of 
the ship during the period the ship is in the Contractor's facility, 
including docking and undocking.  To this end, the Contractor shall 
maintain weight change information pertinent to the ship's stability during 
the docking period.  The Department of National Defense will supply the 
Contractor with cross curves of stability, hydrostatic curves, tank status, 
location of centre of gravity, and other information relevant to the ship's 
condition upon handing over of the vessel.
