<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>1.     Progress payments will only be made upon receipt of satisfactory 
       progress claim forms specified in the Contract.

       Authority for appropriate certification after inspection and 
       acceptance of the Work takes place.  Upon certification, the 
       original and two (2) copies are then forwarded to the Contracting 
       Authority for certification and onward submission to the Project 
       Manager's paying office for all remaining certifications and payment 
       action.

3.     Progress claims shall not be submitted by the Contractor until all 
       Work identified in the claim has been completed.

4.     GOODS AND SERVICES TAX (GST) IS TO BE CALCULATED AND PAID ON THE 
       ENTIRE AMOUNT OF THE CLAIM BEFORE THE 10 PERCENT HOLDBACK IS APPLIED. 
       AT THE TIME THAT THE HOLDBACK IS RELEASED, THERE WILL BE NO GST 
       PAYABLE, AS IT WAS INCLUDED IN THE PREVIOUS PAYMENTS.
