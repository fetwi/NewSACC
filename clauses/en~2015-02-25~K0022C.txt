<p>Section 30 of general conditions 2035 is amended by adding the
following subsection:</p>
<p>If the Contractor has submitted to Canada a
cancellation policy that is acceptable to Canada, subsection 2 will

subsection 1, will be paid cancellation cost in accordance with the
provisions of the Contractor's cancellation policy.</p>
