<p>
  Use the following clause in contracts where the contractor is required to ship dangerous goods/hazardous products. Suppliers have responsibilities regarding the shipment of dangerous goods/hazardous products which are specified in the
  <cite>

    Transportation of Dangerous Goods Act
   
</cite>
  , 1992 and
  <cite>

    Hazardous Products Act
   
</cite>
  , and their associated Regulations.
 </p>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>The Contractor must label and ship dangerous goods/hazardous products falling within the <cite>Transportation of Dangerous Goods Act</cite>, 1992, <abbr title="chapter">c</abbr>.34 and the <cite>Hazardous Products Act</cite>, <abbr title="Revised Statutes of Canada">R.S.C.</abbr> 1985, <abbr title="chapter">c.</abbr> H-3 and their regulation(s) in accordance with the said Acts and regulation(s) accompanied by the required safety data sheet(s) completed in both English and French.</p>
</div></pre>
