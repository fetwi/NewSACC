<pre><div class="sacc-item">
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">Bidders must provide bid financial security consisting of:
            <ol type="a">
<li value="1">a security deposit as defined in clause <a href="https://buyandsell.gc.ca/policy-and-guidelines/standard-acquisition-clauses-and-conditions-manual/5/E/E0008T/active">E0008T</a>;  or</li>
<li value="2">a bid bond form <a href="http://www.tpsgc-pwgsc.gc.ca/app-acq/forms/504-eng.html" rel="external">PWGSC-TPSGC 504</a>, which must be accepted as security by one of the bonding companies, in accordance with the <cite>Guidance on Source Lists of Companies Licensed to provide Surety Within Canada</cite>. </li>
</ol>
</li>
<li value="2">Security deposits in the form of government guaranteed bonds with coupons attached will be accepted only if all coupons that are unmatured, at the time the security deposit is provided, are attached to the bonds. Bidders must provide written instructions concerning the action to be taken with respect to coupons that will mature while the bonds are pledged as security, when such coupons are in excess of the security deposit requirement.</li>
<li value="3">If the financial security is in the form of a bill of exchange or a government guaranteed bond and:
            <ol type="a">
<li value="1">the bid price is $250,000 or less, the amount of the security deposit must represent <var>____</var> <var>(___)</var> percent of the bid price; or</li>
<li value="2">the bid price exceeds $250,000, the amount of the security deposit must be $<var>_____</var> plus <var>____</var> <var>(___)</var> percent of the amount by which the bid price exceeds $250,000, to a maximum of $<var>_____</var> .</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li value="4">If the financial security is a bid bond, the amount of the bond must represent <var>____</var> <var>(___)</var> percent of the bid price.</li>
<li value="5">Bidders who provide a security deposit as bid financial security must submit their bid under seal (does not apply in Quebec).</li>
</ol>
