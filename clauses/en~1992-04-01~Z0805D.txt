<p>
  As prescribed in
  <abbr title="Federal Acquisition Regulation">
   FAR
  </abbr>
  45.106(d), insert the following clause in solicitations and contracts when a fixed-price, time-and-material, or labour-hour contract is 
contemplated and that the acquisition cost of all Government-furnished property 
to be involved in the contract is $50,000 or less; unless a contract with an 
educational or nonprofit organization is contemplated.
 </p>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">The <abbr title="United States">U.S.</abbr> Government shall deliver to the Contractor,
at the time and locations stated in this Contract, the
Government-furnished property described in the Schedule or
specifications. If that property, suitable for its intended use, is
not delivered to the Contractor, the Contracting Officer shall
equitably adjust affected provisions of this Contract in accordance
with the Changes clause, if applicable, or the clause that
establishes procedures for amending the Contract, when:
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">the Contractor submits a timely written request for
an equitable adjustment; and</li>
<li value="2">the facts warrant an equitable adjustment.</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li value="2">Title to Government-furnished property shall remain
in the <abbr title="United States">U.S.</abbr> Government. The Contractor shall use the
Government-furnished property only in connection with this
Contract. The Contractor shall maintain adequate property control
records in accordance with sound industrial practice and will make
such records available for <abbr title="Canadian Commercial Corporation">CCC</abbr> or <abbr title="United States">U.S.</abbr> Government inspection at all
reasonable times, unless the clause at <cite>Federal Acquisition
Regulation</cite> 52.245-1, Property Records, is included in this
Contract.</li>
<li value="3">Upon delivery of Government-furnished property to the
Contractor, the Contractor assumes the risk and responsibility for
its loss or damage, except:
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">for reasonable wear and tear;</li>
<li value="2">to the extent property is consumed in performing this
Contract; or</li>
<li value="3">as otherwise provided for by the provisions of this
Contract.</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li value="4">Upon completing this Contract, the Contractor shall
follow the instructions of the Contracting Officer regarding the
disposition of all Government-furnished property not consumed in
performing this Contract or previously delivered to <abbr title="Canadian Commercial Corporation">CCC</abbr> or the <abbr title="United States">U.S.</abbr>
Government. The Contractor shall prepare for shipment, deliver <abbr title="Free on Board">FOB</abbr>
origin, or dispose of the Government property, as may be directed
or authorized by the Contracting Officer. The net proceeds of any
such disposal shall be credited to the Contract Price or shall be
paid to <abbr title="Canadian Commercial Corporation">CCC</abbr> or the <abbr title="United States">U.S.</abbr> Government as directed by the Contracting
Officer.</li>
</ol>
</div></pre>
