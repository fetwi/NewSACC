<h3 class="clause-heading">Remarks â€“ Recommended Use of SACC Item</h3><section class="remarks group-sacc-remarks field-group-section">
<pre>Use the following clause in all contracts with contractors located in 
California, when ownership will not be transferred to Canada until delivery of 
the goods, and when the contracting authority is to be informed of all duties 
and/or taxes that may or may not be required to be paid on the exported goods.

Use this clause in conjunction with D4003C.

Use this clause in conjunction with K9010C when the contract provides for 
progress or advance payments, or when the goods must remain in the State of 
California for a period of time.

Refer to Supply Manual procedures 6D.456 to 6D.462 and 7E.621 to 7E.623.</pre>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>The Contractor must inform the Contracting Authority of all customs duties 
and of all sales, consumption, use, excise, personal property or any other 
taxes the Contractor proposes to pay or not to pay.  The Contractor must 
carry out any instructions the Contracting Authority may give in respect of 
payment or non-payment of these taxes.
