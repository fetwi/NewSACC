 Use the following clause in non-competitive bid solicitations for fixed time 
rate contracts, for commercial services valued at $50,000 or more, submitted to 
a Canadian-based bidder.

Use this clause in conjunction with
 
  C0100C
 
 or
 
  C0705C
 
 , as applicable.

<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>The Bidder certifies that the rate proposed:</p>
<div type="1">
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">is not in excess of the lowest rate charged anyone
else, including the Bidder's most favoured customer, for the like
quality and quantity of the service;</li>
<li value="2">does not include an element of profit on the sale in
excess of that normally obtained by the Bidder on the sale of
services of like quality and quantity, and</li>
<li value="3">does not include any provision for discounts to
selling agents.</li>
</ol>
