<pre>Use the following clause in contracts when progress payment against milestone 
will be made in accordance with an established schedule of milestones using form 
PWGSC-TPSGC 1111 and the amount claimed is not subject to holdback.  Use clause 
H3009C when the amount claimed is subject to holdback.

Use this clause in conjunction with H0004C, and if applicable use with H3022C or 
H3024C and H4012C.

Contracting officers should review the policy on progress payments (Supply 
Manual, Section 6E).  Milestone payment is a form of progress payment which is 
addressed under the policy related to progress payments.</pre>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>Canada will make milestone payments in accordance with the Schedule of 
Milestones detailed in the Contract and the payment provisions of the 
Contract if:

(a)    an accurate and complete claim for milestone payment using 
       PWGSC-TPSGC 1111, and any other document required by the Contract 
       have been submitted in accordance with the invoicing instructions 
       provided in the Contract.  Form PWGSC-TPSGC 1111 is available at the 
       following Website:  http://www.pwgsc.gc.ca/acquisitions/text/forms/
       forms-e.html;
       
(b)    all the certificates appearing on form PWGSC-TPSGC 1111 have been 
       signed by the respective authorized representatives; 
       
(c)    all work associated with the milestone and as applicable any 
       deliverable required has been completed and accepted by Canada.



</pre>
