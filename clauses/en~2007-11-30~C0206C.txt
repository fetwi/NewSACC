<pre>Use the following clause in contracts when the work is subject to a limitation 
of expenditure.

Use Option 1 when all the work is subject to a limitation of expenditure.
Use Option 2 when only a portion of the work is subject to a limitation of 
expenditure.

Use this clause in conjunction with C6000C.</pre>
