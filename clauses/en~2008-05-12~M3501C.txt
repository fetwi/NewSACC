<p>
  Use the following clause in standing offers for the purchase of petroleum 
products when prices are subject to adjustment in accordance with any increase 
or decrease established by the petroleum producer.
 </p>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">The prices detailed in the Standing Offer are subject
to upward or downward adjustment to allow for:
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">changes due to price adjustment in petroleum products
that are a direct result of increased or decreased prices imposed
by the petroleum producer. A copy of the Offeror's notification of
price increase or decrease from the petroleum producer must be
provided to the Standing Offer Authority; and/or</li>
<li value="2">imposition of any new or changes to any existing
levies, tariffs or fees of whatsoever nature applicable to any
petroleum product, authorized, imposed or agreed to by Canada or
any provincial government or by any Governmental Regulatory
Authority.</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li value="2">The Standing Offer will be revised to reflect the
actual price of the increase or decrease at time of delivery. The
Offeror must not invoice at prices other than those specified in
the Standing Offer.</li>
</ol>
</div></pre>
