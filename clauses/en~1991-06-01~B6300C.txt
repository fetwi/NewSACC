<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>The Contractor shall conform to such Canadian Forces supply instructions as 
   issued by the Technical Services Agency covering the demanding, handling, 
   storage and maintaining of adequate records of contract issue stores 
   owned by Canada.  Notice of any shortages shall be given in duplicate on 
   form CF 152 to the Contracting Officer named on page 1, who shall 
   determine whether such shortage or shortages are normal, consideration 
   being given to the volume of such stores handled by the Contractor.  
   Each notice shall be supported by a letter setting forth the reasons for 
   the deficiencies and indicating on a percentage basis the relationship 
   of the quantity of such shortage to the total quantity acquired per item.  
   The Contractor shall be responsible for any shortage in excess of such 
   normal shortage. 
</pre>
