<p>
  Use the following clause in contracts when a client department, other than 
National Research Council Canada, claims exemption of customs duties for goods 
imported under Customs Tariff, Schedule II, Annex Code 1760. The goods must be 
used in teaching, research or other specified use.
 </p>
<p>
  Contracting officers must insert the end-use and the authority number specified 
by the client department.
 </p>
</section>
