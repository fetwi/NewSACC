<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>When the Contractor performs work in the Canadian Forces Base (CFB) Esquimalt Dockyard, that is usually performed by employees of the Ship Repair Group, such work must normally be performed in accordance with the methods, practices, or work distribution prevailing at the said Dockyard which takes into consideration the trade jurisdictional limitations of the Federal Government Dockyards Trades and Labour Council (Victoria) affiliates.</p>
<p>Should there be a need for any work permit, this requirement must be borne by the Contractor or subcontractor. In this instance, the work permit will be ten dollars ($10) per worker per week and will be paid only when the workers of the outside Contractor or subcontractor do not belong to the same unions affiliated with the Federal Government Dockyards Trades and Labour Council (Victoria).</p>
