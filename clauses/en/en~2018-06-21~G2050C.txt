<h3 class="clause-heading">Remarks â€“ Recommended Use of SACC Item</h3><section class="remarks group-sacc-remarks field-group-section">
<p>
  Use the following clause where the contractor has care, custody or control of government property for maintenance or repair such as dry cleaners and repair 
facilities.  For storage facilities, use clause
  
   G2052C
  
  .
 </p>
<p>
  The value and basis of valuation of government property must be filled in the clause.  The basis of valuation of this property should be established with the 
client and mentioned in the contractor's insurance policy.  Contracting officers must insert one of the options provided in the second blank of paragraph 1.
 </p>
<p>
  The limits are to be evaluated on a case-by-case basis. For further assistance, contact
  <abbr title="Public Works and Government Services Canada">
   PWGSC
  </abbr>
  Risk Management Advisory Services, at:
  
   NCR.RMIAS-SCGRA@pwgsc.gc.ca
  
  .
 </p>
</section>
