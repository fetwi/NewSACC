<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>Where the Bidder has a labour agreement, or other suitable
instrument, in place with its unionized labour, it must be valid
for the proposed period of any resulting contract. Documentary
evidence of the agreement or suitable instrument must be provided
on or before bid closing date.</p>
