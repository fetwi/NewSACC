<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>The benefits that apply to this procurement are contained in:
Section 5.3, <cite>Teslin Tlingit Council Final Agreement</cite> (TTFA),
clauses:</p>
<ul class="hierarchy">
<li><span class="number">13.12.1.1</span> Government shall provide
written notice to the Teslin Tlingit Council of any invitation for
public tenders for contracts associated with the management of a
Designated Heritage Site directly related to the history or culture
of Teslin Tlingit Council Traditional Territory.</li>
<li><span class="number">13.12.1.2</span> The Teslin Tlingit
Council shall have the first opportunity to accept any fixed term
contract offered by Government associated with the management of
Designated Heritage Site directly related to the history or culture
of Teslin Tlingit within the Teslin Tlingit Council Traditional
Territory.</li>
<li><span class="number">13.12.1.5</span> Government shall include
in any contract opportunities associated with the management of a
Designated Heritage Site directly related to the history or culture
of Teslin Tlingit in the Teslin Tlingit Council Traditional
Territory:
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">a criterion for Teslin Tlingit employment; and</li>
<li value="2">a criterion for special Teslin Tlingit knowledge or
experience related to the Heritage Site.</li>
</ol>
</li>
</ul>
