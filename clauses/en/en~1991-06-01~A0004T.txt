<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>GATT AGREEMENT: As this requirement is subject to the GATT Agreement on 
Government Procurement, the following terms and conditions are applicable 
to this bid solicitation.  

       l. Suppliers who are not registered on the Department of Supply and 
       Services Source List must complete a copy of the Regional 
       Application for Source Listing, form DSS-MAS 10550.  

       2. Bid documents and supporting information may be submitted either 
       in English or in French.  

       3. All prices quoted must be expressed in Canadian dollars.  

       4. For suppliers located in Canada: Prices must be firm with 
       Canadian customs duties and excise taxes as applicable INCLUDED, and 
       goods and services tax as applicable EXCLUDED.  

       For suppliers located outside of Canada: Prices must be firm with 
       Canadian customs duties, goods and services tax and excise taxes 
       EXCLUDED.  

       5. Before award of contract, suppliers may be requested to provide 
       specific information with respect to their legal and financial 
       status, and their technical capability to supply the requirement as 
       stipulated in the bid solicitation.  

       6. All bidders must indicate the country of origin of imported 
       products to their respective country, including imported material or 
       components used by the bidders or their sub-contractors in the 
       manufacture of the end product.  

       7. Although the Minister reserves the right to award the contract 
       either on a FOB plant or FOB destination basis, suppliers must 
       provide prices FOB their plant or shipping point and FOB 
       destination(s) as indicated in Appendix A of the bid solicitation.  

       8. Contract(s) will be awarded to competent/qualified supplier(s) 
       who provide the best value to the Crown with respect to price, 
       technical compliance and delivery requirements.  

       9. Use of Canadian Labour and Materials, para. 20 of form DSS-MAS 
   1026 (S-100A), General Conditions - Supplies - Firm Price, do not apply 
   to this solicitation/contract.
