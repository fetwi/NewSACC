<h3 class="clause-heading">Remarks – Recommended Use of SACC Item</h3><section class="remarks group-sacc-remarks field-group-section">
<p>
  Use the following clause in cost reimbursable contracts with hourly rates for 
on-site maintenance services.
 </p>
<p>
  If Incoterms 2000 apply, replace
  <abbr title="Free on Board">
   FOB
  </abbr>
  Destination with
  <abbr title="Carriage Paid To">
   CPT
  </abbr>
  Carriage Paid To 
(...named place of destination),
  <abbr title="Carriage &amp; Insurance Paid">
   CIP
  </abbr>
  Carriage and Insurance Paid to (...named 
place of destination) or
  <abbr title="Delivered Duty Paid">
   DDP
  </abbr>
  Delivered Duty Paid (...named place of destination).
 </p>
<p>
  Add or delete paragraphs as applicable to meet specific requirements.
 </p>
</section>
