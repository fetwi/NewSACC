<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>1.     Where the Contractor is required to supply other than commercial 
       off-the-shelf batteries or must make a special production run of 
       batteries, the following over/under run allowances are acceptable:

       (a)    for quantities 1 to 500, plus or minus 5 percent;
       (b)    for quantities 501 to 5000, plus or minus 2.5 percent; and
       (c)    for quantities in excess of 5000, plus or minus 1 percent.
