<h3 class="clause-heading">Remarks â€“ Recommended Use of SACC Item</h3><section class="remarks group-sacc-remarks field-group-section">
<p>
  Use the following clause in contracts for the Department of National Defence to 
outline the contractor's responsibility to inspect and provide an 
inspection/test report of actual measurements taken when procurement is for 
Safety Critical Class 3 Threaded Fasteners,
  <abbr title="North Atlantic Treaty Organization">
   NATO
  </abbr>
  Stock Number classes 5305, 
5306, 5307, 5310 and 5315.
 </p>
<p>
  Use this clause in conjunction with
  
   D5510C
  
  or
  
   D5515C
  
  .
 </p>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">For items identified in the Contract as Safety
Critical Class 3 fasteners, <abbr title="North Atlantic Treaty Organization">NATO</abbr> Stock Number classes 5305, 5306,
5307, 5310 and 5315, the Contractor must provide to the Quality
Assurance Representative (QAR) a valid inspection/test report of
the results obtained for the following parameters:
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">"GO" functional diameter size;</li>
<li value="2">pitch diameter size;</li>
<li value="3">major diameter size (external threads only);</li>
<li value="4">minor diameter size (does not apply to
<span class="input-required"><var>______</var> <kbd>(<em>insert applicable
specification</em>)</kbd></span> external threads);</li>
<li value="5">root radius (applies to <span class="input-required"><var>______</var>
<kbd>(<em>insert applicable specification</em>)</kbd></span>
external threads only);</li>
<li value="6">flank angle;</li>
<li value="7">lead (including helix variations);</li>
<li value="8">circularity;</li>
<li value="9">taper;</li>
<li value="10">runout; and</li>
<li value="11">surface roughness.</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li value="2">Definitions of these terms can be found in the latest
issue of <span class="input-required"><var>______</var> <kbd>(<em>insert

<li value="3">If the differential between "GO" functional and pitch

<li value="4">The Contractor must provide actual measurement

<li value="5">If the inspection/test report is not available from
