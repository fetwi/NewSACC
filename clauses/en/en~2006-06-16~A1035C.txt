could give the impression or lead to the interpretation that a partnership could 
be established between the contractor and Canada.  Contracting officers must not

<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>Nothing in the Contract is intended to constitute or must be interpreted as 
   constituting a partnership between the Parties, and the Contractor must 
   not represent itself as a representative of the Government of Canada in 
   its dealings with third parties.
