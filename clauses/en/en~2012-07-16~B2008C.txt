<p>
  Use the following clause in contracts when institutions listed in Schedule I,
  <br/>
  I.1 and II of the
  <cite>

    Financial Administration Act
   
</cite>
  must comply with the Treasury
  <br/>
  Board policy on Common Look and Feel (CLF) Standards for the Internet.
 </p>
<p>
<abbr title="Common look and feel">
   CLF
  </abbr>
  Standards offer a consistent and predictable presentation of government services and content,
  <br/>
  which facilitate effective online interaction between the Government of Canada and Canadians.
  <br/>
<abbr title="Common look and feel">
   CLF
  </abbr>
  provides standards on web addresses, common Web page formats, e-mail, accessibility,
  <br/>
  interoperability and usability of Web sites.
 </p>
<p>
<abbr title="Common look and feel">
   CLF
  </abbr>
  Standards are revised periodically by TB. Contracting officers are invited to consult the most
  <br/>
  recent version available on the
  <cite>

    Standard on Web Usability
   
</cite>
  Web site.
 </p>
<p>
  Any contract for electronic goods or services that are related to public facing
  <br/>
  Web sites or sub-sites and public facing Web applications of the Government of
  <br/>
  Canada, performed for a department or agency that is an institution listed in
  <br/>
  the above mentioned schedules of the Act, should contain a clause referring the
  <br/>
  contractor to the obligation of complying with the most recent version of the
  <br/>
<abbr title="Common look and feel">
   CLF
  </abbr>
  Standards.
 </p>
<p>
<abbr title="Common look and feel">
   CLF
  </abbr>
  Standards may also be complemented by standards and guidelines developed by
  <br/>
  institutions. It is therefore necessary to refer to those standards and
  <br/>
  guidelines as well.
 </p>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>The Work must comply with the standards established in the
Treasury Board (TB) policy on <cite>Standard on Web Usability</cite></p>
<p>The Work must also comply with the standards and guidelines that may be developed by the department or agency for whom the Work is being performed and which are a complement of the above mentioned <abbr title="Treasury Board">TB</abbr> policy. Such standards and guidelines are available from the department's or agency's <abbr title="Common look and feel">CLF</abbr> Centre of Expertise. For Public Works and Government Services Canada (PWGSC), contact the Communications Officer at: CommunicationsCQauWeb.CommunicationsWebQC@tpsgc-pwgsc.gc.ca.</p>
