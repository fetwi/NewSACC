<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>As provided for in <abbr title="United States">U.S.</abbr> Contract no. <var>____</var> which
includes the following:</p>
<p>This clause applies to cost reduction proposals initiated and
developed by the Contractor for changing the drawings, designs,
specifications, or other requirements of this Contract. This clause
does not, however, apply to any such proposal unless it is
identified by the Contractor, at the time of its submission to the
 <abbr title="United States">U.S.</abbr> Contracting Officer, as a proposal submitted pursuant to this
clause. The cost reduction proposals contemplated are those
that:</p>
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">would require, in order to be applied to this
Contract, a change to this Contract; and</li>
<li value="2">would result in savings to the <abbr title="United States">U.S.</abbr> Government by
providing
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">a decrease in the cost of performance of this
Contract, without impairing any of the items' essential functions
and characteristics such as service life, reliability, economy of
operation, ease of maintenance, and necessary standardized
features; or</li>
<li value="2">items, regardless of the acquisition cost, producing
a net reduction in the cost of government-furnished property,
operations, maintenances, or other areas which exceed any increased
acquisition cost, without impairing any of the items' essential
functions and characteristics.</li>
</ol>
</li>
</ol>
<p>Any Value Engineering Proposal must include the information
outlined in sub clause 2) of the Value Engineering Incentive clause
of the Contract between <abbr title="Canadian Commercial Corporation">CCC</abbr> and the <abbr title="United States">U.S.</abbr> Government and be
submitted in accordance with instructions from the <abbr title="United States">U.S.</abbr> Contracting
Officer.</p>
<p>In connection with the Value Engineering Incentive clause, the
Contractor will receive a share percentage of all savings in the
amount of <var>___</var> percent of the savings under this Contract
plus <var>___</var> percent of the projected collateral savings and
<var>_____</var> percent of the savings payments for a period of
two (2) years.</p>
<p><strong>Note</strong>: See <abbr title="United States">U.S.</abbr> Contract for percentage factors.</p>
