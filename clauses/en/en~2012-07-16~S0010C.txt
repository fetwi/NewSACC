<h3 class="clause-heading">Remarks â€“ Recommended Use of SACC Item</h3><section class="remarks group-sacc-remarks field-group-section">
<p>
  Use the following clause in supply arrangements when periodic usage reports are 
required from the supplier.
 </p>
<p>
  The supply arrangement authority must consult
  
   8.75.1
  
  of the
  <cite>

    Supply Manual
   
</cite>
  for 
instructions on the reporting requirements. The reporting requirements must be 
detailed in an annex to the supply arrangement and must include the data to be 
reported on by the supplier.
 </p>
<p>
  The reporting periods and the number of calendar days by which each report must 
be submitted after the reporting period must be indicated in the clause.
 </p>
<p>
  The supply arrangement authority must ensure that the supplier fulfills all the 
reporting requirements detailed in the supply arrangement.
 </p>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>The Supplier must compile and maintain records on its provision of goods, services or both to the federal government under contracts resulting from the Supply Arrangement. This data must include all purchases, including those paid for by a Government of Canada Acquisition Card.</p>
<p>The Supplier must provide this data in accordance with the
reporting requirements detailed in Annex "<var>______</var>". If
some data is not available, the reason must be indicated. If no
goods or services are provided during a given period, the Supplier
must still provide a "NIL" report.</p>
<p>The data must be submitted on a <span class="input-required"><var>_______</var> <kbd>(<em>insert "quarterly basis"
or specify an alternate reporting period</em>)</kbd></span> to the Supply
Arrangement Authority.</p>
<p class="description">(If an alternate reporting period is required, delete the
quarterly periods provided below and define the alternate reporting
period.)</p>
<p>The quarterly reporting periods are defined as follows:</p>
<ul class="no-markers">
<li>1st quarter: April 1 to June 30;</li>
<li>2nd quarter: July 1 to September 30;</li>
<li>3rd quarter: October 1 to December 31;</li>
<li>4th quarter: January 1 to March 31.</li>
</ul>
<p>The data must be submitted to the Supply Arrangement Authority
no later than <span class="input-required"><var>______</var>
<kbd>(<em>insert number of days</em>)</kbd></span> calendar days after the end
of the reporting period.</p>
</div></pre>
