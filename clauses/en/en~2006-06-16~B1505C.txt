<p>
  Use the following clause in contracts where the contractor is required to ship hazardous materials.  Suppliers have responsibilities regarding the shipment of hazardous materials which are specified in the
  <cite>

    Hazardous Products Act
   
</cite>
  , the Controlled Products Regulation and the federal and provincial occupational safety and health legislation.
 </p>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>The Contractor must label and ship goods falling within the
<cite>Hazardous Products Act</cite>, <abbr title="Revised Statutes of Canada">R.S.C.</abbr> 1985, <abbr title="chapter">c.</abbr> H-3 and regulation(s) in accordance with the said Act and regulation(s) accompanied by the required material safety data sheet(s) completed in either English or French. The label must clearly identify the contents of the hazardous material and the material safety data sheet must explain what those hazards are.</p>
