<h3 class="clause-heading">Remarks â€“ Recommended Use of SACC Item</h3><section class="remarks group-sacc-remarks field-group-section">
<p>
  Use the following clause in contracts when the price of the work is subject to a limitation of expenditure.
 </p>
<p>
  Use Option 1 when all the work is subject to a limitation of expenditure. Use Option 2 when only a portion of the work is subject to a limitation of expenditure.
 </p>
<p>
  Use this clause in conjunction with
  
   C6001C
  
  ; for option 2, clause
  
   C6001C
  
  applies only to the portion of the work which is subject to a limitation of expenditure.
 </p>
<p>
  Alternatively, use clause
  
   C0204C
  
  for contracts with task authorizations.
 </p>
</section>
