<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>All Work shall be subject to government quality assurance.  The Contractor 
will be notified officially by the Technical Services Section, Canadian 
Defence Liaison Staff, 1 Grosvenor Square, London W1X 0AB, England, on

Contractor's plant or area.

Proof of government quality assurance is required in the form of properly 
   completed Certificate of Conformity in accordance with STANAG 4107.  
   Such proof is required by DND prior to payment of invoices.
