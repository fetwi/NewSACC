  Use the following clause in bid solicitations to advise bidders that they could 
propose improvements to the technical requirements contained in the bid 
solicitation.
 </p>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>Should bidders consider that the specifications or Statement of
Work contained in the bid solicitation could be improved
technically or technologically, bidders are invited to make
suggestions, in writing, to the Contracting Authority named in the
bid solicitation. Bidders must clearly outline the suggested
improvement as well as the reason for the suggestion. Suggestions
that do not restrict the level of competition nor favour a
particular bidder will be given consideration provided they are
submitted to the Contracting Authority at least
<var>__________</var> days before the bid closing date. Canada will
have the right to accept or reject any or all suggestions.</p>
</div></pre>
