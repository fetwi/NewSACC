<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">The Contractor must ensure proper labelling and packaging in the supply and shipping of dangerous goods/hazardous products to the Government of Canada.</li>
<li value="2">The Contractor will be held liable for any damages caused by improper packaging, labelling or carriage of dangerous goods/hazardous products.</li>
<li value="3">The Contractor must clearly mark all merchandise labels with the percentage of volume that is a hazardous item. Failure to do so will result in the Contractor being held responsible for damages caused in the movement of goods/products by government vehicles or government personnel.</li>
<li value="4">The Contractor must adhere to all applicable laws regarding dangerous goods/hazardous products.</li>
</ol>
