<h3 class="clause-heading">Remarks â€“ Recommended Use of SACC Item</h3><section class="remarks group-sacc-remarks field-group-section">
 Use the following clause in contracts when the work is subject to a ceiling 
price and costs will be audited in accordance with Contract Cost Principles 
1031-2.

Use Option 1 when all the work is subject to a ceiling price and the costs will 
be audited in accordance with Contract Cost Principles
 
  1031-2
 
 .
Use Option 2 when only a portion of the work is subject to a ceiling price and 
costs will be audited in accordance with Contract Cost Principles
 
  1031-2
 
 .

Use this clause in conjunction with
 
  C6000C
 
 for ceiling price contracts; or
 
  C6001C
 
 for contracts with a limitation of expenditure.  The audit provision is 
contained in the general conditions.
</section>
