<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>The general conditions are amended by deleting in its entirety
the section entitled "Copyright", and replacing it with the
following:</p>
<blockquote>"Without affecting any existing intellectual property
rights or relating to information or data supplied by Canada for
purposes of the Contract, copyright in anything conceived,
developed, or produced as part of the Work under the Contract will
belong to the Contractor."</blockquote>
