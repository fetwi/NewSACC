<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>This procurement is conditionally limited to Canadian goods.</p>
<p>Subject to the evaluation procedures contained in the bid solicitation, bidders acknowledge that only bids for items with a certification that the item(s) offered are Canadian goods, as defined in clause A3050T, may be considered.</p>
<p>Failure to provide this certification completed with the bid will result in the item(s) offered being treated as non-Canadian goods.</p>
<p class="description"><strong>Instruction to contracting officers:</strong> Choose one of the following options.</p>
<ul class="no-markers">
<li><blockquote>"Bidders must clearly identify beside each item listed in the bid solicitation document which items meet the definition of
Canadian good and complete the following certification."</blockquote></li>
<li><strong>OR</strong><br/>
<blockquote>"Bidders must clearly identify below which items meet the definition of Canadian good and complete the certification below."</blockquote></li>
</ul>
<p>The Bidder certifies that:</p>
<p><var title="checkbox">( )</var> the item(s) offered and identified as Canadian goods are Canadian goods as defined in paragraph 1 of clause A3050T.</p>
