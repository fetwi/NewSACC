<h3 class="clause-heading">Remarks â€“ Recommended Use of SACC Item</h3><section class="remarks group-sacc-remarks field-group-section">
<p>
  Use the following clause in contracts when a contractor, who has care, custody 
or control of government property, is required to use a rigger to lift heavy 
equipment.
 </p>
<p>
  Use this clause in conjunction with
  
   G2020C
  
  .
 </p>
<p>
  If a licensed motor vehicle is required as part of the rigging operation and 
transportation of equipment, use this clause in conjunction with
  
   G2020C
  
  .  
Contracting officers must consult with Risk Management Advisory Services,
  <abbr title="Public Works and Government Services Canada">
   PWGSC
  </abbr>
  , 
by e-mail to:
  
   NCRRMIAS.RCNSCGRA@tpsgc-pwgsc.gc.ca
  
  , to determine whether Automobile Liability Insurance is required.
 </p>
<p>
  Rigger's liability insurance provides liability coverage for loss or damage to 
government property in the contractor's care, custody or control.
 </p>
<p>
  The value and basis of valuation of government property must be filled in the 
clause.  The basis of valuation of this property should be established with the 
client and mentioned in the contractor's insurance policy.  Contracting officers 
must insert one of the options provided in the second blank of paragraph 1.
 </p>
<p>
  The values are to be evaluated on a case-by-case basis and the minimum liability 
amount must be determined in consultation with Risk Management Advisory 
Services,
  <abbr title="Public Works and Government Services Canada">
   PWGSC
  </abbr>
  , by email to:
  
   NCRRMIAS.RCNSCGRA@tpsgc-pwgsc.gc.ca
  
  .
 </p>
</section>

<ol type="1">
<li value="1">The Contractor must obtain Rigger's Liability
Insurance, in an amount usual for a contract of this nature, but
for not less than $<var>___________</var> per accident or
occurrence and in the annual aggregate. The Contractor's Riggers
Liability Insurance must provide coverage for loss or damage to all
Government Property under its care, custody or control, and must be
maintained in force throughout the duration of the Contract. The
Government Property must be insured on <span class="input-required"><var>___________</var>
basis. <kbd>(<em><strong>insert one of the following basis of
valuation</strong>: "Replacement Cost (new)"; "Actual Cash
Value (depreciated cost)" <strong>or</strong> "Agreed
Value (appraisal)"</em>)</kbd></span>.
<br/>The Contractor must notify Canada promptly about any losses or
damages to Government Property and monitor, investigate and
document losses of or damage to ensure that claims are properly
made and paid.</li>
<li value="2">The Rigger's Liability Insurance policy must include
the following:
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">Notice of Cancellation: The Insurer will endeavour to

of policy cancellation.</li>
<li value="2">Loss Payee: Canada as its interest may appear or as
it may direct, for loss or damage to Government property in the
Contractor's care, custody or control.</li>
<li value="3">Waiver of Subrogation Rights: Contractor's Insurer to
waive all rights of subrogation against Canada as represented by
<span class="input-required"><var>________</var> <kbd>(<em>insert client

Canada for any and all loss of or damage to the property however
caused.</li>
</ol>
</li>
</ol>
