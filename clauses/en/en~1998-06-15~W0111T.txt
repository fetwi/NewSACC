<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>The benefits that apply to this procurement are contained in:
Section 5.6, <cite>Little Salmon/Carmacks First Nation Final Agreement
(LSCFA)</cite>, clauses:</p>
<ul class="hierarchy">
<li><span class="number">13.12.1.1</span> Government shall provide
written notice to the Little Salmon/Carmacks First Nation of any
invitation for public tenders for contracts associated with the
management of a Designated Heritage Site directly related to the
history or culture of Little Salmon/Carmacks People within the
Traditional Territory of the Little Salmon/Carmacks First
Nations.</li>
<li><span class="number">13.12.1.2</span> Government shall include
the Little Salmon/Carmacks First Nation in any invitational tender
for contracts associated with the management of a Designated
Heritage Site directly related to the history or culture of Little
Salmon/Carmacks First Nation.</li>
<li><span class="number">13.12.1.3</span> The Little
Salmon/Carmacks First Nation shall have the first opportunity to
accept any contract offered by Government other than by public or
invitational tender associated with the management of a Designated
Heritage Site directly related to the history or culture of Little
Salmon/Carmacks People within the Traditional Territory of the
Little Salmon/Carmacks First Nation upon the same terms and
conditions as would be offered to others.</li>
<li><span class="number">13.12.1.7</span> Government shall include
in any contract opportunity associated with the management of a
Designated Heritage Site directly related to the history or culture
of Little Salmon/Carmacks People in the Traditional Territory of
the Little Salmon/Carmacks First Nations:
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">a criterion for Little Salmon/Carmacks People
employment; and</li>
<li value="2">a criterion for special knowledge or experience of
Little Salmon/Carmacks People related to the Designated Heritage
Site.</li>
</ol>
</li>
</ul>
