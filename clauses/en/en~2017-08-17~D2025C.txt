<h3 class="clause-heading">Remarks – Recommended Use of SACC Item</h3><section class="remarks group-sacc-remarks field-group-section">
<p>
  Use the following clause in all contracts where there is a potential use of wood packaging materials in shipping, such as but not limited to crates, boxes, packing cases, dunnage, pallets, cable drums and spools/reels, which must conform to the
  
   International Standards for Phytosanitary Measures No. 15: Regulation of Wood Packaging Material in International Trade (ISPM 15)
  
  .
 </p>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>All wood packaging materials used in shipping must conform to the International Standards for Phytosanitary Measures No. 15: Regulation of Wood Packaging Material in International Trade (ISPM 15).</p>
<p>Pertinent additional information on Canada's import and export programs is provided in the following Canadian Food Inspection
Agency policy directives:</p>
<p>D-98-08 - Entry Requirements for Wood Packaging Materials Produced in All Areas Other Than the Continental United States</p>
<p>D-13-01 – Canadian Heat Treated Wood Products Certification Program (HT Program)</p>
