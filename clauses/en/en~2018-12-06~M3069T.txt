<p>
  Use the following clause in competitive requests for standing offers when the 
requirement is a mix of goods and services, and the competition is conditionally 
limited to Canadian goods and Canadian services as defined in paragraph 5 of 
clause
  
   A3050T
  
  .
 </p>
<p>
  Use this clause in conjunction with clause
  
   A3050T
  
  and
  
   M3060C
  
  .
 </p>
<p>
  Contracting officers must consult the Canadian Content Policy (
  
   Section 3.130
  
  of the Supply Manual) to establish if the requirement is subject to the policy.
 </p>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>This procurement is conditionally limited to Canadian goods and
Canadian services.</p>
<p>Subject to the evaluation procedures contained in the request
for standing offer, offerors acknowledge that only offers with a
certification that the goods and services offered are Canadian
goods and Canadian services, as defined in clause A3050T, may be considered.</p>
<p>Failure to provide this certification completed with the offer
will result in the goods and services offered being treated as
non-Canadian goods and non-Canadian services.</p>
<p>The Offeror certifies that:</p>
<p><var title="checkbox">( )</var> a minimum of 80 percent of the
total price for the offer consist of Canadian goods and Canadian
services as defined in paragraph 5 of clause A3050T.</p>
<p>For more information on how to determine the Canadian content for a mix of goods, a mix of services or a mix of goods and services, consult Annex 3.6, Example 2, of the Supply Manual.</p>
