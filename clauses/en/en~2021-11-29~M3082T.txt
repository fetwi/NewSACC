<h3 class="clause-heading">Remarks – Recommended Use of SACC Item</h3><section class="remarks group-sacc-remarks field-group-section">
<p>
  Use this clause in PART 7 in the “A. Standing Offer” portion of the
  <abbr title="Standing Offer">
   SO
  </abbr>
  /
  <abbr title="Request for Standing Offers">
   RFSO
  </abbr>
  where any of the call-ups under the resulting Standing Offer may be subject to the COVID-19 Vaccination Policy for Supplier Personnel.
 </p>
<p>
  Use this clause in conjunction with:
 </p>
<ul>
<li>

    2035: General Conditions - Higher Complexity – Services
   
   ,
   
    2040: General Conditions - Research and Development
   
   , or
   
    4013: Compliance with on-site measures, standing orders, policies, and rules
   
   ;
  </li>
<li>

    4014: Suspension of the work
   
   (only if a ‘Suspension of the work’ clause is not already included in the General Conditions);
  </li>
<li>

    M3080T: COVID-19 vaccination requirement for Standing Offers
   
   ;
  </li>
<li>

    M3081T: COVID-19 mandatory vaccination requirement certification – Standing Offers
   
   ;
  </li>
<li>

    M3015T: Certification – Standing Offers
   
   (only if equivalent language is not already included in the solicitation; clause not needed if using
   <abbr title="Public Works and Government Services Canada">
    PWGSC
   </abbr>
   Standard Procurement Templates); and
  </li>
<li>

    M3015C: Compliance with Certification – Standing Offers
   
   (only if equivalent language is not already included in the contract; clause not needed if using
   <abbr title="Public Works and Government Services Canada">
    PWGSC
   </abbr>
   Standard Procurement Templates).
  </li>
</ul>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><p>Canada will have the right to declare an offer non-responsive, or to set-aside a Standing Offer, if the COVID-19 Vaccination Requirement Certification is or becomes untrue or if the Offeror fails to comply with such Certification during the period of any resulting Contract (call-up).</p> <p>Canada will also have the right to terminate any resulting Call-up for default if the COVID-19 Vaccination Requirement Certification is or becomes untrue or if the Contractor fails to comply with such Certification during the period of the Contract (call-up).</p></pre>
