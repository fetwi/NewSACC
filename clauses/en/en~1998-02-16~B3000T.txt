<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>1.     Products equivalent in form, fit, function and quality will be 
       considered only if the bidder:

       (a)    designates the trade reference of the substitutes and the 
              name of the manufacturer,

       (b)    strikes out the trade reference specified and the words "or 
              equal", and

       (c)    provides complete specifications and descriptive literature 
              for the substitutes.  The bidder warrants that proposed 
              equivalent substitutes will be fully interchangeable with the 
              trade reference specified.
