<h3 class="clause-heading">Remarks – Recommended Use of SACC Item</h3><section class="remarks group-sacc-remarks field-group-section">
<p>
  Use the following clause in contracts for the Department of National Defence 
when the goods are supplied by a Canadian-based contractor.
 </p>
<p>
  Use this clause in conjunction with
  
   D5510C
  
  ,
  
   D5604C
  
  and
  
   D5605C
  
  in the part 
"resulting contract clauses" of the bid solicitation.  At contract award, use 
this clause in conjunction with
  
   D5510C
  
  .
 </p>
<p>
  Use this clause in conjunction with
  
   D5620C
  
  .
 </p>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>Unless otherwise directed by the Department of National Defence
(DND) Quality Assurance Authority, the signature of the <abbr title="Department of National Defence">DND</abbr>  Quality
Assurance Representative on the release document is not
required.</p>
<p> Material must be released for shipment using either DND form CF 1280, Certificate of Release, Inspection and Acceptance,or a release document containing the same information. The Contractor must prepare the release document(s).</p>
<p> For return of repair and overhaul material to the Defence Supply Chain, use forms <abbr title="Department of National Defence">DND</abbr> 2227/DND 2228 in lieu of <abbr title="Department of National Defence">DND</abbr> form CF 1280.</p></div></pre>
</div>
