<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>1.     <strong>For Contractors Located in Canada</strong>

       Preservation and packaging for items _____ shall be in accordance 
       with the Canadian Forces packaging specification D-LM-008-001/SF-001, 
       and shall be marked to D-LM-008-002/SF-001. Level B "PKG DATA FORM 
       REQD", shall be in accordance with D-LM-008-011/SF-001.

2.     <strong>For Contractors Located in United States</strong>

       Preservation and packaging for items _____ shall be in accordance 
       with the current issue of U.S. Department of Defense Military 
       Standard MIL-STD-2073 and shall be marked to MIL-STD-129.

3.      <strong>Approval Authorities</strong>

       Packaging data forms previously approved by Canadian or United 
       States authorities shall be acceptable.

4.     <strong>Coded Packaging Data</strong>

       Approved coded packaging data is shown immediately below the 
       description of the item to which it applies. Where no data is shown, 
       the Contractor shall submit a packaging data form for approval.

5.     <strong>For Items Not Assigned a NATO Stock Number</strong>

       No packaging data form is required.
