applicable provisions as set out in the Basis of Payment.

Canada may exercise this option at any time by sending a written notice to 
   the Contractor at least _____ calendar days before the Contract expiry 
   date.  The option may only be exercised by the Contracting Authority, 
   and will be evidenced for administrative purposes only, through a 
   contract amendment.
