<pre>Use this clause to define Department of National Defence's military packaging 
requirements for militarized items which are covered by the following categories:

(a)	items not covered by a Canadian Forces commodity packaging specification 
(see D3016D) or commercial packaging (see D3018D);

(b)	repairable materiel for national stock;

(c) 	repair and overhaul of repairable materiel.
</pre>

<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>1.     <strong>For Contractors Located in Canada</strong>

       Preservation and packaging for items ______ shall be in accordance 
       with the Canadian Forces packaging specification D-LM-008-001/SF-001, 
       and shall be marked to D-LM-008-002/SF-001. Form Level B "PKG DATA 
       FORM REQD" shall be in accordance with D-LM-008-011/SF-001.

2.     <strong>For Contractors Located in United States</strong>

       Preservation and packaging for items _______ shall be in accordance 
       with the current issue of United States (U.S.) Department of Defense 
       Military Standard MIL-STD-2073 and shall be marked to MIL-STD-129.

3.     <strong>Approval Authorities</strong>

       Packaging data forms previously approved by Canadian or U.S. 
       authorities shall be acceptable.

4.     <strong>Coded Packaging Data</strong>

       Approved coded packaging data is shown immediately below the 
       description of the item to which it applies.  Where no data is shown, 
       the Contractor shall submit a packaging data form for approval.


</pre>
