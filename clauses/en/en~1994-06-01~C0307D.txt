<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>If requested by the Contracting Authority or audit agency designated by the 
Contracting Authority, the Contractor shall prepare and submit to the 
Contracting Authority or the audit agency as applicable, a cost submission, 
upon completion of the contract or annually for multi-year contracts 
spanning more than one contractor fiscal year.  The cost submission shall 
be a statement of the costs incurred by the Contractor, categorized in 
accordance with the Basis of Payment, for the work performed under the 
terms of the contract. 

The cost submission shall be signed and certified by the Contractor's 
senior financial officer and shall contain a breakdown of all applicable 
elements of cost, for example:

-      Direct material
-      Direct labour
-      Direct charges
-      Overhead
-      Subcontracts
-      General and administrative
-      Goods and services tax
-      Royalty
-      Freight
-      Profit

Supporting information for each element of cost shall be available and 
   shall be in sufficient detail that an in-depth audit can be carried out.
</pre>
