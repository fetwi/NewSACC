<h3 class="clause-heading">Remarks â€“ Recommended Use of SACC Item</h3><section class="remarks group-sacc-remarks field-group-section">
<p>
  Use the following clause in professional services contracts, for example: 
consulting, design, training, educational, management, architectural, 
engineering, research &amp; development.
 </p>
<p>
  Use this clause in conjunction with
  
   G2001C
  
  .
 </p>
<p>
  If the professional services are medical in nature or related to healthcare, use 
clause
  
   G2004C
  
  .
 </p>
<p>
  Contracting officers must consult
  <abbr title="Public Works and Government Services Canada">
   PWGSC
  </abbr>
  Risk Management Advisory Services, at:
  
   NCR.RMIAS-SCGRA@pwgsc.gc.ca
  
  if the suggested limit of $1,000,000 per accident or occurrence is not considered adequate as alternate limits may be established.
 </p>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">The Contractor must obtain Errors and Omissions Liability (a.k.a. Professional Liability) insurance, and maintain it in force throughout the duration of the Contract, in an amount usual for a contract of this nature but for not less than $1,000,000 per loss and in the annual aggregate, inclusive of defence costs.</li>
<li value="2">If the policy is written on a claims-made basis, coverage must be in place for a period of at least 12 months after the completion or termination of the Contract.</li>
<li value="3">The following endorsement must be included:
            <blockquote>Notice of Cancellation: The Contractor will provide the Contracting Authority thirty (30) days prior written notice of policy cancellation or any changes to the insurance policy.
            </blockquote>
</li>
</ol>
</div></pre>
