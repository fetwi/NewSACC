<h3 class="clause-heading">Remarks â€“ Recommended Use of SACC Item</h3><section class="remarks group-sacc-remarks field-group-section">
<p>
  Use the following clause in requests for supply arrangements for goods when 
ceiling prices are required.
 </p>
<p>
  Use clause 1 when the supplier must submit ceiling prices for all items; or 
clause 2 when the supplier must submit ceiling prices for one or multiple groups 
of items. Delete the clause not used.
 </p>
</section>

<h2>Clause 1</h2>
Suppliers must submit ceiling prices for all items listed in
Annex(es) "<var>_____</var> ".</li>

<h2>Clause 2</h2>
Suppliers must submit ceiling prices for one or multiple groups
of items. However, suppliers must submit ceiling prices for all

<li value="4">Group D: All items listed in Annex "<var>____</var>".</li>
</ol>
</li>
