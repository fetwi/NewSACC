<h3 class="clause-heading">Remarks â€“ Recommended Use of SACC Item</h3><section class="remarks group-sacc-remarks field-group-section">
<p>
  Use the following clause in requests for standing offers for procurements that 
have been set aside under the federal government Procurement Strategy for 
Aboriginal Business.
 </p>
<p>
  Use this clause in conjunction with
  
   M3030T
  
  ,
  
   A3000C
  
  , and if applicable,
  
   A3002T
  
  .
 </p>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">This procurement is set aside under the federal
government Procurement Strategy for Aboriginal Business. For more information on Aboriginal business requirements of the Set-aside Program for Aboriginal Business, see Annex 9.4 of the <cite>Supply Manual</cite>.</li>
<li value="2">The Offeror:
<ol type="i">
<li value="1">certifies that it meets, and will continue to meet
throughout the duration of the Offer, the requirements described in
the above-mentioned annex.</li>
<li value="2">agrees that any subcontractor it engages under the Offer
must satisfy the requirements described in the above-mentioned
annex.</li>
<li value="3">agrees to provide to Canada, immediately upon request,
evidence supporting any subcontractor's compliance with the
requirements described in the above-mentioned annex.</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li value="3">The Offeror must check the applicable box below:
<ol type="i">
<li value="1"><var title="checkbox">( )</var> The Offeror is an
Aboriginal business that is a sole proprietorship, band, limited
company, co-operative, partnership or not-for-profit
organization.<br/>
<strong>OR</strong></li>
<li value="2"><var title="checkbox">( )</var> The Offeror is either a
joint venture consisting of two or more Aboriginal businesses or a
joint venture between an Aboriginal business and a non-Aboriginal
business.</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li value="4">The Offeror must check the applicable box below:
<ol type="i">
<li value="1"><var title="checkbox">( )</var> The Aboriginal
business has fewer than six full-time employees.<br/>
<strong>OR</strong></li>
<li value="2"><var title="checkbox">( )</var> The Aboriginal business has
six or more full-time employees.</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li value="5">The Offeror must, upon request by Canada, provide all
information and evidence supporting this certification. The Offeror
must ensure that this evidence will be available for audit during
normal business hours by a representative of Canada, who may make
copies and take extracts from the evidence. The Offeror must
provide all reasonably required facilities for any audits.</li>
<li value="6">By submitting an offer, the Offeror certifies that
the information submitted by the Offeror in response to the above
requirements is accurate and complete.</li>
</ol>
</div></pre>
