<p>
  Use the following clause in standing offers if it is foreseen that Canada may 
require an extension of the period of the standing offer.
 </p>
<p>
  Contracting officers must insert the applicable period, e.g., one year; one 
month; 90 days.
 </p>

<p>If the Standing Offer is authorized for use beyond the initial
period, the Offeror offers to extend its offer for an additional
<var>_____</var> period, from <var>_____</var> to <var>_____</var>

the Standing Offer, or at the rates or prices calculated in
accordance with the formula specified in the Standing Offer.</p>
<p>The Offeror will be advised of the decision to authorize the use
of the Standing Offer for an extended period by the Standing Offer
Authority <var>_______</var> days before the expiry date of the
Standing Offer. A revision to the Standing Offer will be issued by
the Standing Offer Authority.</p>
