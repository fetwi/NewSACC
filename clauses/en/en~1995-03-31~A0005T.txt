       into consideration in the evaluation of each bid:

       (a)    Technical compliance M/D
       (b)    Delivery requirement M/D
       (c)    Inspection requirement M/D
       (d)    Packaging requirement M/D
       (e)    Descriptive literature (where applicable)MD
       (f)    Transportation costs (where applicable) M/D
       (g)    Compliance with proposed pricing methods M/D
       (h)    Price M/D
       (i)    Acceptance of other proposed terms and conditions as

       (j)    Completion of the solicitation M/D

2.     In addition to the above, bidders must comply with any other 
       requirement stipulated in the bid solicitation.
