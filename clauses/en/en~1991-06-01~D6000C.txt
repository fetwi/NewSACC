<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>Immediately it is known there will be available a shipment weighing 20,000 
lbs. or over, the Contractor is to notify the _____ Directorate, Department 
of Supply and Services, Hull, Quebec, Canada K1A 0S5 who will issue routing 
instructions.  Shipment is not to be made until such instructions are 
received.  Report of availability must state the following:

       1.  contract number,
       2.  gross weight and freight classification,
       3.  name of railway serving the Contractor's plant site,
       4.  the Contractor's recommended method of shipment and
           cost,
       5.  name of consignee and destination,
       6.  location of stores and date of availability.

Unless otherwise directed, all shipments shall be shipped collect in 
   accordance with the Shipping provisions of this Contract.
</pre>
