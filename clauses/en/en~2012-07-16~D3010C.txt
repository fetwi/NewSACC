<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">The Contractor must mark dangerous goods/hazardous
products material which is classed as dangerous / hazardous as
follows:
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">shipping container - in accordance with the
<cite>Transportation of Dangerous Goods Act</cite>, 1992, <abbr title="chapter">c.</abbr> 34;
and</li>
<li value="2">immediate product container - in accordance with the
<cite>Hazardous Products Act</cite>, <abbr title="Revised Statutes">R.S.</abbr>, 1985, <abbr title="chapter">c.</abbr> H-3.</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li value="2">The Contractor must provide bilingual Material Safety
Data Sheets, indicating the <abbr title="North Atlantic Treaty Organization">NATO</abbr> Stock Number as follows:
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">two (2) hard copies:
<ol type="i">
<li value="1">one (1) copy to be enclosed with the shipment,
and</li>
<li value="2"><p>one (1) copy to be mailed to:</p>
<p>National Defence Headquarters<br/>
MGen George <abbr title="Reference clauses">R</abbr>. Pearkes Building<br/>
101 Colonel By Drive<br/>
Ottawa, Ontario K1A 0K2<br/>
Attention: <abbr title="Director Supply Chain Operations">DSCO</abbr> 5-4-2</p></li>
</ol>
</li>
<li value="2">one (1) copy sent by email to the following address: MSDS-FS@FORCES.GC.CA in word processing format (i.e. <abbr title="Military Standard">MS</abbr> Word or WordPerfect).</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li value="3">The Contractor will be responsible for any damages
caused by improper packaging, labelling or carriage of
goods/products.</li>
<li value="4">The Contractor must ensure they adhere to all levels
of regulations regarding dangerous goods/hazardous products as set
forth by federal, provincial and municipal laws and by-laws.</li>
<li value="5">The Contractor must contact the consignee (i.e.
Supply Depot Traffic Section) at least 48 hours before shipping
dangerous goods/hazardous products in order to schedule a receiving
time.</li>
</ol>
