<p>
  Use the following clause in bid solicitations for goods when price(s) must not 
contain any element representing refundable duties and/or taxes paid on the 
import of materials, parts and components incorporated in the goods that are 
subsequently exported from Canada.
 </p>
<p>
  Use this clause in conjunction with
  
   C2001C
  
  .
 </p>
<p>
  For more information, consult
  
   Annex 4.3
  
  (6) of the
  <cite>

    Supply Manual
   
</cite>
  .
 </p>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>The goods to be purchased under the Contract are for export. The
Bidder certifies that the price(s) quoted in its bid do not contain
any element representing refundable duties and/or taxes paid on the
import of materials, parts and components incorporated in the
goods.</p>
</div></pre>
