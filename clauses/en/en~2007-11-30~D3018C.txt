<h3 class="clause-heading">Remarks â€“ Recommended Use of SACC Item</h3><section class="remarks group-sacc-remarks field-group-section">
 Use the following clause in contracts to define Department of National Defence's 
packaging requirements for the procurement of items which are covered by the 
following categories:

(a)	Commercial Off-the-Shelf (COTS);
(b)	direct to customer, for immediate use (including modifications);
(c)
 <abbr title="Co-Operative Logistics">
  COLOG
 </abbr>
 (co-operative logistics); or
(d)	items not covered by another Canadian Forces commodity packaging 
specification (see D3016C) or military packaging (see D3013C).
</section>
