<p>
  Use the following clause in contracts with task authorizations when periodic usage reports are required from the contractor.
 </p>
<p>
  The reporting periods and the number of calendar days by which each report must be submitted after the reporting period must be indicated in the clause.
 </p>
<p>
  The contracting officer must ensure that the contractor fulfills all the reporting requirements detailed in 
the contract.
 </p>
<p>
  For more information, consult
  
   3.35.1.20
  
  of the
  <cite>

    Supply Manual
   
</cite>
  .
 </p>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>The Contractor must compile and maintain records on its provision of services to the federal government under authorized Task Authorizations issued under the Contract.</p>
<p>The Contractor must provide this data in accordance with the reporting requirements detailed below or in Annex "<var>___</var>". If some data is not available, the reason must be indicated. If services are not provided during a given period, the Contractor must still provide a "nil" report.</p>
<p>The data must be submitted on a <span class="input-required"><var>___________</var> <kbd>(<em>insert "quarterly
basis" or specify an alternate reporting period</em>)</kbd></span> to the Contracting Authority.</p>
<p class="description">(<em>If an alternate reporting period is required, delete the quarterly periods provided below and define the alternate reporting period</em>.)</p>
<p>The quarterly periods are defined as follows:</p>
<p>1st quarter: April 1 to June 30;</p>
<p>2nd quarter: July 1 to September 30;</p>
<p>3rd quarter: October 1 to December 31; and</p>
<p>4th quarter: January 1 to March 31.</p>
<p>The data must be submitted to the Contracting Authority no later
than <span class="input-required"><var>____</var> <kbd>(<em>insert number of days</em>)</kbd></span> calendar days after the end of the reporting period.</p>
<h2>Reporting Requirement- Details</h2>
<p>A detailed and current record of all authorized tasks must be kept for each contract with a task authorization process. This record must contain <span class="input-required"><kbd>(<em>contracting authority to edit the text as applicable</em>)</kbd></span>:</p>
<h3>For each authorized task:</h3>
<ol type="i">
<li value="1">the authorized task number or task revision number(s);</li>
<li value="2">a title or a brief description of each authorized task;</li>
<li value="3">the total estimated cost specified in the authorized Task Authorization (TA) of each task, exclusive of Applicable Taxes;</li>
<li value="4">the total amount, exclusive of Applicable Taxes, expended to date against each authorized task;</li>
<li value="5">the start and completion date for each authorized task; and</li>
<li value="6">the active status of each authorized task, as applicable.</li></ol>
<h3>For all authorized tasks:</h3>
<ol type="i">
<li value="1">the amount (exclusive of Applicable Taxes) specified in the contract (as last amended, as applicable) as Canada's total liability to the contractor for all authorized <abbr title="Task Authorization">TA</abbr>s; and</li>
<li value="2">the total amount, exclusive of Applicable Taxes, expended to date against all authorized <abbr title="Task Authorization">TA</abbr>s.</li>
</ol>
