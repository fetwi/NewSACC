<pre>Use the following clause in competitive requests for standing offers for single 
item (line item) when competition is conditionally limited to Canadian goods as 
defined in A3050T.

Use this clause in conjunction with A3050T and M3060C.

Contracting officers must review the Canadian Content Policy (Supply Manual, 
Chapter 5 and Annex 5.1) to establish if the requirement is subject to the 
policy.</pre>

<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>This procurement is conditionally limited to Canadian goods.

Subject to the evaluation procedures contained in the request for standing 
offer, offerors acknowledge that only offers with a certification that the 
good(s) offered are Canadian goods, as defined in clause A3050T, may be 
considered.

Failure to provide this certification completed with the offer will result 
in the good(s) offered being treated as non-Canadian goods.

The Offeror certifies that:

       (     )       the good(s) offered are Canadian goods as defined in 
paragraph 1 of clause A3050T.
