              Total Estimated Price: $______

2.     Subject to the exercise of the Contract option to extend the 
       Contract period, the Contractor shall be paid the following firm per

       <strong>CATEGORY             FIRM PER DIEM RATE</strong>

       _______              $______

                            UNFUNDED

3.     Goods and Services Tax or Harmonized Sales Tax, estimated at $____, 
       is extra to the "Total Estimated Price" shown herein and shall be 
       paid in accordance with the provisions of clause "_____."

4.     All deliverables are FOB Destination, and Canadian Customs Duty 
       included, where applicable.

5.     <strong>DEFINITION OF A DAY/PRORATION</strong>

       A day is defined as 7.5 hours exclusive of meal breaks.  Payment 
       shall be for days actually worked with no provision for annual leave, 
       statutory holidays and sick leave. Time worked which is more or less 
       than a day shall be prorated to reflect actual time worked in 
       accordance with the following formula:

       <span class="wsStyleUnderline">Hours worked</span>
       7.5    x      per diem rate

6.     <strong>NOTE TO PAYING OFFICE</strong>

       The amount of Canadian dollars has been calculated on the basis of 
       exchange of C$_____ being equal to US$1. Payment shall be made in 
       American funds and the amount required in Canadian funds shall be 
       adjusted to correspond to the rate of exchange in effect when the 
       necessary foreign funds are purchased.

7.     <strong>TRAVEL EXPENSES</strong>

       The Contractor shall be paid for pre-authorized reasonable and 
       proper travel expenses, supported by appropriate receipts, incurred 
       by personnel directly engaged in the performance of the Work, 
       calculated in accordance with the current Treasury Board Travel 
       Directive <strong>(http://www.tbs-sct.gc.ca/Pubs_pol/hrpubs/TBM_113/STA1-1E.html
       #P218_1086</strong><strong></strong>
<strong>1)</strong>, without any allowance thereon for overhead or profit. Charges for air 
travel shall not exceed that for economy class. All payments are subject to 
government audit.

       Estimated Price: $______.
