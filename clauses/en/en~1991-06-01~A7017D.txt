<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>The Contractor shall provide the services of specified employees to perform 
the work required unless it is impossible to do so for reasons which are 
beyond its control, and it shall be responsible for providing replacement(s) 
who shall be of similar ability and attainment.  In such cases the 
Contractor shall notify, in writing, the Worksite Authority, and provide:

       a) the reason for the removal of these employee(s) from the work;

       b) the names of the proposed replacement(s).  

Such notice shall be given at least five (5) days in advance of the date 
   upon which the replacement is to commence work, except for reasons 
   beyond the control of the Contractor.
