<p>
  Use the following clause in contracts when a contractor who will have access to 
confidential and sensitive information is required to obtain from its 
employee(s) or subcontractor(s) a non-disclosure agreement before they are given 
access to the information. This clause is used to supplement the confidentiality 
section of the general conditions.
 </p>
</section>
