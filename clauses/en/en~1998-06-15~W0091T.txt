<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>The benefits that apply to this procurement are contained in:
Section 5.4, <cite>Vuntut Gwich'in First Nation Final Agreement (VGFA)</cite>,
Chapter 10, Schedule A, clauses:</p>
<ul class="hierarchy">
<li><span class="number">9.6</span> The Canadian Parks Service
shall provide timely written notice to the Vuntut Gwitchin First
Nation of any invitation by the Canadian Parks Service respecting
contracts for the provision of goods and services in the Vuntut
Gwitchin First Nation Traditional Territory for the development,
operation and management of the Park.</li>
<li><span class="number">9.7</span> The Canadian Parks Service
shall provide the Vuntut Gwitchin with a right of first refusal to
accept any contract offered by the Canadian Parks Service for the
construction or maintenance of trails within the Vuntut Gwitchin
First Nation Traditional Territory in the following manner:
<ul class="hierarchy">
<li><span class="number">9.7.1</span> the Canadian Parks Service
shall provide notice to the Vuntut Gwitchin First Nation specifying
the terms and conditions of the contract;</li>
<li><span class="number">9.7.2</span> the Vuntut Gwitchin First
Nation shall have 30 days from the date the notice in 9.7.1 is
received to advise the Park superintendent in writing whether it is
exercising its right of first refusal under 9.7;</li>
<li><span class="number">9.7.3</span> if the Vuntut Gwitchin First
Nation does not exercise its right of first refusal under 9.7, the
Canadian Parks Service may offer the contract publicly on the same
terms and conditions specified in the notice pursuant to 9.7.1;
and</li>
<li><span class="number">9.7.4</span> if the contract offered
publicly is not accepted, the Canadian Parks Service may re-offer
the contract on new terms and conditions in accordance with the
procedure set out in 9.7.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
