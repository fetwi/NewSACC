<h3 class="clause-heading">Remarks – Recommended Use of SACC Item</h3><section class="remarks group-sacc-remarks field-group-section">
<pre>Use the following clause in contracts when the requirement may be subject to 
design change/deviation and the procedures are not included in the statement of 
work.

Instructions to contracting officers:

1.	complete the clause;

2.	ensure that the name of the technical or project authority is provided in the 
contract.  Use clause A1022C or A1030C, as applicable;

3.	use one of the options provided in the clause:
	- option 1 when form PWGSC-TPSGC 9038 must be used;
	- option 2 when DND form 672 must be used;

4.	refer to Supply Manual, procedures 6E.627 to 6E.631 and 11.045 to 11.060.</pre>
</section>
