<h3 class="clause-heading">Remarks – Recommended Use of SACC Item</h3><section class="remarks group-sacc-remarks field-group-section">
<p>
  Include the following in contracts that are subject to the Nunavut Directive.
 </p>
<p>
  For each new procurement that is subject to the Directive, an up-to-date supplementary template (Inuit Benefits Plan (IBP),
  <abbr title="Inuit Benefits Plan">
   IBP
  </abbr>
  Progress Report and
  <abbr title="Inuit Benefits Plan">
   IBP
  </abbr>
  Evaluation) and
  <abbr title="Inuit Benefits Plan">
   IBP
  </abbr>
  Holdback clause must be obtained from Strategic Policy Sector’s Indigenous Procurement Policy Division (IPPD).
 </p>
<p>
  For important information on the implementation of Inuit and Nunavut benefits in procurements subject to the
  
   Nunavut Directive
  
  , please see the
  
   Guide on Government Contracts in the Nunavut Settlement Area
  
  .
 </p>
<p>
  For additional advice and guidance on the Nunavut Directive, the use of these clauses, and for the most up-to-date copy of the supplementary templates and
  <abbr title="Inuit Benefits Plan">
   IBP
  </abbr>
  Holdback clause, please contact Strategic Policy Sector’s Indigenous Procurement Policy Division (IPPD) at:
  
   tpsgc.pacontratsnunavut-apnunavutcontracts.pwgsc@tpsgc-pwgsc.gc.ca
  
  .
 </p>
<p>
<strong>
   Note:
  </strong>
  These solicitation clauses cannot be used in conjunction with
  <abbr title="Standard Acquisition Clauses and Conditions">
   SACC
  </abbr>
  Manual items
  
   A3000C
  
  ,
  
   A3000T
  
  ,
  
   A3001T
  
  ,
  
   A3002T
  
  ,
  
   M3030T
  
  ,
  
   M9030T
  
  ,
  
   S3035T
  
  , or
  
   S3036T
  
  .
 </p>
</section>

<li>The Contractor must submit a claim for payment using form PWGSC-TPSGC 1111, Claim for Progress Payment.
        <p>Each claim must show:</p>
<ol type="a">
<li>all information required on form PWGSC-TPSGC 1111;</li>
<li>all applicable information detailed under the section entitled "Invoice Submission" of the general conditions;</li>
</ol>
<p><kbd><cite>Instruction to contracting officers: Insert specific information that must be shown on the claim and is not covered under (a) and (b) above to reflect the requirement. The following are examples only:</cite></kbd></p>
<ol start="3" type="a">
<li>a list of all expenses;</li>
<li>expenditures plus pro-rated profit or fee;</li>
<li>the description and value of the milestone claimed as detailed in the Contract.</li>
</ol>
<p><kbd><cite>Instruction to contracting officers: Use the following paragraph when claims must be accompanied by supporting documents. The documents listed are examples only and must be revised to reflect the requirement.</cite></kbd></p>

<ol type="a">
<li>a copy of time sheets to support the time claimed;
            </li><li>a copy of the invoices, receipts, vouchers for all direct expenses, travel and living expenses;
            </li><li>a copy of the monthly progress report;</li>

</li>
