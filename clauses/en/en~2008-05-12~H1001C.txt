<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>Canada will pay the Contractor upon completion and delivery of
units in accordance with the payment provisions of the Contract
if:</p>
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">an accurate and complete invoice and any other
documents required by the Contract have been submitted in
accordance with the invoicing instructions provided in the
Contract;</li>
<li value="2">all such documents have been verified by Canada;</li>
<li value="3">the Work delivered has been accepted by Canada.</li>
</ol>
