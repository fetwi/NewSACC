<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">A deposit will be paid by the client department or
agency on reels and lags as follows:
<br/>Reels $<var>_____</var> Types/Size <var>_____</var>
<br/>Lags $<var>_____</var> Types/Size <var>_____</var></li>
<li value="2">The deposit is refundable in full on reels and lags
that are returned to the factory transportation costs prepaid and
in good condition, within twelve (12) months from the date of
shipment.</li>
<li value="3">For each month over twelve (12) months, up to and
including the 22nd month, a deduction of 5 percent by month will be
made from the deposit, provided the reel or lag is returned to the
factory transportation costs prepaid and in good condition.</li>
<li value="4">After the 22nd month, 50 percent of the deposit is
refundable for a reel or lag that is returned to the factory
transportation costs prepaid and in good condition.</li>
</ol>
