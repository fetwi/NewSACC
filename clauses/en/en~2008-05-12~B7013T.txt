<h3 class="clause-heading">Remarks â€“ Recommended Use of SACC Item</h3><section class="remarks group-sacc-remarks field-group-section">
 Use the following clause in bid solicitations for fabric requirements when 
pre-production and/or production sample(s) are required.

Use Option 1 when pre-production sample(s) are required.
Use Option 2 when production sample(s) are required.
Use Option 3 when both pre-production and production samples are required.

Use this clause in conjunction with
 
  B7015C
 
 .
</section>
