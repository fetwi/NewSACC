<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>Any person assigned to the performance of any part of the Work
that is performed on government premises must wear in a conspicuous
place the identification badge issued to that person by Canada.</p>
<p>When a person is required to wear a safety helmet, the
Contractor, if requested to do so by the Contracting Authority,
must paint the number appearing on the badge on the front of the
safety helmet.</p>
