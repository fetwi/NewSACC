<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>The Contract is a defence contract within the meaning of the
<cite>Defence Production Act</cite>, <abbr title="Revised Statutes of Canada">R.S.C.</abbr> 1985, <abbr title="chapter">c.</abbr> D-1, and must
be governed accordingly.</p>
<p>Title to the Work or to any materials, parts, work-in-process or
finished work must belong to Canada free and clear of all claims,
liens, attachments, charges or encumbrances. Canada is entitled, at
any time, to remove, sell or dispose of the Work or any part of the
Work in accordance with section 20 of the <cite>Defence Production Act</cite>.</p>
