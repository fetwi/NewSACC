<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>Fish must be processed and packed in an establishment approved by the Department of Fisheries and Oceans in accordance with the <cite>Fish Inspection Act</cite>, <abbr title="Revised Statutes">R.S.</abbr>, 1985, <abbr title="chapter">c.</abbr> F-12, and Regulations, and must be identified as follows:</p>
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">Fresh fish must be identified by the words "Processed under Government Supervision" or "Canada Inspected" within a line drawing of a maple leaf marked on wrappers, inserts containers or master containers.</li>
<li value="2">Frozen fish must be identified by the words "Canada Inspected" within a line drawing of a maple leaf marked on the wrappers, or containers, or where practicable on the whole fish.</li>
</ol>
