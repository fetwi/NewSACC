<pre><div class="sacc-item">
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">Delivery trucks must be equipped with an unloading
device which will permit unloading at sites with no hydraulic,
stationary or other type of unloading facility.</li>
<li value="2">When making deliveries, sufficient personnel must be
provided to permit unloading of any type of vehicle without the
assistance of federal government personnel.</li>
<li value="3">At some sites, the delivery truck must be unloaded
while parked at the curb. When material is placed on the sidewalk,
it must be placed in proximity to the designated entrance so as to
be readily accessible to transport by mechanical handling equipment
utilized by site personnel.</li>
</ol>
