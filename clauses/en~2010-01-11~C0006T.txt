<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>The Bidder certifies that the price proposed</p>
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">is not in excess of the lowest price charged anyone
else, including the Bidder's most favoured customer, for the like
quality and quantity, time, place and method of delivery of the
goods;</li>
<li value="2">does not include an element of profit on the sale in
excess of that normally obtained by the Bidder on the sale of goods
of like quality and quantity, time, place and method of delivery of
the goods; and</li>
<li value="3">does not include any provisions for discounts to
selling agents.</li>
</ol>
