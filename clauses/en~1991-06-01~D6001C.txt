<pre>Use the following clause in all FOB plant (origin) contracts valued at $ 25,000 
or more, where destinations are not known at the time of award of the contract.  
When shipping detail is received by a contracting officer, the Traffic 
Management Group, Consumer Products and Traffic Management Branch will be 
requested to provide shipping instructions, which will be relayed by the 
contracting officer to the contractor.  Include clause C5201C in the contract. 
Buyer will obtain fill-in data concerning the weight from the Traffic Management 
Group.
</pre>

<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>Notwithstanding the Shipping provisions of this Contract, as soon as it is 
known that a shipment weighing _____lbs.  (_____kg) or over will be 
available, and provided the consignee is known, the Contractor shall 
request shipping instructions from the _____ Directorate, Department of 
Supply and Services, Hull, Quebec, Canada K1A 0S5, giving the following 
information:

       1.  shipping weight and cubic measurement per unit,
       2.  freight classification,
       3.  name of shipping point,
       4.  name of rail carrier if plant is served by siding,
       5.  recommended method of shipment and cost.

Shipments are not to be made until instructions have been received.  
</pre>
