<pre>Use the following clause in contracts when the work is subject to a ceiling 
price.

Use Option 1 for a ceiling price contract when all the work is subject to a 
ceiling price.
Use Option 2 for a ceiling price contract when costs will be audited in 
accordance with Contract Cost Principles 1031-2.

Alternatively, use clause C1201C when only a portion of the work is subject to a 
ceiling price.

Use this clause in conjunction with C6000C.
</pre>
