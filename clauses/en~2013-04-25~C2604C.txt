<p>
  Use the following clause in contracts when the contractor is not a resident of Canada and is responsible for customs clearance of any tools, equipment or spare parts 
imported into Canada by its employees or a subcontractor and its employees for 
use in performing the work.
 </p>

  For more information, consult
  
   Annex 4.3
  
  (8) of the
  <cite>

</cite>

 </p>

<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>The Contractor is responsible for customs clearance of any
tools, equipment or spare parts imported into Canada by its
employees or a subcontractor and its employees for use in
performing the Work under the Contract. The Contractor is
responsible for any customs duties, excise taxes and Applicable Taxes, assessed by
the customs officials and payable to the Canada Border Services
Agency.</p>
</div></pre>
