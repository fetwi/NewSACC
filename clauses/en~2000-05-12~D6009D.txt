<h3 class="clause-heading">Remarks – Recommended Use of SACC Item</h3><section class="remarks group-sacc-remarks field-group-section">
<pre>Use this clause in contracts where destination and delivery schedules are not 
known at the time of execution:
</pre>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>1.     The Contractor shall ship prepaid free on board (FOB) destination.  
       Unless otherwise directed, delivery shall be made by the most 
       economical means.  Shipping charges shall be shown as a separate 
       item on the Contractor's invoice.

2.     The Contractor shall make deliveries to Canadian Forces Supply 
       Depots by appointment only.  The Contractor or its carrier shall 
       arrange delivery appointments by contacting the Depot Traffic 
       Section at the appropriate location shown below.  The consignee may 
       refuse shipments when prior arrangements have not been made.

       (a)    7 CF Supply Depot Lancaster Park
              Edmonton, Alta
              (780) 973-4011, ext. 4524

       (b)    25 CF Supply Depot Montreal
              Montreal, Qué.
              (514) 252-2777, ext. 2363


</pre>
