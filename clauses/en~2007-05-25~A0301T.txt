<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>The Part Number and <abbr title="North Atlantic Treaty Organization">NATO</abbr> Supply Code for Manufacturers (NSCM(s),
or the Commercial And Government Entity (CAGE) code indicated in
the bid solicitation are the only ones known to the Department of
National Defence that meet the form, fit and function requirements
of the Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM) approved type design
of the aircraft in which they will be installed.</p>
<p>If the Bidder proposes to supply any part with an alternative
Part Number or <abbr title="NATO Supply Code for Manufacturers">NSCM</abbr>/<abbr title="Commercial and Government Entity">CAGE</abbr> code, the Bidder must provide, either with
its bid or within three (3) working days following receipt of a
request from the Contracting Authority, all the technical
information (e.g. drawings, specifications, engineering reports,
and/or test reports) necessary to clearly demonstrate that the part
proposed has the form, fit and function characteristics equivalent
to the Part Number(s) and <abbr title="NATO Supply Code for Manufacturers">NSCM</abbr>/<abbr title="Commercial and Government Entity">CAGE</abbr> code(s) specified in the bid
solicitation.</p>
<p>Failure to provide the required technical information will
result in the bid being declared non-responsive with respect to any
part for which such information was requested.</p>
<p>If a part is not manufactured by the <abbr title="Original Equipment Manufacturer">OEM</abbr> of the aircraft, then
it must be manufactured by an authorized supplier to the <abbr title="Original Equipment Manufacturer">OEM</abbr> or by
the original manufacturer of the part chosen for use by the <abbr title="Original Equipment Manufacturer">OEM</abbr> of
the aircraft (or the successor of or licensed by that original
manufacturer). Canada reserves the right to verify with the <abbr title="Original Equipment Manufacturer">OEM</abbr> of
the aircraft that the manufacturer of a part proposed is in fact
authorized by the <abbr title="Original Equipment Manufacturer">OEM</abbr> to produce that part or supplies that part to
the <abbr title="Original Equipment Manufacturer">OEM</abbr>.</p>
<p>If the Bidder proposes to supply any part with an alternative
Part Number or <abbr title="NATO Supply Code for Manufacturers">NSCM</abbr>/<abbr title="Commercial and Government Entity">CAGE</abbr> code, the Bidder must provide the
following substitution notice fully completed.</p>
