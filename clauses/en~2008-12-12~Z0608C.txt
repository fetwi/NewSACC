(U.S.) requisition/contract specifies government inspection at plant (identified 
as FAR 52.246, MIL-I-45208, MIL-Q-9859, AQAP 110, AQAP 120, AQAP 130, AQAP 131,

standard as stated in the foreign (U.S.) requisition/contract number.</pre>
