<h3 class="clause-heading">Remarks – Recommended Use of SACC Item</h3><section class="remarks group-sacc-remarks field-group-section">
<pre>Use the following clause for services - firm price.
</pre>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>1.     The Contractor shall be paid the firm lot price of $_____ for work 
       and services performed pursuant to this Contract.

<strong>       TRAVEL AND LIVING EXPENSES</strong>

2.     The Contractor will be paid for pre-authorized reasonable and proper 
       travel and living expenses supported by appropriate receipts, 
       incurred by personnel directly engaged in the performance of the 
       Work, calculated in accordance with the then-current Treasury Board 
       Guidelines on Travel and Living Expenses, without any allowance 
       thereon for overhead or profit.  All payments are subject to 
       government audit.

3.     Goods and Services Tax, estimated at $_____, is extra to the "Total 
       Estimated Price" shown herein and shall be paid in accordance with 
       the provisions of Clause "_____."

4.     Subject to the exercise of the Contract option for additional 
       services in accordance with Clause "_____" herein, the Contractor 
       shall be paid the firm lot price of  $____ to perform all work and 
       services required to be performed in relation to the Contract 
       extension.

<strong>       UNFUNDED</strong>

5.     All deliverables are FOB Destination, and Canadian Customs Duty 
       included, where applicable.

</pre>
