<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>Export packaging and labelling are to be to the highest
manufacturer's standard for the mode of transportation utilized, to
ensure safe arrival at final destination.</p>
<p>Only identical items from one Contract are to be contained in
any one interior package, except that sets of tools, parts, etc.,
are to be packed as individual sets, whenever possible. The
contents of each interior package are to be clearly indicated by
label or tag showing the item number, quantity and Contract
number.</p>
<p>Only items from one Contract shall be packaged in one exterior
container.</p>
<p>One copy of the detailed packing list shall be included with
each package.</p>
<p>All containers are to be clearly marked with the following
information:</p>
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">consignee and address;</li>
<li value="2">project number;</li>
<li value="3">name of project;</li>
<li value="4">consignee identification number, if applicable;</li>
<li value="5">Contract number;</li>
<li value="6">weight (metric), volume (metric);</li>
<li value="7">number of pieces;</li>
<li value="8">any special markings such as inclusion of <abbr title="Canadian International Development Agency">CIDA</abbr>/Canada
decals.</li>
</ol>
<p>The supplier is to apply to <abbr title="Canadian International Development Agency">CIDA</abbr> Procurement Division,
Transportation Section, for decals which can be affixed to
containers prior to shipment.</p>
<p>These packaging requirements must be strictly adhered to for
ease of identification in completing export documentation.</p>
