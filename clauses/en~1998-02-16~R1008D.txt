<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><strong>Construction Documents</strong>

1.     The <em>Consultant</em> shall, after acceptance of the design development 
       documents, prepare and

       (a)    submit for review to the <em>Departmental Representative</em> 
              construction documents detailing the requirements for the 
              construction of the Project at each stage of production as 
              specified in clause ____, Agreements Particulars.

       (b)    submit an updated <em>Cost Plan</em> including a <em>Construction Cost 
              Estimate</em> and <em>Project Schedule</em> at each specified stage of 
              production;

       (c)    provide copies of all construction documents submitted, in 
              the type and number specified in clause _____.

<strong>Pre-Tender </strong><strong><em>Construction Cost Estimate</em></strong><strong> and </strong><strong><em>Project Schedule</em></strong>

The <em>Consultant</em> shall prepare for tender call purposes and submit to the <em>
Departmental Representative</em> for acceptance a final <em>Construction Cost 
Estimate</em> based on the approved construction documents, together with a 
breakdown thereof, and an updated <em>Project Schedule</em>.

</pre>
