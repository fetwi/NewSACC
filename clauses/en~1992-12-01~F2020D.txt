<pre>Use the following clause when:

-	SECURITY CLEARANCE and a Communications-Electronic Security (COMSEC) briefing 
are required (Blocks #7 and 12);

-	Safeguarding of COMSEC documents, of assets or production at the contractor's 
site(s) is required (Blocks #13, 14 and 15 or 16);

- 	Level of CLASSIFIED information or assets is identified in Block #13;

- 	CLASSIFIED information MAY be be required to be processed/produced on EDP/ADP 
equipment at the contractor's site(s) (Block #17).

<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>1.     The Contractor shall, at all times during the performance of the 
       Contract, hold a valid Facility Security Clearance at the level of _______ 
       with approved Document Safeguarding at the level of 
       Communications-Electronic Security (COMSEC) ______, and a COMSEC 
       account, issued by the Industrial and Corporate Security Branch 
       (ICSB) of the Department of Supply and Services (DSS).

2.     Contractor personnel who require access to CLASSIFIED information/
       assets or sensitive work sites shall EACH hold a valid personnel 
       security screening at the level of______ granted by ICSB.

3.     Contractor personnel requiring access to COMSEC information/assets 
       shall have undergone a COMSEC briefing.

4.     The Contractor SHALL NOT perform any sensitive (CLASSIFIED/
       DESIGNATED) Automatic/Electronic Data Processing and/or production 
       until ICSB has issued  written approval. After approval has been 
       granted, these tasks may be performed at the level of ______.

5.     The Contractor shall comply with the provisions of the: 

       a)     Security Requirements Check List (SRCL), attached at Annex ___; 

       b)     Industrial Security Manual (June 1992), and COMSEC Supplement 
              issued through ICSB of DSS.
