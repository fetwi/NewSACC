<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>As these goods are defence supplies, Canadian customs duty, excise taxes 
   and Canadian goods and services tax (in the amount of the difference 
   between excise taxes and goods and services tax calculated on the 
   duty-paid value of goods and excise taxes and goods and services tax 
   calculated on the value for the duty of the goods) are remitted with 
   respect to all importations under Order-in-Council P.C. 1966-2184, dated 
   November 24, 1966.
