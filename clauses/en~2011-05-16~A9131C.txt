examine, possess or transfer controlled goods are not registered,

days from receipt of written notification of the contract award,
ensure that the required application(s) for registration or
exemption are submitted to the <abbr title="Controlled Goods Program">CGP</abbr>. No examination, possession or
transfer of controlled goods must be performed until the Contractor
has provided proof, satisfactory to the Contracting Authority, that
the Contractor and any subcontractor are registered, exempt or
excluded under the <abbr title="Controlled Goods Program">CGP</abbr>.

Contracting Authority, that the Contractor and any subcontractor
are registered, exempt or excluded under the <abbr title="Controlled Goods Program">CGP</abbr>, within thirty

will be considered a default under the Contract except to the
extent that Canada is responsible for the failure due to delay in
processing the application.</p>
