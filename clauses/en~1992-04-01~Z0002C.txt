<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">Explanation of delivery term: "<dfn>FOB origin,
freight prepaid</dfn>" means:
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">Free of expense to the Government, delivered
<ol type="i">
<li value="1">on board the indicated type of conveyance of the
carrier (or of the Government, if specified) at a designated point
in the city, county, and province or state from which the shipments
will be made and from which line-haul transportation service (as
distinguished from switching, local drayage, or other terminal
service) will begin;</li>
<li value="2">to, and placed on, the carrier's wharf (at shipside, within
reach of the ship's loading tackle, when the shipping point is
within a port area having water transportation service) or the
carrier's freight station;</li>
<li value="3">to a <abbr title="United States">U.S.</abbr>/Canadian postal service facility; or</li>
<li value="4">if stated in the solicitation, to any Government-designated
point located within the same commercial zone as the <abbr title="Free on Board">FOB</abbr> origin
point specified in the Contract (commercial zones are prescribed by
the <abbr title="United States">U.S.</abbr> Interstate Commerce Commission at 49 <abbr title="Code of Federal Regulations">CFR</abbr> 1048); and</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li value="2">The cost of transportation, ultimately the
Government's obligation, is prepaid by the Contractor to the point
specified in the Contract.</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li value="2">Contractor responsibilities. The Contractor's
responsibilities are the same as those listed in <abbr title="Federal Acquisition Regulation">FAR</abbr> 47.303-1 (b),
except that the Contractor shall prepare Commercial Bills of Lading
or other transportation receipts and shall prepay all freight
charges to the extent specified in the Contract.</li>
</ol>
</div></pre>
