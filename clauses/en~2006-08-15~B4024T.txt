<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>Bidders must provide products that are of the same description, brand name, model and/or part number as detailed in the item description of the bid solicitation. Bidders are advised that substitute products will not be considered.</p>
