<h3 class="clause-heading">Remarks – Recommended Use of SACC Item</h3><section class="remarks group-sacc-remarks field-group-section">
<p>
  Use the following clause in requests for standing offers when the education and 
experience of individuals will be evaluated.
 </p>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>The Offeror certifies that all the information provided in the
<span lang="en-CA">résumés</span> and supporting material submitted with its offer,
particularly the information pertaining to education, achievements,
experience and work history, has been verified by the Offeror to be
true and accurate. Furthermore, the Offeror warrants that every
individual offered by the Offeror for the requirement is capable of
performing the Work resulting from a call-up against the Standing
Offer.</p>
</div></pre>
