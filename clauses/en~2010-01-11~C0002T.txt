<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>The Bidder certifies that the price proposed</p>
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">is not in excess of the lowest price charged anyone
else, including the Bidder's most favoured customer, for the like
quality and quantity of the goods, services or both;</li>
<li value="2">does not include an element of profit on the sale in
excess of that normally obtained by the Bidder on the sale of
goods, services or both of like quality and quantity; and</li>
<li value="3">does not include any provision for discounts to
selling agents.</li>
</ol>
