<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>Unless otherwise directed by the Department of National Defence
(DND) Quality Assurance Authority, the signature of the <abbr title="Department of National Defence">DND</abbr> Quality
Assurance Representative on the release document is not
required.</p>
<p>Material must be released for shipment using either <abbr title="Department of National Defence">DND</abbr> form <cite>CF
1280</cite>, Certificate of Inspection and Release, or a release document
containing the same information. The Contractor must prepare the
release document(s).</p>
<p>For return of repair and overhaul material to the Canadian
Forces Supply System Upgrade, use forms <cite>DND 2227</cite>/<cite>DND 2228</cite> in lieu
of <abbr title="Department of National Defence">DND</abbr> form <cite>CF 1280</cite>.</p>
