<li value="1">The price of the bid will be evaluated as follows:
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">Canadian-based bidders must submit firm prices,

in the bid solicitation, will be applied as a conversion factor to
the bids submitted in foreign currency.</li>
<li value="3">Although Canada reserves the right to award the
Contract either on an <abbr title="Free on Board">FOB</abbr> plant or <abbr title="Free on Board">FOB</abbr> destination, Canada requests

basis.</li>
<li value="4">For the purpose of the bid solicitation, bidders with
an address in Canada are considered Canadian-based bidders and
bidders with an address outside of Canada are considered
foreign-based bidders.</li>
