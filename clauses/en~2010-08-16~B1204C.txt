<pre>Use the following clause in contracts for the Department of National Defence 
when item(s) contain elastomeric material, e.g. hoses, rubber, adhesive 
compounds, etc.

Use Option 1 for submarine spares containing elastomeric materials; and use 
Option 2 for all other items.

Use this clause in conjunction with D2015C and D2016C, if applicable.</pre>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><strong>Option 1:</strong>

item number</strong></span>) will contain 75 percent of the authorized shelf life as listed 
in ISO 2230 at date of delivery to the Department of National Defence.

<strong>Option 2:</strong>
The Contractor must ensure that item(s) ___________ (<span class="wsInlineRemark"><strong>insert the applicable 
item number</strong></span>) will contain 75 percent of the authorized shelf life as listed 
in CFTO D-05-001/SF-000 at date of delivery to the Department of National 
Defence.
