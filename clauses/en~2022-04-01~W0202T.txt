<pre><p>This procurement is subject to the Directive on Government Contracts, Including Real Property Leases, in the Nunavut Settlement Area (the <a href="https://www.tbs-sct.gc.ca/pol/doc-eng.aspx?id=32610" rel="external">Nunavut Directive</a>).</p> <p>The Nunavut Directive has the following objectives:</p> <ol type="a">
<li>Increased participation by Inuit firms in business opportunities in the Nunavut Settlement Area economy;</li>
<li>Improved capacity of Inuit firms to compete for government contracts and real property leases in the Nunavut Settlement Area; and</li>
<li>Employment of Inuit at a representative level in the Nunavut Settlement Area workforce.</li>
</ol></pre>
