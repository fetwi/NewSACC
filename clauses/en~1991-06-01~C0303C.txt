<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>The amount claimed under the terms and conditions of this Contract, as 
computed in accordance with the Basis of Payment, is subject to government 
audit.

Any payments made pending completion of the audit shall be regarded as 
interim payments only and shall be adjusted to the extent necessary to 
reflect the results of the said audit.  If there has been an overpayment, 
it shall be refunded promptly to Canada.

On completion of the Contractor's obligations under the Contract, the 
Contractor shall, if requested, prepare and submit a cost submission to the 
Science Contracting Officer specified in the Contract.  The cost submission 
shall be signed and certified by the Contractor's Senior Financial Officer 
and shall contain a breakdown of the following elements of cost, as 
applicable:

-     Direct labour                   -     Direct materials

-     Direct charges                  -     Subcontracts

Supporting information for each element of cost shall be available and 
   shall be in sufficient detail that an in-depth audit can be performed. 
</pre>
