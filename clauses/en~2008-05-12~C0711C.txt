<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>Time charged and the accuracy of the Contractor's time recording
system are subject to verification by Canada, before or after
payment is made to the Contractor. If verification is done after
payment, the Contractor must repay any overpayment, at Canada's
request.</p>
