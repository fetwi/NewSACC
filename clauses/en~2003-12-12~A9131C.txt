<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>As this Contract requires production of and/or access to controlled goods 
that are subject to the <em>Defence Production Act</em>, the Contractor is hereby 
advised that only persons who are registered, exempt or excluded under the 
Controlled Goods Program (CGP) are lawfully entitled to examine, possess or 
transfer controlled goods.

The Contractor must maintain its registration or exemption from the CGP for 
the duration of the Contract.

Details on the CGP are available at: <strong class="wsStyleUnderline">http://www.cgp.gc.ca</strong>.
