<h3 class="clause-heading">Remarks â€“ Recommended Use of SACC Item</h3><section class="remarks group-sacc-remarks field-group-section">
<p>
  Use this clause in contracts to define the palletization requirements for 
shipments to Canadian Forces Supply Depots.
 </p>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">For all shipments exceeding 0.566 m3 or 15.88 kg (20
ft3 or 35 lbs), except for those shipped by courier, the following
applies:
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">The Contractor must strap, and if necessary wrap,
shipments on standard 1.22 m x 1.02 m (48 in. x 40 in.) wood
pallets. The four-way forklift entry pallet must be supplied at no
charge to Department of National Defence. Total height, including
pallet, must not exceed 1.19 m (47 in.). The pallet load must not
extend further than 2.54 cm (1 in.) from any edge of the
pallet.</li>
<li value="2">The Contractor must group items by stock number (on
the same pallet) within consolidated shipments. Pallet loads
composed of more than one stock number must be marked as "<strong>Mixed
Items</strong>".</li>
<li value="3">Individual items exceeding 1.22 m (48 in.) in length
or 453.6 kg (1000 lbs) must be secured to larger pallets or must
have 10.16 cm x 10.16 cm (4 in. x 4 in.) skids securely fastened to
the bottom of the item. Skids must be separated by a minimum of
71.12 cm (28 in.).</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li value="2">Any exception requires the prior approval of the
Contracting Authority.</li>
</ol>
</div></pre>
