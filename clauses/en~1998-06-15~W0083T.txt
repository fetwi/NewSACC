<p>The benefits that apply to this procurement are contained in:
Section 5.3, <cite>Teslin Tlingit Council Final Agreement (TTFA)</cite>,
clauses:</p>
<ul class="hierarchy">
<li><span class="number">17.14.2.2</span> Government shall provide
written notice to the Teslin Tlingit Council of any invitation for
public tenders for contracts associated with silviculture within
the Teslin Tlingit Council Traditional Territory.</li>
<li><span class="number">17.14.2.3</span> The Teslin Tlingit
Council shall have the first opportunity to accept any fixed term
contract offered by Government associated with silviculture within
the Teslin Tlingit Council Traditional Territory.</li>
<li><span class="number">17.14.2.6</span> Government shall include
a criterion for Teslin Tlingit employment in any contract
opportunities associated with silviculture in the Teslin Tlingit
Council Traditional Territory.</li>
</ul>
