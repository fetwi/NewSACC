<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<p>This procurement is conditionally limited to Canadian
services.</p>
<p>Subject to the evaluation procedures contained in the request
for standing offer, offerors acknowledge that only offers with a
certification that the services offered are Canadian services, as
defined in clause A3050T, may be considered.</p>
<p>Failure to provide this certification completed with the offer
will result in the services offered being treated as non-Canadian
services.</p>
<p>The Offeror certifies that:</p>
<p><var title="checkbox">( )</var> the services offered are
Canadian services as defined in paragraph 4 of clause A3050T.</p>
<p>For more information on how to determine the Canadian content
for a mix of goods, a mix of services or a mix of goods and
services, consult Annex 3.6.(9), Example 2, of the <cite>Supply Manual</cite>.</p>
</div></pre>
