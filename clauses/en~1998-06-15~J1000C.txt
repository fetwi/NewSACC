not delivered the supplies or services specified therein, in accordance 
with the specifications, terms, and conditions of the Contract, the 
Minister of Public Works and Government Services hereby terminates the

The Minister reserves Canada's right under the Contract to charge you for 
all losses and damages which may be suffered by Canada by reason of your 
default, including any amount in excess of the Contract Price which Canada 
may be obliged to pay in procuring the said supplies and services elsewhere.
