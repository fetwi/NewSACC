<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">The Contractor must submit a claim for payment using
form PWGSC-TPSGC 1111, Claim for Progress Payment.
<br/>Each claim must show:
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">all information required on form PWGSC-TPSGC 1111;</li>
<li value="2">all applicable information detailed under the section
entitled "Invoice Submission" of the general conditions;</li>
</ol>
<span class="description"><strong><em>Instruction to contracting officers:</em></strong>
<em>Insert specific information that must be shown on the claim and
is not covered under (a) and (b) above to reflect the requirement.
The following are examples only</em>.</span>
<ol type="a">
<li value="3">a list of all expenses;</li>
<li value="4">expenditures plus pro-rated profit or fee;</li>
<li value="5">the description and value of the milestone claimed as
detailed in the Contract.</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li value="2">Applicable Taxes, must be calculated on the total amount of
the claim before the holdback is applied. At the time the holdback
is claimed, there will be no Applicable Taxes payable as it was claimed and
payable under the previous claims for progress payments.</li>
<li value="3">The Contractor must prepare and certify one original
and two (2) copies of the claim on form PWGSC-TPSGC 1111, and
forward it to the <span class="input-required"><var>__________</var> <kbd>(<em>insert "Project" or
"Technical" or "Inspection"</em>)</kbd></span> Authority identified under
the section entitled "Authorities" of the Contract for appropriate
certification after inspection and acceptance of the Work takes
place.
<br/>The <span class="input-required"><var>________</var> <kbd>(<em>insert
"Project" or "Technical" or "Inspection"</em>)</kbd></span> Authority
will then forward the original and two (2) copies of the claim to
the Contracting Authority for certification and onward submission
to the Payment Office for the remaining certification and payment
action.</li>
<li value="4">The Contractor must not submit claims until all work
identified in the claim is completed.</li>
</ol>
