<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>If required, a present value analysis will be performed by Canada during 
the evaluation phase to determine the price for evaluation purposes.  This 
analysis will be based on anticipated disbursements by Canada, with an 
annual interest rate determined by the Contracting Authority.  To this end, 
the bid must include a schedule of Canada's anticipated monthly 
disbursements on account of the proposed Work.
