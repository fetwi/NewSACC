<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>"This is further to your facsimile/letter of _____ addressed to _____.

As your have indicated that you are unwilling or unable to deliver the 
supplies described in the above-referenced Contract in accordance with the 
specifications, terms and conditions of the Contract, the Minister of 
Public Works and Government Services hereby terminates the said Contract 
pursuant to the Default provisions of the General Conditions forming part 
of the Contract.

The Minister reserves Canada's right under the Contract to charge you for 
all loss and damage which may be suffered by Canada by reason of your 
default, including any amount in excess of the Contract Price which Canada 
may be obliged to pay in procuring the said supplies elsewhere."
