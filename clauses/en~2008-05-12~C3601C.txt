<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>The prices detailed in the Contract are subject to adjustment to reflect 
the actual minimum wholesale prices for milk established by the provincial 
Milk Marketing Board.  A copy of the Contractor's notification of price 
adjustment from the provincial Milk Marketing Board must be provided to the 
Contracting Authority.

The Contract will be amended to reflect the actual price of the adjustment 
at time of delivery.  The Contractor must not invoice at prices other than 
those specified in the Contract.


</pre>
