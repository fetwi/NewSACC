<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">The portion of the price directly related to the base
transaction cost of paper is subject to price adjustment (increase
or decrease) at any time during the standing offer period but not
before <span class="input-required"><var>_______</var> <kbd>(<em>insert
the date corresponding to the end of the 30-day period after the
issuance of the standing offer</em>)</kbd></span>. The price will be

that came into effect by applying it to the applicable base
transaction cost.</li>
<li value="2">To request a price adjustment, the Offeror must
provide the Standing Offer Authority with a notice indicating the
increase or decrease in the base transaction cost of paper
identified below. Such notice must contain the price as publicly
announced by at least three (3) paper suppliers who supply the
grade specified in the Standing Offer and the date the price came
into effect.</li>
<li value="3">The base transaction cost of paper subject to price
adjustment is as follows:

represent <var>_____</var> percent of the unit price(s).<br/>
Brand name and paper supplier: <var>___________________</var>.</li>
<li value="2">text stock (if applicable): <var>______</var> <abbr title="pounds">lb</abbr>. at
a base transaction cost per <abbr title="per hundred weight">Cwt</abbr>. of $<var>______</var> , and which
represent <var>______</var> percent of the unit price(s).<br/>
Brand name and paper supplier: <var>___________________</var>.</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li value="4">The Offeror must provide a copy of the quotation from
the paper supplier(s) to support the above base transaction cost
and a copy of the revised quotation from the paper supplier(s) to
support the adjusted base transaction cost.</li>
<li value="5">Any price adjustment must be approved by the Standing
Offer Authority and will be evidenced through a revision to the
Standing Offer.</li>
</ol>
