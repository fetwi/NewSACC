<pre>Fresh chilled or frozen meats must be delivered in accordance with Canadian 
   Forces Purchase Description stipulating that frozen products shall be 
   maintained at -18oC or lower and chilled preserved products at 4oC or 
   lower until delivery to the Canadian Forces, and that frozen chilled 
   preserved products shall not be delivered in unrefrigerated vehicles. 
   The products shall be delivered to the consignee in good condition, 
   shall show no evidence of deterioration and, if a frozen product, shall 
   not be frozen for longer than 90 days, since processing in the fresh 
   state, prior to delivery. 
</pre>
