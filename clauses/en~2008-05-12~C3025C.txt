<h3 class="clause-heading">Remarks â€“ Recommended Use of SACC Item</h3><section class="remarks group-sacc-remarks field-group-section">
 Use the following clause in contracts when the basis of payment for exchange 
rate adjustment is based on "actual costs incurred"  and the method of payment 
may be any one of: payment on delivery, milestone or progress.  A key 
requirement of this clause is that the contractor must provide evidence of 
payment when submitting its claim for exchange rate adjustment to Canada.
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">The price must be adjusted either upwards or
downwards to reflect the actual costs incurred associated with the
actual foreign currency exchange rate obtained by the Contractor
when remitting payment to a foreign-based supplier or subcontractor
for the goods, services or both.</li>
<li value="2">On each invoice or claim for payment submitted under
the Contract, the Contractor must indicate the foreign value or
exchange rate adjustment amount (either upward, downward or no
change) as a separate item. The Contractor must provide appropriate
documentation showing evidence of payment to a foreign-based
supplier or subcontractor for the goods, services or both included
in the claimed amount.</li>
<li value="3">Canada will have the right to audit any revision to
costs and prices under this clause.</li>
</ol>
