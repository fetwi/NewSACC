<cite><a href="http://laws-lois.justice.gc.ca/eng/acts/H-3/" rel="external">Hazardous Products Act</a></cite>, <abbr title="Revised Statutes of Canada">R.S.C.</abbr> 1985, <abbr title="chapter">c.</abbr> H-3 and regulation(s) in accordance with the said Act and regulation(s) accompanied by the required material safety data sheet(s) completed in either English or French.
