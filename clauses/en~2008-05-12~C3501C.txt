<p>
  Use the following clause in contracts for the purchase of petroleum products 
when prices are subject to adjustment in accordance with any increase or 
decrease established by the petroleum producer.
 </p>
<p>
  Contracting officers must use option 1 or option 2, as applicable.
 </p>
</section>
<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre><div class="sacc-item">
<ul class="no-markers">
<li><h2>Option 1</h2>
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">The prices detailed in the Contract are subject to
upward or downward adjustment <var>_____</var> calendar days after
<span class="input-required"><var>_____</var> <kbd>(<em><strong>insert the applicable
date</strong></em>)</kbd></span> to allow for:
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">changes due to price adjustment in petroleum products
that are a direct result of increased or decreased prices imposed
by the petroleum producer. A copy of the Contractor's notification
of price increase or decrease from the petroleum producer must be
provided to the Contracting Authority; and/or</li>
<li value="2">imposition of any new or changes to any existing
levies, tariffs or fees of whatsoever nature applicable to any
petroleum product, authorized, imposed or agreed to by Canada or
any provincial government or by any Governmental Regulatory
Authority.</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li value="2">The Contract will be amended to reflect the actual
price of the increase or decrease at time of delivery. The
Contractor must not invoice at prices other than those specified in
the Contract.</li>
</ol></li>
