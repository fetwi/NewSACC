(term usually stated in months or years), under the same terms and 
conditions, at the ______________ (prices or rates) shown in the Basis of 
Payment.

Should Canada decide to exercise the option, it will do so by notifying the 
   Contractor, in writing, no later than ______ days prior to the expiry of 
   this Contract or any extension thereof.
