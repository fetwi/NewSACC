<pre>Use the following clause in competitive contracts including contracts for the 
Department of National Defence (clauses C2600T and C2600C may apply), when 
delivery is FOB destination and the contractor is responsible for all delivery 
charges, administration, costs and risks of transport and customs clearance, 
including the payment of customs duties and taxes.  

Use clause C5200T in the bid solicitation and C5200C in the contract.</pre>
