<p>
  Use the following clause in contracts to define Department of National Defence's packaging requirements for the procurement of items which are covered by the following categories:
 </p>
<ol type="a">
<li>
   Commercial Off-the-Shelf (COTS);
  </li>
<li>
   direct to customer, for immediate use (including modifications);
  </li>
<li>
<abbr title="Co-Operative Logistics">
    COLOG
   </abbr>
   (co-operative logistics); or
  </li>
<li>
   items not covered by another Canadian Forces commodity packaging specification (see D3016C) or military packaging (see D3013C).
  </li>
</ol>
</section>
