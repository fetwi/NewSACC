<pre>Use the following clause in competitive requests for standing offers for 
multiple items (line item) when competition is solely limited to Canadian goods 
and the certification must be done on an aggregate basis as defined in paragraph 
3 (a) of clause A3050T.

Use clause M3052T for multiple items when the certification must be done on an 
item by item basis.

Use this clause in conjunction with A3050T and M3060C.

Contracting officers must review the Canadian Content Policy (Supply Manual, 
Chapter 5 and Annex 5.1) to establish if the requirement is subject to the 
policy.</pre>
