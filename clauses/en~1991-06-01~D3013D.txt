<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>1.  For contractors located in Canada:  

       Preservation and packaging shall be in accordance with the Canadian 
       Forces Packaging Specification D-LM-008-OO1/SF-001, and shall be 
       marked to D-LM-008-OO2/SF-001.  Level B "PKG DATA FORM REQD", shall 
       be in accordance with CFPS D-LM-008-011/SF-001.  

2.  For contractors located in U.S.A.:  

       Preservation and packaging shall be in accordance with U.S. 
       Department of Defense Military Standard MIL-STD-794 and shall be 
       marked to MIL-STD-129.  Level B "PKG DATA FORM REQD" shall be in 
       accordance with MIL-STD-834 and MIL-STD-726.  

3.  Approval Authorities.  

       When the "PKG REQTS CODE" for a specific item indicates "PKG DATA 
   FORM REQD", packaging data forms previously approved by Canadian or 
   United States authorities shall be acceptable.   
</pre>
