<pre>Dangerous goods/Hazardous Materials - material which is classed as 
dangerous/hazardous shall be marked :

a. shipping container - in accordance with the Transportation of Dangerous 
Goods Act: and

b. Immediate product container - in accordance with the Hazardous Products 
Act.

Bilingual Material Safety Data Sheets (2 copies) indicating the NATO Stock 
Number shall be provided as follows:

1.     one (1) copy to be enclosed with the shipment, and
