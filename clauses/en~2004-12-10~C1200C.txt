<h3 class="clause-heading">Legal text for SACC item</h3><pre>The Contractor will be paid its costs reasonably and properly incurred in 
the performance of the Work, as established by government audit in 
accordance with Contract Cost Principles 1031-2, plus _________ (<strong><em>insert as 
applicable: "a fixed fee of $_______ or "a profit of ______ percent"</em></strong>) of 
audited costs, to a ceiling price of $ ________.  The results and findings 
of government's audit shall be conclusive.

This ceiling price is subject to downward adjustment so as not to exceed 
the actual cost incurred plus the specified _______ (<strong><em>insert as applicable: 
"fixed fee" or "profit</em></strong>").  Upon completion of the audit, the price shall be 
adjusted to the extent necessary to reflect the results of the audit.  If 
there has been any overpayment, it shall be promptly refunded to Canada.

No increase in the total liability of Canada or in the price of Work 
resulting from any design changes, modifications or interpretations of the 
specifications will be authorized or paid by Canada to the Contractor 
unless such design changes, modifications or interpretations have been 
approved, in writing, by the Contracting Authority, prior to their 
incorporation in the Work.
