<p>
  Use the clause in contracts with United States (U.S.)-based contractors to 
define Department of National Defence's military packaging requirements for 
militarized items which are covered by the following categories:
 </p>
<ol type="a">
<li>
   items not covered by a Canadian Forces commodity packaging specification 
(see
   
    D3016C
   
   ) or commercial packaging (see
   
    D3018C
   
   );
  </li>
<li>
   repairable material for national stock;
  </li>
<li>
   repair and overhaul of repairable material.
  </li>
</ol>
<p>
  Use this clause in conjunction with
  
   D3013C
  
  in the part "resulting contract 
clauses" of the bid solicitation.  At contract award, use either this clause or
  
   D3013C
  
  depending on whether the contractor is Canadian-based or
  <abbr title="United States">
   U.S.
  </abbr>
  based.
 </p>
</section>
