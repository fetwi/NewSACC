<p>The Contractor must follow these procedures for any proposed design change/deviation to contract specifications.</p>
<h2>Option 1</h2>
<p>The Contractor must complete Part 1 of form <a href="http://www.tpsgc-pwgsc.gc.ca/app-acq/forms/documents/9038.pdf" rel="external">PWGSC-TPSGC 9038 (PDF 241 KB)</a> - (<a href="https://buyandsell.gc.ca/help/accessibility-help#20">Help on File Formats</a>), Design Change/Deviation, and forward <span class="input-required"><var>_______</var> <var>___</var><kbd>(<em>insert number</em>)</kbd></span> copies to the <span class="input-required"> <var>______</var> Authority <kbd>(<em>insert "Technical" or "Project", as applicable</em>)</kbd> </span>and one (1) copy to the Contracting Authority.</p>
<h2>Option 2</h2>
<p>The Contractor must complete Part 1 of the Design Change/Deviation form <abbr title="Department of National Defence">DND</abbr> 672 and forward <span class="input-required"><var>_______</var> <var>___</var><kbd>(<em>insert number</em>)</kbd></span> copies to the <span class="input-required"> <var>______</var> Authority <kbd>(<em>insert "Technical" or "Project", as applicable</em>)</kbd> </span> and one (1) copy to the
Contracting Authority.</p>
<p>The Contractor will be authorized to proceed upon receipt of the design change/deviation form signed by the Contracting Authority. A contract amendment will be issued to incorporate the design change/deviation in the Contract.</p>
