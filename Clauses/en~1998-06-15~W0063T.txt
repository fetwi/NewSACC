<p>The benefits that apply to this procurement are contained in:
Section 5.1, <cite>First Nation of Nacho Nyak Dun Final Agreement</cite>
(NNDFA), clauses:</p>
<ul class="hierarchy">
<li><span class="number">17.14.2.2</span> Government shall provide
written notice to the First Nation of Nacho Nyak Dun of any
invitation for public tenders in respect of contracts associated
with silviculture within the Traditional Territory for the first
Nation of Nacho Nyak Dun.</li>
<li><span class="number">17.14.2.3</span> The First Nation of Nacho
Nyak Dun shall have the first opportunity to accept any fixed term
contract offered by Government associated with silviculture within
the Traditional Territory of the First Nation of Nacho Nyak
Dun.</li>
<li><span class="number">17.14.2.6</span> Government shall include
a criterion for Nacho Nyak Dun employment in any contract
opportunities associated with silviculture within the Traditional
Territory of the First Nation of Nacho Nyak Dun.</li>
</ul>
