<pre><div class="sacc-item">
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">Products that are equivalent in form, fit, function and quality to the item(s) specified in the bid solicitation will be considered where the Bidder:
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">designates the brand name, model and/or part number of the substitute product;</li>
<li value="2">states that the substitute product is fully interchangeable with the item specified;</li>
<li value="3">provides complete specifications and descriptive literature for each substitute product;</li>
<li value="4">provides compliance statements that include technical specifics showing the substitute product meets all mandatory performance criteria that are specified in the bid solicitation; and</li>
<li value="5">clearly identifies those areas in the specifications and descriptive literature that support the substitute product's compliance with any mandatory performance criteria.</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li value="2">Products offered as equivalent in form, fit, function and quality will not be considered if:
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">the bid fails to provide all the information requested to allow the Contracting Authority to fully evaluate the
equivalency of each substitute product; or</li>
<li value="2">the substitute product fails to meet or exceed the mandatory performance criteria specified in the bid solicitation for that item.</li>
</ol>
</li>
<li value="3">In conducting its evaluation of the bids, Canada may, but will have no obligation to, request bidders offering a substitute product to demonstrate, at the sole cost of bidders, that the substitute product is equivalent to the item specified in the bid solicitation.</li>
</ol>
</div></pre>
