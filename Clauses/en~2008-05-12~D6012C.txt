<p>In addition to the shipping requirements contained in the
section entitled "Transportation Charges" in the general
conditions, the Contractor must ship the goods in accordance with
the following shipping instructions:</p>
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">for shipments (truck/rail) weighing under 10,000 lbs
and (air) under 1,000 lbs, the Contractor must, unless otherwise
directed, ship the goods in accordance with normal shipping
practice;</li>
<li value="2">for shipments (truck/rail) weighing 10,000 lbs and
over and (air) 1,000 lbs and over, the Contractor must:
<ol type="i">
<li value="1">provide the Contracting Authority with the following
information:
<ul>
<li>contract number;</li>
<li>commodity and freight classification;</li>
<li>gross weight and cube of shipment;</li>
<li>name of railway serving the Contractor's plant site (if
applicable);</li>
<li>shipping point and address;</li>
<li>date of availability;</li>
<li>consignee and destination address;</li>
<li>Contractor's recommended method of shipment and cost;</li>
<li>type of packaging and dimensions of each package;</li>
<li>if shipment is dangerous goods or hazardous products, the
United Nations number, class, division, packing group and packing
instructions;</li>
<li>contact name and phone number; and</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li value="2">not make any shipment before receiving directions from the
Contracting Authority concerning mode of shipment, carrier,
routing, and method of billing for transportation charges.</li>
</ol>
</li>
</ol>
