<pre>All meat and meat products, poultry and poultry products, lard, shortening 
   and margarine containing animal fats, and soups containing ingredients 
   of animal origin, shall be inspected and stamped "CDA Inspected for CG" 
   by inspectors of the Food Products and Inspection Branch, Agriculture 
   Canada, and delivered direct from an establishment registered under the 
   Canada Meat Inspection Act and Regulations, to the consignee.  Unstamped 
   products will not be accepted. 
</pre>
