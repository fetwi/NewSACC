<pre>1.     Upon completion of the Contract or annually for multi-year contracts 
       spanning more than one contractor fiscal year, the Contractor must 
       complete the form PWGSC-TPSGC 7953, Contractors Cost Submission, and 
       forward three (3) copies to the Contracting Authority.  Costs will 
       be computed in accordance with Contract Cost Principles 1031-2.

2.     The cost submission must contain a breakdown of all applicable cost 
       elements as detailed in the Contract and must be signed and 
       certified accurate by the Contractor's Senior Financial Officer, 
       unless stated otherwise in writing by the Contracting Authority.

3.     Supporting information for each cost element must be available in 
       sufficient detail to allow for an in-depth audit.
