<p>The benefits that apply to this procurement are contained in
Part 6 - Bid Criteria of Article 24 - Government Contracts of the
Nunavut Land Claims Agreement (NLCA), section:</p>
<p><strong>24.6.1</strong> Whenever practicable, and
consistent with sound procurement management, and subject to
Canada's international obligations, all of the following criteria,
or as many as may be appropriate with respect to any particular
contract, shall be included in the bid criteria established by the
Government of Canada for the awarding of its government contracts
in the Nunavut Settlement Area:</p>
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">the existence of head offices, administrative offices
or other facilities in the Nunavut Settlement Area;</li>
<li value="2">the employment of Inuit labour, engagement of Inuit
professional services, or use of suppliers that are Inuit or Inuit
firms in carrying out the contracts; or</li>
<li value="3">the undertaking of commitments, under the contract,
with respect to on-the-job training or skills development for
Inuit.</li>
</ol>
