<pre>1.     Deliverable end items shall conform to drawings and specifications 
       approved by the Design Authority. The following shall be forwarded 
       to the Design Authority for approval prior to the commencement of 
       manufacture:

       (a)    two (2) data lists of all drawings and specifications;
       (b)    two (2) sets of drawings detailing:
              (1)    complete round markings;
              (2)    method of packing, including dimensions;
              (3)    packing markings; and
              (4)    method of palletization.

2.     The Design Authority shall return, within thirty (30) days, one copy 
       either approved or showing the changes which Canada requires.
