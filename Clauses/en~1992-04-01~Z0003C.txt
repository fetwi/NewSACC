<ol type="1">
<li value="1">Free of expense to the <abbr title="United States">U.S.</abbr> Government, on board the

<li value="2">Supplies shall be delivered to the destination
consignee's wharf (if destination is a port city and supplies are

the expense of the Contractor. The <abbr title="United States">U.S.</abbr> Government shall not be
liable for any delivery, storage, demurrage, accessorial, or other
charges involved before the actual delivery (or "constructive
placement" as defined in carrier tariffs) of the supplies to the
destination, unless such charges are caused by an act or order of
the Government acting in its Contractual capacity. If rail carrier
is used, supplies shall be delivered to the specified unloading
platform of the consignee. If motor carrier (including "piggyback")
is used, supplies shall be delivered to truck tailgate at the
unloading platform of the consignee. If the Contractor uses rail
carrier or freight forwarder for less than carload shipments, the
Contractor shall assure that the carrier will furnish tailgate
delivery if transfer to truck is required to complete delivery to
consignee.</li>
