<pre>The Contractor shall not insure the risks to Canada arising from the use or 
operation of an aircraft chartered by Canada on a long-term basis except 
where aircraft owners or operators insist that such insurance shall be 
obtained and maintained during the period of the Contract.  In this 
situation, the insurance coverage to be obtained and maintained by Canada 
shall not be less than the minimum insurance coverage set forth in the Air 
Transportation Regulations sections 6, 7 and 8.

A copy or evidence of such insurance is to be provided to the Lessee.
</pre>
