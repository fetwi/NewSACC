<pre>As the goods to be supplied under the Contract are defence supplies, 
customs duties on importation to Canada may be remitted under the Defence 
Supplies Remission Order, Order-in-Council P.C. 1992-2415 dated November 26, 
1992, published in the Canada Gazette Part II as SOR/92-691.

The Contractor will be responsible for pre-arranging remission on 
   importation or for paying customs duties on importation and applying to 
   Revenue Canada for a refund.  The importer is responsible for applying 
   to Department of Supply and Services in good time for the certification 
   required by the Order.
</pre>
