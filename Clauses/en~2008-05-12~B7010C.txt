<li value="1">The Contractor must clearly mark labels in accordance with the following:
<ol type="a">
<li value="1"><strong>Marking</strong>: A label must be positioned as described in the technical data. The label and markings must be in accordance with Department of National Defence specification D-80-001-055/SF-001. The markings must include characters not less than 1/8 inch (3.2 mm) and not more than 1/4 inch (6.3 mm) in height the following information:
<ol type="1">
<li value="1">Contract Number (e.g. W8463-2-BD0W/01-PC);</li>
<li value="2"><abbr title="North Atlantic Treaty Organization">NATO</abbr> Stock Number (number will be designated in the Contract for item or size) (e.g. 8415-21-909-7043);</li>
<li value="3">size identification (see Scale of Measurements, e.g. 6732); and</li>
<li value="4">date of start of production (month and year, e.g. 12 2007).</li>
</ol>
<br/>When the marking of items as required above is not feasible, the <abbr title="North Atlantic Treaty Organization">NATO</abbr> Stock Number must be etched or indelibly marked where
possible.</li>
<li value="2"><strong>Care labelling</strong>: As specified in the technical data, the care symbols must be in accordance with Canadian General Standards Board CAN/<abbr title="Canadian General Standards Board ">CGSB</abbr>-86.1 and as detailed in item description of the Contract.</li>
</ol>
