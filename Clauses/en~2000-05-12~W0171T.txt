<p>The benefits that apply to this procurement are contained in:
Section 5.7, <cite>Tr'ondëk Hwëch'in Final Agreement</cite> (THFA),
clauses:</p>
<ul class="hierarchy">
<li><span class="number">13.12.1.1</span> Government shall provide
written notice to the Tr'ondëk Hwëch'in of any public
tender for contracts associated with the management of a Designated
Heritage Site directly related to the history or culture of
Tr'ondëk Hwëch'in within the Traditional Territory of the
Tr'ondëk Hwëch'in.</li>
<li><span class="number">13.12.1.2</span> Government shall include
the Tr'ondëk Hwëch'in in any invitational tender for
contracts associated with the management of a Designated Heritage
Site directly related to the history or culture of the
Tr'ondëk Hwëch'in within the Traditional Territory of the
Tr'ondëk Hwëch'in.</li>
<li><span class="number">13.12.1.3</span> The Tr'ondëk
Hwëch'in shall have the first opportunity to accept any
contract offered by Government other than by public or invitational
tender associated with the management of a Designated Heritage Site
directly related to the history or culture of Tr'ondëk
Hwëch'in within the Traditional Territory of the Tr'ondëk
Hwëch'in upon the same terms and conditions as would be
offered to others.</li>
<li><span class="number">13.12.1.7</span> Government shall include
in any contract opportunities associated with a Designated Heritage
Site directly related to the history or culture of Tr'ondëk
Hwëch'in within the Traditional Territory of the Tr'ondëk
Hwëch'in:
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">a criterion for the employment of Tr'ondëk
Hwëch'in or engagement of Tr'ondëk Hwëch'in Firms;
and</li>
<li value="2">a criterion for special knowledge or experience of
Tr'ondëk Hwëch'in related to the Designated Heritage
Site.</li>
</ol>
</li>
</ul>
</div></pre>
