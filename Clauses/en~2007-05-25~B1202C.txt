<p>The Contractor must apply age control for age sensitive elastomeric materials as specified in Canadian Forces specification <cite>CFTO D-05-001-001/SF-000</cite> (refers to Society of Automotive Engineering, Aerospace Recommended Practices for age control of aerospace elastomeric materials) to all aircraft, aircraft components, aircraft accessories and to elastomeric items when the elastomeric items are used in contact with fuel, hydraulic fluid, oil, alcohol, or oxygen, or when the elastomeric items form part of a pneumatic, coolant or any other fluid or gaseous systems.</p>
<p>Canada will not accept products that do not comply with the above specification.</p>
