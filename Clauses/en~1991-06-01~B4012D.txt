<pre>All open bearing are to be inhibited with a preservative compound 
conforming to standard 31-GP-3M (MIL-C-16173) Gr 2 or MIL-C-11796B.

All seals or shielded bearings must be of current production. Bearings 
   manufactured within a period of one year from date of delivery to DND 
   will be accepted as current production.
