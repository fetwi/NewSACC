<p>Bidders must submit as part of their bid:</p>
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">a recommended list of publications for approval by
the Technical Authority;</li>
<li value="2">a price for any or all publications based on:
<ol type="i">
<li value="1">options 1 to 3 below for the bilingual publications
and</li>
<li value="2">options 1 or 2 below for the unilingual publications and
for the right to translate or reproduce the unilingual
publications.</li></ol>
<p>The price structure must be itemized in such a way as to clearly
reflect the price differential between the different options and to
clearly indicate what costs are directly attributable to the second
official language requirement; and</p></li>
<li value="3">delivery lead times required for the option(s)
selected.</li>
</ol>
<h2>Bilngual Publications</h2>
<h3>Option 1: Newly Written Manuals</h3>
<p>All publications provided in both English and French, in one of
the following formats: side-by-side, separate issues, separate
joined, facing pages, or over and under. The selected format must
be in full compliance with the latest issue of <cite>C-01-100-100/AG-006,
Specification - Writing, Format and Production of Technical
Publications</cite>.</p>
<h3>Option 2: Existing Manuals</h3>
<p>All publications provided as existing commercial or foreign
government off-the-shelf manuals in both English and French, in
side-by-side format. The format must be in full compliance with the
latest issue of <cite>C-01-100-100/AG-005, Adoption of Commercial and
Foreign Government Publications</cite>. When existing commercial
publications are not available, Option 1 must apply.</p>
<h3>Option 3: Alternate Format</h3>
<p>All publications provided in the format approved by the
Technical Authority, in both English and French. The format must be
in full compliance with the latest issue of <cite>C-01-100-100/AG-005,
Adoption of Commercial and Foreign Government
Publications</cite>. This option requires the written approval of
the Technical Authority before contract award.</p>
<h2>Unilingual Publications</h2>
<h3>Option 1: Newly Written Manuals</h3>
<p>All publications provided in full compliance with the latest
issue of <cite>C-01-100-100/AG-006, Specification - Writing, Format and
Production of Technical Publications</cite>.</p>
<h3>Option 2: Existing Manuals</h3>
<p>All publications provided as existing commercial or foreign
government off-the-shelf manuals must be in full compliance with
the latest issue of <cite>C-01-100- 100/AG-005, Adoption of Commercial and
Foreign Government Publications</cite>. When existing commercial
publications are not available, Option 1 must apply.</p>
<h3>Right to Translate and Reproduce</h3>
<p>For publications provided as existing unilingual commercial
manuals in full compliance with the latest issue of
<cite>C-01-100-100/AG-005,Adoption of Commercial and Foreign Government
Publications</cite>. Canada must be given the right to translate and
reproduce, for government purposes, any or part of those
publications. Bidders must submit a price to give that right to
Canada.</p>
