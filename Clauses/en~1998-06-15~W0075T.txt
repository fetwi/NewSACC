<p>The benefits that apply to this procurement are contained in:
Section 5.2, <cite>Champagne and Aishihik First Nations Final Agreement</cite>
(CAFA), clauses:</p>
<ul class="hierarchy">
<li><span class="number">17.14.2.2</span> Government shall provide
written notice to the Champagne and Aishihik First Nations of any
invitation for public tenders for contracts associated with
silviculture within the Champagne and Aishihik First Nations
Traditional Territory.</li>
<li><span class="number">17.14.2.3</span> The Champagne and
Aishihik First Nations shall have the first opportunity to accept
any fixed term contract offered by Government associated with
silviculture within the Champagne and Aishihik First Nations
Traditional Territory.</li>
<li><span class="number">17.14.2.6</span> Government shall include
a criterion for Champagne Aishihik Person employment in any
contract opportunities associated with silviculture within the
Champagne and Aishihik First Nations Traditional Territory.</li>
</ul>
