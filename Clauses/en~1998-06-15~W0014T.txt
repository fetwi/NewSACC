<p>The benefits that apply to this procurement are contained in:
Section 1, James Bay and Northern Quebec Agreement (JBNQA),
clause:</p>
<ul class="hierarchy">
<li><span class="number">7.1</span> Whenever practicable and
consistent with sound procurement management, all of the following
criteria, or as many as may be appropriate with respect to any
particular government contract, shall be included in the bid
evaluation criteria established by the contracting authority for
the awarding of government contracts in the Territory:
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">the contribution by Inuit in carrying out the
contract, which will include, but shall not be limited to, the
employment of Inuit labour, the engagement of Inuit professional
services or the use of Inuit suppliers;</li>
<li value="2">creation of permanent head offices, administrative
offices or other facilities in the Territory; and,</li>
<li value="3">the undertaking of commitments, under the contract,
with respect to on-the-job training or skills development for the
Inuit.</li>
</ol>

</ul>
