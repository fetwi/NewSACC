<p>Contracts with former public servants (FPS) in receipt of a
pension or of a lump sum payment must bear the closest public
scrutiny and reflect fairness in the spending of public funds. In
order to comply with Treasury Board policies and directives on
contracts with <abbr title="Former public servant">FPS</abbr>, bidders must provide the information required
below.</p>
<h2>Definitions</h2>
<p>For the purposes of this clause,<dfn>"former public servant"</dfn> is any former member of a department as
defined in the <cite><a href="http://laws-lois.justice.gc.ca/eng/acts/f-11/ " rel="external">Financial Administration Act</a></cite>, <abbr title="Revised Statutes">R.S.</abbr>,
1985, <abbr title="chapter">c</abbr>. F-11, a former member of the Canadian Armed Forces or a
former member of the Royal Canadian Mounted Police. A former public
servant may be:</p>
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">an individual;</li>
<li value="2">an individual who has incorporated;</li>
<li value="3">a partnership made of former public servants; or</li>
<li value="4">a sole proprietorship or entity where the affected
individual has a controlling or major interest in the entity.</li>
</ol>
<p><dfn>"lump sum payment period"</dfn> means the period measured in
weeks of salary, for which payment has been made to facilitate the
transition to retirement or to other employment as a result of the
implementation of various programs to reduce the size of the Public
Service. The lump sum payment period does not include the period of
severance pay, which is measured in a like manner.</p>
<p><dfn>"pension"</dfn> means, in the context of the fee abatement
formula, a pension or annual allowance paid under the <cite><a href="http://laws-lois.justice.gc.ca/eng/acts/P-36/FullText.html " rel="external">Public Service Superannuation Act</a></cite> (PSSA), <abbr title="Revised Statutes">R.S.</abbr>, 1985, <abbr title="chapter">c.</abbr> P-36, and
any increases paid pursuant to the <cite><a href="http://laws-lois.justice.gc.ca/eng/acts/S-24/page-2.html" rel="external">Supplementary Retirement Benefits Act</a></cite>, <abbr title="Revised Statutes">R.S.</abbr>, 1985, <abbr title="chapter">c.</abbr> S-24 as it affects the <abbr title="Public Service Superannuation Act">PSSA</abbr>. It
does not include pensions payable pursuant to the <cite><a href="http://laws-lois.justice.gc.ca/eng/acts/C-17/page-1.html" rel="external">Canadian Forces Superannuation Act</a></cite>, <abbr title="Revised Statutes">R.S.</abbr>, 1985, <abbr title="chapter">c.</abbr> C-17, the
<cite><a href="http://laws.justice.gc.ca/eng/acts/D-1.3/" rel="external">Defence Services Pension Continuation Act</a></cite>, 1970, <abbr title="chapter">c.</abbr>
D-3, the <cite><a href="http://laws.justice.gc.ca/eng/acts/R-10.6/" rel="external">Royal Canadian Mounted Police Pension Continuation Act</a></cite>, 1970, <abbr title="chapter">c.</abbr> R-10, and the <cite><a href="http://laws-lois.justice.gc.ca/eng/acts/R-11/page-19.html" rel="external">Royal Canadian Mounted Police Superannuation Act</a></cite>, <abbr title="Revised Statutes">R.S.</abbr>, 1985, <abbr title="chapter">c.</abbr> R-11, the
<cite><a href="http://laws-lois.justice.gc.ca/eng/acts/M-5.01/index.html" rel="external">Members of Parliament Retiring Allowances Act</a></cite>, <abbr title="Revised Statutes">R.S.</abbr>,
1985, <abbr title="chapter">c.</abbr> M-5, and that portion of pension to the <cite><a href="http://laws-lois.justice.gc.ca/eng/acts/C-8/index.html" rel="external">Canada Pension Plan Act</a></cite>, <abbr title="Revised Statutes">R.S.</abbr>, 1985, <abbr title="chapter">c.</abbr> C-8.</p>

<strong>No</strong> <var title="checkbox">( )</var></p>
<p>If so, the Bidder must provide the following information:</p>
<ol type="a">

<p>A contract for the services of a <abbr title="Former public servant">FPS</abbr> who has been retired for

<h2>Work Force Reduction Program</h2>
<p>Is the Bidder a <abbr title="Former public servant">FPS</abbr> who received a lump sum payment pursuant to
the terms of a work force reduction program? 
<strong>Yes</strong> <var title="checkbox">( )</var>
<strong>No</strong> <var title="checkbox">( )</var></p>
<p>If so, the Bidder must provide the following information:</p>
<ol type="a">
<li value="1">name of former public servant;</li>
<li value="2">conditions of the lump sum payment incentive;</li>
<li value="3">date of termination of employment;</li>
<li value="4">amount of lump sum payment;</li>
<li value="5">rate of pay on which lump sum payment is based;</li>
<li value="6">period of lump sum payment including start date, end
date and number of weeks;</li>
<li value="7">number and amount (professional fees) of other contracts
subject to the restrictions of a work force reduction program.</li>
</ol>
<p>For all contracts awarded during the lump sum payment period,
the total amount of fees that may be paid to a <abbr title="Former public servant">FPS</abbr> who received a
lump sum payment is $5,000, including the Goods and Services Tax or
Harmonized Sales Tax.</p>

submitted by the Bidder in response to the above requirements is
accurate and complete.</p>
