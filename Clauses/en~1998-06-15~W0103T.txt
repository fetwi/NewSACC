<p>The benefits that apply to this procurement are contained in:
Section 5.5, <cite>Selkirk First Nation Final Agreement (SFA)</cite>,
clauses:</p>
<ul class="hierarchy">
<li><span class="number">15.7.1.1</span> In evaluating any
competitive proposal, bid or tender for the survey of Selkirk First
Nation Settlement Land, Government shall include among the factors
for consideration, employment of Selkirk People, and Selkirk First
Nation and Selkirk People ownership or equity investment in the
firm submitting the proposal, bid or tender, and in any
subcontractor to that firm</li>
<li><span class="number">15.7.1.2</span> Selkirk First Nation and
Government shall ensure that qualifications and experience
requirements for employment of Selkirk People in the surveying of
Selkirk First Nation Settlement Land, shall be established at
levels appropriate to the nature of the tasks being performed in
that employment, and shall take into account the local knowledge of
Selkirk People.</li>
</ul>
